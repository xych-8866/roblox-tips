Islands – Ultimate Guide: Best Automation, Fast Money & Factory Setup

🎮 Game Introduction

Islands is a Roblox farming and crafting game. Build your island, farm crops, mine resources, and automate production. This guide covers the best early-game farms, industrial automation, and how to reach endgame fast.

💡 Basic Tips for Beginners

  • Start with wheat and carrots – They grow fast and sell for decent profit.
  • Build a crafting table immediately – Unlocks better tools and storage.
  • Upgrade your backpack – More inventory slots save time running back and forth.
  • Visit the market daily – Buy cheap resources, sell high-demand items.

🔥 Advanced Strategies & Hidden Mechanics

  • Auto-farm setup – Place sprinklers (copper + iron) to water crops automatically.
  • Industrial smelter chain – Connect chests to smelters using conveyor belts for passive ingot production.
  • Boss respawn timer – The Wizard Boss respawns every 20 minutes. Defeat for rare drops (enchanted books).
  • Cow breeding exploit – Keep two cows in a fenced area with wheat; they produce calves every 10 minutes.

📊 Best Early-Mid Game Investments

  • Fishing rod ($500) – Catch fish for food and rare items (enchanted fish).
  • Iron tools – Mine iron faster; upgrade to gold when possible.
  • Automated farm (wheat + windmill) – Passive income of ~$5k per hour.
  • Quarry – Unlocks at level 20; produces stone and coal automatically.

❌ Common Mistakes That Slow Progress

  • Not using sprinklers – Hand-watering is too slow for large farms.
  • Selling raw resources – Process them into ingots or food for 3x profit.
  • Ignoring storage chests – You'll run out of inventory space quickly.
  • Forgetting to craft a totem – Totems (like windmill) are essential for automation.

🎯 Final Advice for Endgame

  • Save $500k for the "Industrial Forge" – smelts 10x faster.
  • Join an island co-op to trade rare resources (titanium, gems).
  • Unlock the "Mining Drill" at level 50 for automated ore collection.
  • Complete all achievements for the "Master Farmer" title (gives +10% crop yield).
