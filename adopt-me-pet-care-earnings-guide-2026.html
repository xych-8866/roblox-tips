Adopt Me Pet Care & Earnings Guide 2026 – Raise Pets & Make Money

Raising pets in Adopt Me isn't just about getting them to full grown – it's also a way to earn bucks. This guide covers pet care tasks, aging strategies, and how to profit from your pets.

Pet Care Basics

Every pet has 4 needs: Hunger, Thirst, Sleep, and Fun. You need to keep them high to earn task XP. Each completed task (eating, drinking, sleeping, playing) gives XP and a small amount of bucks.

Age Stages

  • Newborn: 0 XP
  • Junior: 25 XP
  • Pre-Teen: 60 XP
  • Teen: 100 XP
  • Post-Teen: 150 XP
  • Full Grown: 200 XP

Each task gives 5-10 XP depending on the task type.

How to Earn Bucks While Raising Pets

1. Task Completion Rewards

Each pet task you complete gives 2-5 bucks. While it's not a lot, it adds up over time. With 4 pets equipped, you'll earn 10-20 bucks per task cycle.

2. Camping Chair AFK

Place your pet on a camping chair. It gradually earns XP and bucks while you're AFK. Overnight = 100-200 bucks + significant XP.

3. Family Member Bonus

Add a family member (or alt account). The baby gives 2x task speed, meaning more tasks = more XP + more bucks per hour.

4. Complete Daily Tasks

Daily quests reward extra bucks. Complete them while raising pets for double profit.

5. Sell Full Grown Pets

The most profitable strategy: raise pets to full grown and sell them for bucks. A full grown legendary sells for 20,000-50,000 bucks depending on the pet.

Pet Aging Strategy

  • If you're active: Use Family Member + Speed Potions. This cuts aging time in half.
  • If you're AFK: Use Camping Chair and check back every 30 minutes.
  • For profit: Age 4 pets at once (one per slot), make a neon, then sell the neon for 2-3x the cost of individual pets.

Best Pets to Age for Profit

  • Legendaries: Highest sale price.
  • Ultra-Rares: Good balance of speed and profit.
  • Event Pets: Limited time, higher demand after event ends.

Pro Tips

  • Don't waste time aging common pets – they sell for very little.
  • Save Speed Potions for legendary pets – they're worth the investment.
  • Use an alt account as a "baby" for 2x task speed.
  • If you have multiple pets, age them in batches of 4 to make neons.
