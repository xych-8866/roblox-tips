Brookhaven RP Quick Money 2026 – Fastest Cash Methods

Cash is essential for buying houses, cars, and customization. This guide ranks the fastest ways to earn cash in Brookhaven RP, from active jobs to passive income.

1. Pizza Delivery (Best Active Method)

Go to the pizza place, grab a pizza, and deliver it to the marked house. Each delivery takes 45 seconds and pays 50 cash. In one hour you can make 4,000+ cash. No cooldown – repeat immediately.

2. Hidden Cash Stashes (Daily)

Collect these hidden stashes every day for free cash:

  • Camping Tent: $200 daily
  • Beach Cave: $150 every 4 hours
  • Police Station Basement: $100 daily
  • Hospital Roof: $75 daily

Total daily passive cash: ~$500+ with minimal effort.

3. Firefighter Job (Good AFK)

Stand near the fire station. When a fire alarm rings, go to the fire truck, drive to the fire, and extinguish it. Each fire pays $75. Spawns every 5-10 minutes.

4. Car Salesman (Passive Income)

Stand near the car dealership. When NPCs approach, click "Sell Car". Each sale gives $100. Can be done while AFK.

5. Lemonade Stand (Low but AFK)

Set up a lemonade stand in a busy area. Each sale gives $10. AFK overnight earns $500-1,000.

Comparison Table

  • Pizza Delivery: 4,000+/hour (active)
  • Hidden Stashes: 500/day (passive, 5 min)
  • Firefighter: 500-1,000/hour (semi-active)
  • Car Salesman: 300-500/hour (AFK)
  • Lemonade Stand: 100-200/hour (AFK)

Pro Strategy

Start your session by collecting all hidden stashes (5 minutes, ~$500). Then do Pizza Delivery for 30 minutes ($2,000). Repeat. You'll earn $5,000+ per hour.
