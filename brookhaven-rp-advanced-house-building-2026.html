Choosing the Right House Shell

Your house shell determines the layout possibilities. Here are the best options for serious builders:

  • Modern Mansion ($80k) – Largest space, 4 bedrooms, open floor plan, pool area.
  • Beach House ($45k) – Ocean views, open layout, good for natural light builds.
  • Luxury Penthouse ($100k) – Two-story, balcony, best for modern builds.
  • Suburban Home ($25k) – Best budget option, good for cozy designs.

Advanced Layout Strategies

  • Open Concept – Remove interior walls to create a large living/dining/kitchen space. This makes small houses feel larger.
  • Zoning – Use rugs and lighting to define zones within open spaces (living area vs dining area).
  • Flow – Place furniture so there's a clear path through the room. Avoid blocking doorways.
  • Focal Points – Each room should have a focal point (fireplace, TV, large window, artwork).

Color Schemes That Work

  • Scandinavian: White + light wood + gray + green plants.
  • Modern: Black + white + gold/brass accents.
  • Cozy: Beige + brown + warm lighting + wool textures.
  • Beach: White + light blue + sand + driftwood.
  • Industrial: Gray + black + metal + exposed structures.

Lighting is Everything

Lighting sets the mood. Use these strategies:

  • Layered Lighting: Combine overhead (chandeliers), task (table lamps), and ambient (floor lamps).
  • Warm White: Best for living areas. Cool white is for kitchens and bathrooms.
  • Accent Lighting: Use spotlights on artwork or architectural features.
  • Hidden LED Strips: Place under cabinets or behind furniture for a modern glow.

Free and Cheap Items to Use

  • Check the furniture store daily for free event items (Halloween, Christmas, Summer).
  • Use basic items creatively – a bookshelf can become a room divider.
  • Potted plants are cheap and add life to any room.
  • Rugs are the cheapest way to change a room's feel.

Pro Tips for Limited Cash

  • Start with a small house ($5k) and upgrade later – you can sell it for full price.
  • Focus on one room at a time – complete it before moving to the next.
