Blox Fruits Gem Farming 2026 – Fastest Methods & Best Spots

Gems are used for race rerolls, item shop, and event purchases. This guide covers the most efficient ways to farm gems.

1. Sea Events (Best Gems per Hour)

  • Ship Raid – 50-150 gems per raid, spawns every 30 minutes.
  • Sea Beast – 30-80 gems, random spawn. Use a boat and long-range fruit (Magma).
  • Rogue Sea Beast – 200-300 gems, spawns only at night. Best gem source.

2. Daily Chest Route (Third Sea)

Start at Floating Turtle, collect all chests around the island, then go to Sea of Treats. Full route takes 15 minutes, gives 20-50 gems. Respawns every 4 hours.

3. Elite Pirates

Kill elite pirates near Great Tree. Each gives 1-5 gems. Spawn every 20 minutes. Good for passive farming.

4. Weekly Quests

Talk to the Quest Giver in Second Sea. Weekly challenges reward 500 gems. Refresh every Monday.

5. Trading Tips

Gems are not tradeable, but you can exchange fruits for gems via NPC in the Café (1 legendary fruit = 100 gems).
