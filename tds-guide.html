⚔️ Tower Defense Simulator – Best Towers & Loadout Guide (2026)

🏆 Best Towers for Each Role

  • Early Game: Scout or Militant – cheap and fast firing.
  • Mid Game: Minigunner (high DPS) + Farm (economy).
  • Late Game: Ranger (long range) + Accelerator (boss damage).
  • Support: Commander (fire rate boost) + DJ Booth (range boost).

💰 Economy Management

  • Place 2-3 Farms before wave 10 – interest grows fast.
  • Upgrade Farms to level 3 before wave 15 – maximizes cash generation.
  • Avoid over-upgrading early towers – sell them later for better ones.
  • Use Cowboy if you prefer combat + income hybrid.

🗺️ Best Loadout for Fallen Mode

  • Farm + Militant + Minigunner + Ranger + Commander + DJ Booth
  • Place Militants at corners, Minigunner in straight lines.
  • Keep DJ Booth in center for max range coverage.
  • Upgrade Commander to level 4 for full map fire rate buff.

⚡ Golden Perks & Skins

  • Golden Minigunner: Best investment – 2x damage.
  • Golden Scout: Great for early waves.
  • Golden Cowboy: Best farming tower.
