How to Get the Golden Mermaid

  1. Collect 500 Summer Shells through minigames.
  2. Complete the Beach Cleanup quest 5 times.
  3. Trade 3 ultra-rare pets to the event merchant.
  4. Once completed, you receive a Golden Mermaid egg.

Farming Strategy (F2P)

For maximum Summer Shells per hour, rotate between these activities:

  • Sandcastle Building – 15 shells per 2 minutes (best AFK).
  • Beach Cleanup – 10 shells per round (fast and easy).
  • Fishing Tournament – 5-30 shells per catch (better on weekends with 2x shells).
  • Volleyball – 20 shells per win (best if you have a group).

Pro Tips

  • Save at least 500 shells for the Golden Mermaid before spending on other items.
  • Use a Baby or Family member to increase task speed while grinding shells.
  • The Golden Mermaid will hold high value after the event ends – keep one for trading.
  • Trade spare ultra-rare pets for shells if you're short on time.
