Blox Fruits July 2026 Update – New Content & Changes

The July 2026 update brings fresh content, balance changes, and limited-time events to Blox Fruits. Here's everything you need to know.

New Fruit: Blizzard (Reworked)

The Blizzard fruit has been significantly improved:

  • New moves: Ice dash, freezing AOE, and snowstorm ultimate.
  • Best for: PvP and crowd control.
  • Rarity: Legendary.
  • Value: Expected to rise quickly after the update.

Balance Changes

  • Kitsune: Speed boost slightly decreased.
  • Dragon: Damage against sea events increased by 15%.
  • Buddha: Range restored to previous values (after June nerf).
  • Magma: Sea event damage increased by 20%.
  • Control: Damage increased by 30% (pre-rework buff).

New Raid Boss: Abyss Guardian

A new raid boss has been added to Third Sea:

  • Requirements: Level 2000+.
  • Rewards: 400 fragments, Abyss Crown (accessory), random legendary fruit.
  • Strategy: Requires a team of 5 players. Use Magma or Blizzard for best damage.

Quality of Life Improvements

  • Fruit storage now holds 5 fruits (up from 3).
  • Raid matchmaking improved – empty slots filled faster.
  • New minimap markers for sea events.
  • Beli drops from NPCs increased by 10%.

July Event: Summer Festival (Part 2)

Running from July 5 to July 25:

  • 2x fragments from all raids and bosses.
  • Double Beli from NPC kills.
  • New limited-time fruit: "Summer Flame" – a seasonal fruit with unique abilities.
  • Summer Festival currency can be exchanged for exclusive items.

What to Do Now

  • Farm fragments during the event – best time to awaken fruits.
  • Check the new fruit storage – you can hold up to 5 fruits.
  • Join Abyss Guardian raids with your crew.
  • Save Beli for stat resets – balance changes may affect your build.

Pro Tips

  • Blizzard is the new meta – get it while it's still cheap.
  • Control is rising – hold it for the rework.
  • Use the 2x fragment event to awaken Dough or Dragon.
  • Save Summer Festival currency for exclusive items – they'll hold value.
