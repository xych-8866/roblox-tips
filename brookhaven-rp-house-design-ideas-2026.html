Brookhaven RP House Design Ideas 2026 – Modern & Cozy

Inspiration for decorating your Brookhaven house without spending Robux.

Modern Minimalist

Use white/gray color scheme. Place minimalist furniture (marble coffee table, white sectional sofa). Add indoor plants for contrast.

Cozy Cabin

Wood textures, fireplace, warm lighting. Use brown and beige colors. Add bookshelves and wool rugs.

Beach House

Light blue and sand colors. Place pool, lounge chairs, palm trees. Open floor plan with large windows.

Furniture Placement Tips

  • Use grid lock (press G) for perfect alignment.
  • Layer rugs under furniture for depth.
  • Add wall art and mirrors to make rooms feel larger.
  • Use lighting (lamps, chandeliers) to create mood.

Free Items to Collect

Check the furniture store daily for free event items. Holiday items (Halloween, Christmas) are often free.
