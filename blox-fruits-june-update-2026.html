Blox Fruits June 2026 Update – All New Content & Changes

The June 2026 update brings new fruits, balance changes, and improvements to Blox Fruits. Here's everything you need to know.

New Fruit: Phoenix (Reworked)

The Phoenix fruit has been completely reworked. It now has:

  • New moves: Fire dash, healing wings, and AOE flame burst.
  • Best for: PvP and events (healing + damage).
  • Rarity: Mythical (similar to Dragon).
  • Value: Expected to rise quickly after the update.

Balance Changes

  • Buddha: Range slightly reduced (still the best grinding fruit).
  • Dragon: Damage reduced by 10% in PvP (still strong).
  • Kitsune: Speed boost decreased slightly.
  • Magma: Sea event damage increased by 15%.
  • Control: Now deals 25% more damage (pre-rework preview).

New Sea Event: Kraken

A new sea event has been added:

  • Kraken: Spawns randomly in Third Sea at night.
  • Rewards: 300-500 fragments, guaranteed legendary fruit, Kraken Crown (accessory).
  • Strategy: Requires at least 3 players. Use Magma or Phoenix for best damage.

Quality of Life Improvements

  • Fruit drop rates from chests have been increased slightly.
  • Raid matchmaking now automatically fills empty slots with NPCs.
  • New "Fruit Storage" system – store up to 3 extra fruits (available at the Café).
  • Improved loading times for Third Sea.

June Event: Summer Festival

Limited-time event running from June 20 to July 10:

  • Double fragments from all raids and bosses.
  • 30% bonus Beli from NPC kills.
  • Special "Summer Fruit" available from event NPC (random legendary).
  • Summer Festival currency can be exchanged for exclusive items.

What to Do Now

  • Farm fragments during the double fragment event – it's the best time to awaken fruits.
  • Check your fruit storage for Phoenix – if you already had it, you get the rework for free.
  • Join Kraken hunts with crew members for the best rewards.
  • Save Beli for stat resets – balance changes may affect your build.
