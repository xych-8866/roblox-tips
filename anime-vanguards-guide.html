Anime Vanguards – Ultimate Tower Defense Guide: Best Units, Evolution & Infinite Mode

🎮 Game Introduction

Anime Vanguards is a Roblox tower defense game inspired by popular anime. Summon heroes, evolve them, and defend against waves of enemies. This guide covers the best units, evolution priorities, and endgame farming strategies.

💡 Basic Tips for Beginners

  • Summon from the Starter Banner – Costs 50 gems, gives guaranteed rare unit on first pull.
  • Place units on corners – They cover two lanes, maximizing efficiency.
  • Upgrade your summoning altar – First upgrade to level 3 increases 5-star rates by 2%.
  • Complete daily quests – Each gives 100 gems and 1 evolution shard.

🔥 Advanced Strategies & Hidden Mechanics

  • Unit synergy – Pair “Shadow Monarch” with “Necromancer” for +30% damage to summoned units.
  • Evolution trick – Evolve units during “Full Moon” event (every 6 hours) for a chance at exclusive skins.
  • Infinite mode farm – Stop at wave 45 and reset; gives best gem/time ratio (800 gems per 20 min).
  • Hidden boss – On wave 30, break all tombstones to spawn “Phantom King” (guaranteed legendary drop).

📊 Best Units Tier List (Endgame)

  • S-Tier (Must-have): Shadow Monarch (AoE king), Dragon Emperor (single target), Time Mage (slow).
  • A-Tier (Strong): Berserker (high DPS), Frost Archer (crowd control), Paladin (tank).
  • B-Tier (Decent): Ninja (early game), Cleric (healer).

❌ Common Mistakes That Waste Gems

  • Summoning on the wrong banner – “Legendary+” banner costs 500 gems but has low 6-star rates. Stick with “Rare+” until you have 3 S-tiers.
  • Ignoring unit placement order – Place buffers (like Time Mage) before damage dealers for full effect.
  • Not using consumables – Damage potions (+25% for 5 waves) cost only 20 gems. Use on boss waves.
  • Selling evolved units – Evolved units give 50% less gems when sold. Keep them.

🎯 Final Advice for Infinite Mode Wave 100+

  • Unlock “Double Speed” gamepass for 2x wave progression.
  • Have at least 2 S-tier units at max evolution (level 80).
  • Use “Time Stop” item on waves 90, 95, 100 bosses.
  • Join the official Discord for trade offers (rare units).
