Rivals – Complete PvP Guide: Best Characters, Abilities & Ranked Strategy

🎮 Game Introduction

Rivals is a Roblox PvP battler where you choose unique characters, each with special moves. Fight in 1v1 or 2v2 matches, climb the ranked ladder, and unlock skins. This guide covers the best characters, ability combos, and how to counter meta picks.

💡 Basic Tips for Beginners

  • Learn your character's abilities – Each has two special moves and a passive. Read the descriptions in the menu.
  • Use block (F) against projectiles – Block reduces damage by 60% but breaks after 3 hits.
  • Dodge (double tap) has invincibility frames – Use it to avoid ultimates.
  • Save your ultimate for the second round – Using it early often wastes it.

🔥 Advanced Strategies & Hidden Mechanics

  • Jump canceling – After a whiffed attack, jump to cancel recovery frames. This lets you escape punishes.
  • Edge guarding – Push opponents near the ledge, then use a knockback ability. Many characters can't recover.
  • Passive stacking – Some characters (like Ronin) gain damage per hit. Stack 5 hits before using ultimate.
  • Tech chasing – When opponent rolls on the ground, predict their direction and attack where they will appear.

📊 Character Tier List (Ranked 1v1)

  • S-Tier (Meta): Ronin (high combo damage), Vex (unpredictable teleports), Titan (armor on attacks).
  • A-Tier (Strong): Ember (fire DoT), Frost (slows enemies), Shadow (stealth).
  • B-Tier (Niche): Golem (tanky but slow), Ranger (good for zoning).
  • C-Tier (Weak): Sprout (low damage), Scout (outclassed).

❌ Common Mistakes That Lose Matches

  • Spamming dodge – Dodge has a 2-second cooldown. Use it only for incoming ults.
  • Ignoring cooldowns – Use your abilities on cooldown, but don't waste them when opponent is blocking.
  • Not adapting to opponent's playstyle – If they spam projectiles, switch to a rushdown character.
  • Forgetting to use passive – Each character has a unique passive (e.g., Ronin's damage ramp). Read it.

🎯 Final Advice for Climbing Ranks

  • Master one S-tier character and one A-tier counterpick.
  • Warm up in casual mode for 10 minutes before ranked.
  • Watch replays of your losses to see where you got punished.
  • Stay calm – losing two in a row? Take a break.
