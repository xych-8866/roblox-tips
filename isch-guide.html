🎣 Fisch – Best Rods & Rare Fish Locations Guide (2026)

🎣 Best Rods for Beginners

  • Plastic Rod (starter): Good for learning basics, cheap to repair.
  • Wood Rod: Low durability but higher catch rate for common fish.
  • Iron Rod: Best early game investment – balanced stats.

🐟 Rare Fish Locations

  • Golden Koi: Found in the pond behind waterfall (night time).
  • Glowing Angler: Deep cave near dock #3 (use glow bait).
  • Ancient Salmon: Upper river during rain.

💎 Best Bait & Lures

  • Worm: Common fish – high bite rate.
  • Shrimp: Rare fish – medium bite rate.
  • Glow Worms: Legendary fish – low bite rate but high value.

💰 Fast Money Strategy

  • Fish during "Lucky Hour" (in-game 6-8 PM) for 2x sell price.
  • Sell rare fish to the Collector NPC near lighthouse (20% bonus).
  • Upgrade rod first – better rod = faster money.
