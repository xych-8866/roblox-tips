#1 Farming Area: Void Zone (Last Area)

The Void zone gives the highest base diamond drops. Breakables spawn faster here than any other zone. Stay near the middle platform for maximum efficiency.

Best Enchant Loadout (F2P)

  • Diamonds V – mandatory (gives 2x diamonds)
  • Treasure Hunter V – more breakables = more diamonds
  • Crits V – faster breakable destruction
  • Fortune V – extra diamond chance

Potions to Stack

  • Diamond Potion (3x diamonds) – lasts 30 minutes
  • Speed Potion V – increases breakable spawn rate
  • Lucky Potion – more rare drops (sell for diamonds)

Best Pets for Farming

Use your highest damage pet with “Rainbow” or “Shiny” trait. Pet damage directly affects breakable respawn time. If you have a Huge pet, equip it – it gives +100% breakable speed.

Daily Routine

  1. Collect all free rewards (daily gift, hour chests).
  2. Equip Diamonds V + Treasure Hunter V.
  3. Drink Diamond Potion + Speed Potion.
  4. Farm Void Zone for 30 minutes – repeat.

Expected diamonds per hour: 50k–80k with decent setup.
