Adopt Me Pet Aging Guide 2026 – Fastest Way to Full Grown

Getting a pet to full grown is the most time‑consuming part of making a neon. This guide shows the fastest methods for solo players, families, and AFK players.

Age Stages Explained

Each pet goes through these stages: Newborn → Junior → Pre-Teen → Teen → Post-Teen → Full Grown. Each stage requires a certain number of "tasks" to complete. Tasks include eating, sleeping, playing, showering, and school.

Method 1: Family Member (Fastest Active)

Add a baby to your family (or an alt account). The baby gives 2x task speed because you can do tasks for both your pet and the baby simultaneously. This is the fastest active method.

Method 2: Speed Potion (Cheap Boost)

Buy Speed Potions from the hospital for 20 bucks each. They reduce task cooldowns by 25% for 10 minutes. Stack with Family member for maximum efficiency.

Method 3: School Tasks (Best XP)

School tasks give the most XP per task. Complete the "Homework" and "Class" tasks repeatedly. Stay in the school area for the fastest rotation.

Method 4: AFK Aging (Overnight)

Use an alt account (or a friend) to join your family. Put your pet on a camping chair, let your alt account feed and take care of the pet while you AFK. Works best with a second device.

Comparison Table

  • Family Member (active): 2-3 hours to Full Grown
  • Family + Speed Potion: 1.5-2 hours
  • AFK Overnight: 6-8 hours (passive)
  • Solo (no family): 4-5 hours

Pro Tips

  • Always keep your pet's tasks queued – don't let the task bar empty.
  • Use the Camping Chair to reduce hunger drain while AFK.
  • Complete daily tasks first – they give bonus XP for all pets.
  • Age 4 pets at once (one per slot) to make neon faster.
