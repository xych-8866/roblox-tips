Which Egg is Best for Legendaries?

  • Best Overall: Royal Egg (6% chance, always available).
  • Best Event Egg: Summer 2026 Egg (10% chance, limited time).
  • Best Guaranteed Legendary: Golden Egg (100% chance, requires star rewards).
  • Best Value for Money: Pet Egg (2.5% chance, cheap).

Hatching Strategy

  • If you have limited bucks: Buy Pet Eggs (cheaper, decent chance).
  • If you have plenty of bucks: Buy Royal Eggs (higher chance, better return).
  • During events: Save event currency for event eggs – they have higher legendary chances.
  • Star Rewards: Save stars for Golden/Diamond eggs – guaranteed legendaries.

Pro Tips

  • Don't buy Cracked Eggs – they have the worst legendary chance.
  • Hatch in bulk – some players believe bulk hatching increases legendary chances (anecdotal).
  • Use Lucky Potions (if available) – they slightly increase rare drops.
  • Trade legendaries you don't want for ones you do – trading is more reliable than hatching.
