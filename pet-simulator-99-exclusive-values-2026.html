What Are Exclusive Pets?

Exclusive pets are only available during limited-time events (Easter, Halloween, Christmas, Summer, etc.). They cannot be hatched from standard eggs and are typically obtained through event currency or special minigames. Their value fluctuates based on availability and demand.

How to Get Exclusive Pets

  • Participate in limited-time events and save event currency.
  • Trade diamonds or other pets for exclusives from other players.
  • Open special event eggs during the active event window.
  • Redeem exclusive pet codes during holidays (very rare).

Trading Tips for Exclusive Pets

  • Exclusive pets often rise in value 3-6 months after the event ends.
  • Check value lists daily – prices change quickly after updates.
  • Never trade exclusives for multiple low-tier pets – you lose value.
  • Use the Trading Plaza and avoid trust trades.

Value Trends (June 2026)

Golden Pop Cat is the most sought-after exclusive due to its limited availability. Rainbow Cupcake is rising after the Valentine event ended. Older exclusives (2024) are stable but have lower demand. Focus on exclusives from the last 12 months for best trade potential.
