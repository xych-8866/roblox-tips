🌈 Rainbow Friends – How to Survive All Monsters (Chapter Guide)

👹 Chapter 1 – Blue

  • Blue's room: He is blind – move slowly, do not run. Crouch walk.
  • Objective: Collect 5 blue orbs. They spawn in corners.
  • Hide spots: Cardboard boxes. Enter when Blue grunts.

🍫 Chapter 2 – Green

  • Green's maze: Follow the arrows on walls; avoid the puddles (they slow you).
  • Special item: Chocolate bar – throw to distract Green for 8 seconds.
  • Shortcut: After second turn, break left wall (fake wall) – skip half maze.

🟪 Chapter 3 – Purple

  • Purple darkness level: Use the glow stick (found at start).
  • Monster behavior: Purple moves only when you look away. Keep eyes on it while moving backwards.
  • Exit trick: Reach the fuse box, turn on light – Purple disappears.

🧸 Chapter 4 – Yellow & Orange

  • Yellow (crying): Hides in lockers. Avoid opening lockers near him.
  • Orange (fast): Sprint in straight lines; he cannot turn quickly.
  • Strategy: Let Yellow pass, then rush to next room. Use Orange’s poor turning to loop around obstacles.

🔚 Chapter 5 – Final Boss (Red)

  • Red's attack: Launches fireballs – hide behind pillars.
  • Weapon: Water balloons (found around arena). Throw at Red to stun.
  • Sequence: Stun Red → run to button → repeat 3 times.

🎁 Secrets & Badges

  • Unlock "Rainbow Master": Beat game without being caught once.
  • Hidden plushies: One per chapter (e.g., behind vending machine in Ch.1). Collect all for special ending.
