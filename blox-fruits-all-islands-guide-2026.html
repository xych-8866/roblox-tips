Blox Fruits All Islands Guide 2026 – Best Grinding Spots & Hidden Secrets

Blox Fruits has three seas, each with unique islands. This guide covers every island, including the best grinding spots, hidden chests, NPCs, and boss locations.

First Sea Islands (Level 1-700)

1. Starter Island

Level Range: 1-50

Best Grinding Spot: Bandit camp near the spawn point – respawns quickly.

Hidden Chest: Behind the waterfall near the bridge – contains 500 Beli.

Boss: None.

Tip: This is your starting point. Buy a boat as soon as you can.

2. Jungle

Level Range: 50-150

Best Grinding Spot: Inside the jungle temple – bandits spawn in groups.

Hidden Chest: Inside the temple, behind the stone statue – contains 1,000 Beli.

Boss: Gorilla King (spawns every 15 minutes) – drops 5k Beli + common fruit.

3. Pirate Village

Level Range: 100-250

Best Grinding Spot: Behind the bar – bandits and pirates spawn in clusters.

Hidden Chest: In the well behind the bar – contains 500 Beli.

Boss: None.

Tip: Complete bartender quests for extra XP and Beli.

4. Desert

Level Range: 200-400

Best Grinding Spot: Near the oasis – desert bandits spawn in groups of 3-4.

Hidden Chest: In the pyramid – requires picking up a torch puzzle.

Boss: None.

Tip: Use AOE fruits (Light, Ice) to grind faster in this zone.

5. Frozen Village

Level Range: 350-650

Best Grinding Spot: Ice cave – yetis spawn in groups of 4-5.

Hidden Chest: Behind the ice waterfall – contains 2,000 Beli.

Boss: Darkbeard (spawns at night) – drops Dark Coat + 30 fragments.

Tip: This is the best grinding spot in First Sea. Stay here until level 600.

6. Sky Islands

Level Range: 600-700

Best Grinding Spot: Cloud platform near the teleporter – sky bandits.

Hidden Chest: On the highest cloud – contains 500 Beli.

Boss: None.

Tip: Use this island to reach level 700 and unlock Second Sea.

Second Sea Islands (Level 700-1500)

7. Kingdom of Rose

Level Range: 700-900

Best Grinding Spot: The bridge area – pirates spawn constantly.

Hidden Chest: Behind the castle, underwater – contains 3,000 Beli.

Boss: Diamond (spawns every 20 minutes) – drops diamond accessories.

8. Ice Castle

Level Range: 800-1000

Best Grinding Spot: Inside the castle – ice guards spawn in groups.

Hidden Chest: In the dungeon, behind a breakable wall – contains 2,000 Beli.

Boss: Awakened Ice Admiral – drops 50 fragments + winter cape.

9. Green Zone

Level Range: 1000-1200

Best Grinding Spot: The forest area – elite pirates spawn in groups.

Hidden Chest: Under the giant tree – contains 5,000 Beli.

Boss: Venom (rare spawn) – drops Venom fruit.

10. Floating Turtle

Level Range: 1200-1500

Best Grinding Spot: The shell area – turtle soldiers spawn in large groups.

Hidden Chest: Underneath the turtle, underwater – contains 10,000 Beli.

Boss: None.

Third Sea Islands (Level 1500-2550)

11. Great Tree

Level Range: 1500-1700

Best Grinding Spot: Around the base of the tree – acolytes spawn in groups.

Hidden Chest: Inside the tree hollow – contains 5,000 Beli.

Boss: None.

12. Sea of Treats

Level Range: 1700-1900

Best Grinding Spot: Near the candy village – candy soldiers spawn in clusters.

Hidden Chest: Inside the gingerbread house – contains 10,000 Beli.

Boss: Dough King (requires Dough raid) – drops Dough fruit + 250 fragments.

13. Mansion

Level Range: 1900-2100

Best Grinding Spot: Inside the mansion – ghost NPCs spawn in large groups.

Hidden Chest: In the secret room behind the bookshelf – contains 15,000 Beli.

Boss: Rip_Indra (requires God's Chalice) – drops Pale Scarf + 300 fragments.

14. Cake Land

Level Range: 2100-2300

Best Grinding Spot: The cake tower area – cake guards spawn in large groups.

Hidden Chest: On the top floor of the cake tower – contains 20,000 Beli.

15. Frozen Dimension

Level Range: 2300-2550

Best Grinding Spot: The ice field – frost giants spawn in groups of 5.

Hidden Chest: Under the giant ice block – contains 25,000 Beli.

Boss: Leviathan (sea event) – drops Leviathan Crown + 250 fragments.

Island Navigation Tips

  • Always carry a boat – you'll need it for sea travel between islands.
  • Use the teleport system once unlocked – it saves time.
  • Server hop if an island is too crowded – more players means slower spawns.
  • Mark important islands with waypoints to return quickly.

Pro Tips

  • Stay in each island until you've outleveled it – don't rush to the next sea.
  • Frozen Village (First Sea) and Floating Turtle (Second Sea) are the best grinding spots.
  • In Third Sea, Great Tree and Cake Land are the most efficient for XP.
  • Always collect hidden chests – they add up to significant Beli over time.
