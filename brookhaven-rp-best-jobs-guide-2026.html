Brookhaven RP Best Jobs Guide 2026 – Highest Paying Roles

Not all jobs are created equal. This guide ranks every job in Brookhaven RP by pay per hour, difficulty, and AFK potential, so you can earn cash faster.

Job Ranking Criteria

Each job is rated by:

  • Pay per hour: How much cash you can earn in one hour.
  • Difficulty: How much skill/attention is required.
  • AFK Potential: Can you do it while not actively playing?

1. Pizza Delivery (Best Overall)

Pay: $50 per delivery (4,000+/hour).

Difficulty: Very Low – just drive and deliver.

AFK Potential: None – requires active play.

Best For: Everyone – fastest cash in the game.

Tip: Use a fast car to reduce travel time.

2. Police Officer (High Risk, High Reward)

Pay: $100 per arrest (1,500-2,500/hour depending on criminals).

Difficulty: Medium – requires chasing criminals.

AFK Potential: None.

Best For: Players who enjoy PvP-style gameplay.

Tip: Work with a partner to catch criminals faster.

3. Firefighter (Semi-AFK)

Pay: $75 per fire (500-1,000/hour).

Difficulty: Low – drive to fire, put it out.

AFK Potential: Low – can wait at the station for alarms.

Best For: Players who want a mix of active and passive.

4. Cashier (Best AFK)

Pay: $40 per checkout (300-500/hour).

Difficulty: Very Low – just stand at the register.

AFK Potential: High – customers come automatically.

Best For: AFK farming while doing other things.

Tip: Works best in busy servers (more customers).

5. Hot Dog Stand (Pure AFK)

Pay: $15 per sale (500-1,000/hour).

Difficulty: None – just place it.

AFK Potential: Very High – works while you're offline.

Best For: Overnight AFK cash.

Tip: Place it near the nursery or playground for more customers.

Best Strategy for Maximum Cash

  1. Active: Do Pizza Delivery for 30 minutes ($2,000).
  2. AFK: Set up Hot Dog Stand and leave it running overnight ($5,000+).
  3. Daily: Collect hidden cash stashes ($500/day, 5 minutes).
  4. Combine: Pizza Delivery + Hot Dog Stand + Hidden Stashes = $10,000+ per day.

Pro Tips

  • Start with Pizza Delivery – it's the fastest and easiest.
  • Buy a Hot Dog Stand as soon as possible – it pays for itself in one night.
  • Check the job board for daily bonuses – some jobs pay extra on certain days.
  • If you have a group, take turns doing different jobs for variety.
