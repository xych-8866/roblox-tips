Horror Elevator – Complete Survival Guide: All Floors, Monsters & Secrets

🎮 Game Introduction

Horror Elevator is a Roblox horror game where you ride an elevator to random floors, each with unique monsters and puzzles. Survive to earn coins and unlock new elevators. This guide covers every floor type, monster behavior, and best strategies.

💡 Basic Tips for Beginners

  • Listen for floor announcements – The elevator voice warns about danger level (1-5). Higher = scarier.
  • Stay near the elevator door – You can escape back to the elevator at any time, but only before the monster appears.
  • Use your flashlight sparingly – Some monsters (e.g., “The Warden”) are attracted to light.
  • Save coins for “Emergency Button” – Costs 500 coins, instantly returns you to lobby.

🔥 Advanced Strategies & Hidden Mechanics

  • Floor skipping exploit – If you press the button before the doors fully open, you can skip dangerous floors (works 50% of the time).
  • Monster luring – Some monsters (like “Clown”) follow sound. Throw a coin (press G) to distract them.
  • Secret floor “???” – Reach floor 50 without dying to unlock a secret golden elevator with 2x coin rewards.
  • Item stacking – Carry both “Flashbang” and “Smoke Grenade” to escape any monster combo.

📊 Hardest Floors Tier List

  • S-Tier (Instant death): Floor 13 (The Dollmaker), Floor 33 (The Mirror).
  • A-Tier (Very hard): Floor 7 (The Maze), Floor 21 (The Nursery).
  • B-Tier (Manageable): Floor 3 (Screaming Statues), Floor 10 (Dark Library).

❌ Common Mistakes That Get You Killed

  • Running from The Warden – He’s faster than you. Hide in a closet instead.
  • Ignoring the “sanity meter” – Low sanity makes monsters appear more often. Take a “Calm Pill” (found in drawers).
  • Hiding in the same spot twice – Monsters remember your last hiding position.
  • Not checking the map – Some floors have multiple exits; memorize them before the monster spawns.

🎯 Final Advice for Reaching Floor 50

  • Save 2k coins for “Revive Token” – gives you an extra life.
  • Play in a group of 3 – one person distracts, others hide.
  • Always bring a flashlight and a smoke grenade for floors 30+.
  • Watch speedruns on YouTube to learn optimal hiding spots.
