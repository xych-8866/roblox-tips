Brookhaven RP Secret Jobs 2026 – Hidden Work Locations

Earn extra cash with these unofficial jobs that players don't know about.

1. Museum Tour Guide

Go to the museum and stand near the dinosaur skeleton. Click the "Give Tour" button – pays $50 per tour.

2. Movie Theatre Staff

Behind the ticket counter, click "Sell Tickets". Each ticket sold pays $5. Can sell up to 100 tickets per day.

3. Gas Station Attendant

At the gas station, stand near the pump. Click "Help Customer" – $30 each time.

4. Park Ranger

Inside the park ranger hut, click "Clean Trash". $20 per cleanup.
