Arsenal Weapon Tier List & Pro Strategies (2026)

🔥 Pro Tip: Learn 2-3 weapons from S-Tier and master them. Switching weapons too much reduces muscle memory.

S-Tier (Best of the Best)

  • M4A1-S – Low recoil, fast reload, 1-shot headshot. Best all-rounder.
  • MP5K-SD – Silent and accurate. Great for flanking.
  • Railgun – One-shot kill. Hard to use but deadly in close range.

A-Tier (Competitive)

  • AK-47 – High damage but high recoil. Best for tap-firing.
  • SCAR-H – 2-shot body kill. Slow fire rate, use as DMR.
  • P90 – Large magazine, good for spray and pray.

B-Tier (Situational)

  • UMP45 – Decent for beginners.
  • Dual Deagles – High damage but slow swap speed.
  • Bow – Silent but hard to hit moving targets.

🗺️ Map Control Tricks

Dust II: Always watch Long A and B tunnels. Use flashbangs to push.
Rust: Control the central tower. Shotguns dominate close quarters.
Facility: Use vents to surprise enemies. Stay crouched to reduce noise.

🎯 Aim & Crosshair Placement

Keep crosshair at head level always. Pre-aim corners before peeking. Practice tracking in aim trainer (use !aimtrain in official Arsenal servers).

⚡ Movement Tips

Jump while shooting to make yourself harder to hit. Use sliding to move faster after respawn. Learn rocket-jumping with explosive weapons.

🎮 Settings Optimization

  • Field of View (FOV): 90-100
  • Mouse Sensitivity: 800 DPI, in-game 3.5
  • Disable mouse acceleration
  • Enable "Hit Sound" for feedback
