How Enchants Work

Enchants are passive bonuses that apply to all your equipped pets. Each pet can hold 1 enchant. You can have up to 6 enchants active at once (one per pet). The effects stack, so having multiple copies of the same enchant is valuable.

How to Get Enchants

  • Enchant Crafting: Use diamonds and gems at the Enchant Forge (Void World).
  • Trading: Buy enchants from other players in the Trading Plaza.
  • Rewards: Some enchants are given as rewards for achievements or events.

Pro Tips

  • Prioritize Diamonds V first – it's the single biggest upgrade.
  • Don't waste resources on Glitch V or Luck V – they're overpriced for what they do.
  • Save gems – they're harder to get than diamonds.
  • Enchants apply to ALL pets – so even if you only have one good pet, the enchant works.
