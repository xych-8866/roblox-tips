Pet Master Simulator – Ultimate Guide: Best Pets, Fast Coins & Legendary Eggs

🎮 Game Introduction

Pet Master Simulator is a Roblox pet-collecting idle game. Collect eggs, hatch pets, and battle other players. Earn coins to upgrade your pet slots and unlock rare zones. This guide covers the best pets, coin farming methods, and egg strategies.

💡 Basic Tips for Beginners

  • Open basic eggs first – Cost 100 coins, give common pets. Sell duplicates for 50 coins each.
  • Upgrade your pet slots to 5 – More pets = more coins per minute.
  • Use the "Auto Clicker" upgrade – Automatically collects coins while AFK.
  • Complete the tutorial – Gives a free Legendary egg (guaranteed rare pet).

🔥 Advanced Strategies & Hidden Mechanics

  • Egg stacking – Save 10 eggs of the same type, then open them in a row for a higher chance at shiny pets.
  • Coin multiplier zones – The "Rainbow Zone" (unlocked at level 20) gives 3x coins but costs 500 coins per entry.
  • Pet fusion – Combine two level 30 pets to create a stronger hybrid pet (random stats).
  • Daily reward exploit – Log in at exactly 12:00 AM server time for a bonus Super Egg.

📊 Best Pets Tier List (PvP & Farming)

  • S-Tier (Best): Rainbow Phoenix (2x coin multiplier), Dark Knight (high PvP damage).
  • A-Tier (Strong): Crystal Dragon (balanced), Golden Griffin (good for farming).
  • B-Tier (Decent): Flame Wolf (beginner), Water Serpent (okay stats).
  • C-Tier (Avoid): Slime (weak), Bat (low coin generation).

❌ Common Mistakes That Slow Progress

  • Buying expensive eggs too early – Stick with Golden Eggs ($5k) until you have 5 pets at level 20.
  • Ignoring pet levels – Level up your main pets to 30 before fusing.
  • Skipping daily events – Events (like Double Coin Weekend) can double your earnings.
  • Not using the trading plaza – Trade duplicate rare pets for coins or other pets.

🎯 Final Advice for Becoming Pet Master

  • Save 500k coins for the "Master's Egg" – guaranteed legendary pet.
  • Join a pet trading Discord for fair trades.
  • Focus on one S-tier pet and max it to level 100.
  • Use the "Potion of Luck" (10k coins) before opening 10 eggs at once.
