Brookhaven RP House Building Guide 2026 – Design & Decoration Tips

Learn how to build, design, and decorate your dream house in Brookhaven RP. No Robux required – use in-game money from hidden cash spots.

Getting Started: Buy a House

Open the House Menu (home icon on screen). Browse available houses. Starter house: $5,000. Premium houses: $20k-$100k. Save up from jobs and hidden cash.

Best House Layouts (F2P Friendly)

  • Modern Mansion ($80k) – largest space, 4 bedrooms, pool.
  • Beach House ($45k) – ocean view, open floor plan.
  • Suburban Home ($25k) – cozy, best for beginners.

Furniture Placement Tricks

  • Use the grid lock (press G on PC or tap grid button) for perfect alignment.
  • Stack items using move tool – raise/lower with arrows.
  • Create custom rooms by resizing walls (some houses allow wall editing).
  • Add color themes – use paint bucket on furniture.

Decoration Tips (No Robux)

  • Buy from Store using in-game cash.
  • Event items (Halloween, Christmas) are often free – collect them.
  • Use matching sets – kitchen sets, bedroom sets look more professional.
  • Add outdoor gardens and fences for curb appeal.

Money for House Upgrades

Collect hidden cash stashes (check our secret money guide) and repeat Pizza Delivery job. $50 per delivery – 20 deliveries = $1000.

Show Off Your Build

Once finished, invite friends via House Party mode. You can also sell house tours on Discord servers.
