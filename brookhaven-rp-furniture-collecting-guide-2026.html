How Furniture Works in Brookhaven RP

You can buy furniture from the in-game catalog or find it in the world. Furniture is stored in your inventory and can be placed in any house you own.

Free Daily Furniture (Furniture Store)

Every day, the furniture store gives away one free item. Check it daily:

  • Monday: Small rug
  • Tuesday: Basic chair
  • Wednesday: Small table
  • Thursday: Indoor plant
  • Friday: Wall art
  • Saturday: Lamp
  • Sunday: Decorative item (varies)

Hidden Furniture in the World

  • Abandoned Chair: Behind the gas station, under a tarp. Free.
  • Beach Umbrella: Under the pier, click to collect. Free.
  • Campfire: At the campsite, click the fire after 8 PM in-game time. Free.
  • Park Bench: Hidden behind the park entrance, collectable after 5 visits.
  • Street Lamp: Near the museum, click it 10 times to unlock.

Best Furniture for Your House

  • Must-have: Rugs (make rooms feel complete), Plants (add life), Lighting (set mood).
  • For small houses: Use multi-functional furniture (benches with storage, tables that double as desks).
  • For large houses: Invest in sectional sofas, dining tables, and bookshelves.

How to Save Money on Furniture

  • Collect daily free items first – they add up over time.
  • Buy furniture during events – many items are discounted.
  • Check the furniture store for clearance sales (usually on Sundays).
  • Trade duplicate furniture with friends.

Pro Tips

  • Visit the furniture store every day – the free item rotates and some are rare.
  • Keep event furniture – they're limited and often become valuable.
  • Use hidden furniture to save cash for bigger purchases.
  • Plan your room layout before buying furniture – don't waste money on items that don't fit.
