Adopt Me Neon & Mega Guide 2026 – How to Make Neons Fast

Making neon and mega neon pets is the most profitable way to trade in Adopt Me. This guide covers the fastest aging methods, best pets to neon, and profit strategies.

What Are Neons and Megas?

  • Neon: Combine 4 full-grown pets of the same type. The neon version glows and is worth 2-3x more.
  • Mega Neon: Combine 4 neons of the same type. The mega version has color-changing effects and is worth 4-5x more than 16 normals.

Fastest Aging Methods

1. Family Member Bonus (Best Active)

Add a baby to your family (or an alt account). The baby gives 2x task speed – you get tasks for both your pet and the baby simultaneously.

2. Speed Potions (Cheap Boost)

Buy Speed Potions from the hospital for 20 bucks each. They reduce task cooldowns by 25% for 10 minutes. Stack with Family member for maximum efficiency.

3. Camping Chair AFK (Overnight)

Place your pet on a camping chair – it earns XP and bucks while you're AFK. Overnight = significant progress.

4. School Tasks (Best XP)

School tasks give the most XP per task. Stay in the school area for the fastest rotation.

Profit Strategy

  1. Buy 4 pets of the same type (preferably legendaries).
  2. Age them to full grown using the fastest methods above.
  3. Combine them into a neon.
  4. Sell the neon for 2-3x the cost of 4 normals.
  5. Repeat with other pets.

Pro Tips

  • Age 4 pets at once (one per slot) to make neon faster.
  • Use alt accounts as family members – each alt gives 2x task speed.
  • Don't waste time aging common pets – they sell for very little.
  • Check value lists before selling – some pets are worth more than others.
  • Save your best neons for trading – they hold value better.
