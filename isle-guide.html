Isle – Complete Survival Guide: All Endings, Hidden Locations & Crafting

🎮 Game Introduction

Isle is a Roblox survival game where you are stranded on a mysterious island. Craft tools, build shelter, and uncover secrets. Multiple endings depending on your choices. This guide covers all endings, hidden items, and best survival routes.

💡 Basic Tips for Beginners

  • Craft a stone axe first – Used for wood and as a weapon.
  • Build a shelter before night – Night time spawns aggressive wolves.
  • Find the old campsite – Contains a map and a water bottle.
  • Check the boat wreck – Has canned food and a flare gun.

🔥 Advanced Strategies & Hidden Mechanics

  • Hidden cave entrance – Behind the waterfall at the north river; contains a rare compass that points to the secret laboratory.
  • All endings guide – “Escape” ending: repair the boat (needs 10 wood, 5 rope, 1 sail). “Laboratory” ending: find all 4 keycards. “Cult” ending: follow the glowing stones at midnight.
  • Infinite food exploit – Berry bushes respawn every 5 minutes. Mark them on your map.
  • Monster avoidance – The “Island Beast” spawns on day 7. Hide in the cave until it passes.

📊 Endings Difficulty & Rewards

  • Easiest (Escape): Requires basic resources, gives 500 XP.
  • Medium (Laboratory): Requires puzzle solving, gives 1k XP + “Scientist” title.
  • Hardest (Cult): Requires precise timing, gives 5k XP + “Chosen One” title.

❌ Common Mistakes That Get You Killed

  • Eating unknown mushrooms – Some are poisonous. Only eat red mushrooms (safe) or cooked meat.
  • Making noise at night – Running or chopping wood attracts wolves. Crouch walk.
  • Ignoring hydration – Dehydration drains health fast. Always carry a full water bottle.
  • Not saving the game – You can save at any tent. Without saving, dying resets progress.

🎯 Final Advice for 100% Completion

  • Unlock all 3 endings by replaying with different choices.
  • Find all 5 hidden totems to unlock the “Island Guardian” pet.
  • Use the “Explorer” perk (buy with 10k XP) to see all resources on the map.
  • Join the Isle Discord for co-op tips and secret locations.
