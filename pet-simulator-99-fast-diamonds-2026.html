Pet Simulator 99 Fast Diamonds 2026 – Best Daily Methods

Diamonds are the most valuable currency in PS99. This guide covers the fastest ways to get diamonds daily, including hidden chests, quests, and farming strategies.

1. Daily Login Rewards

Login every day without missing a day. Day 7 gives 500 diamonds, Day 14 gives 1,000 diamonds, Day 30 gives 5,000 diamonds. Don't skip – the streak bonuses are huge.

2. Hidden Diamond Chests

These chests spawn daily and give free diamonds:

  • Tech World: Behind the giant computer – 250 diamonds (respawns every 4 hours).
  • Fantasy World: Behind the crystal castle – 250 diamonds.
  • Void World: On the floating island – 500 diamonds (respawns every 6 hours).
  • Jungle Zone: Inside the waterfall – 100 diamonds.
  • Ice Zone: Behind the ice cave – 200 diamonds.

3. Coin Jar Farming

Break coin jars in Tech World and Void World. Each jar gives 5-20 diamonds. Best route: Teleport between Tech World and Void World, breaking jars in both zones. This yields 200-300 diamonds per run (takes 10 minutes).

4. Fishing Tournament

Fish in the event area. Rare fish can be traded for 50-200 diamonds each. Use the Golden Fishing Rod for better rates. Best time: weekends when rare fish spawn rates are doubled.

5. Daily Quests

Complete daily quests for diamond rewards:

  • Break 500 breakables: 100 diamonds.
  • Hatch 10 eggs: 150 diamonds.
  • Trade 3 times: 200 diamonds.
  • Complete all 3 daily quests: 500 bonus diamonds.

6. Trading Plaza Flipping

Sell unwanted event pets for diamonds. Common event pets sell for 100-500 diamonds. Check value lists before trading – some pets are undervalued.

Daily Diamond Farming Schedule

  1. Login: Collect daily login rewards (5 seconds).
  2. Daily Quests: Complete all 3 daily quests (10 minutes).
  3. Hidden Chests: Collect all 5 hidden diamond chests (5 minutes).
  4. Coin Jar Farming: Run the Tech World → Void World route (10 minutes).
  5. Fishing: Fish for 10 minutes (if it's a weekend).
