Apeirophobia – Complete Escape Guide: All Levels, Monsters & Secrets

🎮 Game Introduction

Apeirophobia is a Roblox horror game where you must escape infinite liminal spaces. Each level has unique puzzles and monsters. This guide covers every level, monster behaviors, and the secret ending.

💡 Basic Tips for Beginners

  • Stay in the light – Darkness drains your sanity. If sanity hits 0, you hear footsteps that attract monsters.
  • Listen for breathing – If you hear heavy breathing, hide immediately (closets, under beds).
  • Use the “F” key to interact – Some objects (books, drawers) contain clues.
  • Complete the tutorial level – It explains hiding mechanics and gives a free flashlight.

🔥 Advanced Strategies & Hidden Mechanics

  • Level 2 shortcut – Instead of finding 3 keys, break the window in the cafeteria to skip to the exit.
  • Monster luring – Throw a can or bottle to distract the “Skinwalker” for 5 seconds.
  • Secret ending requirement – Collect all 8 “Fragments of Memory” (one per level) and insert them into the final door.
  • Infinite running bug – Hold shift while jumping; if timed correctly, you can run without stamina drain.

📊 Level Difficulty Tier List

  • S-Tier (Hardest): Level 5 (Backrooms), Level 7 (Hospital).
  • A-Tier (Challenging): Level 3 (School), Level 6 (Mansion).
  • B-Tier (Easy): Level 1 (Library), Level 2 (Office).

❌ Common Mistakes That Get You Killed

  • Running near lockers – Monsters hear footsteps. Crouch walk when searching for hiding spots.
  • Ignoring sanity – Low sanity makes monsters more aggressive. Use a “Calm Pill” to restore 20% sanity.
  • Staying in one spot too long – Monsters will patrol back to your location. Keep moving.
  • Not using the map – Memorize dead ends to avoid getting cornered.

🎯 Final Advice for Beating Level 8 (Final)

  • Save 3 “Flashbang” items for the final chase – use when the monster is 2 steps behind.
  • Follow the red lights on the floor – they lead to the correct path.
  • Play with friends – one person distracts the boss while others activate switches.
  • Watch speedruns to learn optimal routes for each level.
