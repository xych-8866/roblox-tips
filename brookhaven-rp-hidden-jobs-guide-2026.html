Brookhaven RP Hidden Jobs Guide 2026 – Secret Roles & How to Unlock

Beyond the basic jobs (pizza delivery, police, firefighter), Brookhaven has secret jobs that offer better pay and unique rewards. This guide reveals how to unlock them all.

What Are Hidden Jobs?

Hidden jobs are roles that don't appear in the main job menu. You must discover and unlock them by interacting with specific NPCs, completing quests, or reaching certain locations.

1. Museum Tour Guide

How to Unlock: Go to the museum, stand near the dinosaur skeleton, and click the "Give Tour" button.

Pay: $50 per tour (takes 60 seconds).

Requirements: None – available from the start.

Tip: Best done when the museum has multiple players – more players = more tips.

2. Movie Theatre Staff

How to Unlock: Behind the movie theatre ticket counter, click "Sell Tickets".

Pay: $5 per ticket, up to 100 tickets per day.

Requirements: Must have watched at least one movie in the theatre.

Tip: Do this at the start of your session – it's passive daily cash.

3. Gas Station Attendant

How to Unlock: Stand near the gas pump at the gas station. Click "Help Customer" when the prompt appears.

Pay: $30 per customer (takes 20 seconds).

Requirements: Own a vehicle (any car).

Tip: Good for quick cash – customers spawn every 2-3 minutes.

4. Park Ranger

How to Unlock: Inside the park ranger hut, click "Clean Trash" to start the job.

Pay: $20 per cleanup (takes 15 seconds).

Requirements: Visit the park at least once.

Tip: Can be repeated indefinitely – good for AFK while watching videos.

5. Secret Delivery Driver

How to Unlock: Find the red van behind the grocery store, get in, and click "Start Delivery". You must complete 3 deliveries in 5 minutes.

Pay: $150 per successful route.

Requirements: Must have a driver's license (unlocked at the DMV).

Tip: Best pay among hidden jobs, but requires driving skill and route memorization.

6. Lifeguard

How to Unlock: Go to the beach, click the lifeguard chair, and select "Start Shift".

Pay: $25 per rescue (players drowning in the water).

Requirements: Must be able to swim (press Space to swim faster).

Tip: Best during peak hours when many players are at the beach.

  • Combine hidden jobs with regular jobs for maximum cash/hour.
