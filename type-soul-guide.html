Type Soul – Complete Race Guide: Best Clans, Shikai & PvP Mastery

🎮 Game Introduction

Type Soul is a Roblox RPG based on Bleach. Choose your race (Shinigami, Quincy, Hollow), unlock abilities, and battle other players. This guide covers the best races, Shikai/Bankai progression, and PvP combos.

💡 Basic Tips for Beginners

  • Start as Shinigami – Most balanced and easiest to learn.
  • Complete the first 10 quests – Unlocks your Shikai (special ability).
  • Farm mobs in Karakura Town – Best XP for levels 1-30.
  • Join a squad – Squad bonuses include +10% XP and +5% damage.

🔥 Advanced Strategies & Hidden Mechanics

  • Shikai reroll trick – If you don't like your Shikai, delete your character and restart before level 15.
  • Bankai quest exploit – Defeat 50 players in PvP to skip 3 quest steps (faster progression).
  • Secret hollow cave – Behind the waterfall in Hueco Mundo, enter to find rare evolution items (Hogyoku).
  • Combo cancelling – Flash step (double tap dash) between skills to reset attack animation.

📊 Best Races & Clan Tier List (PvP)

  • S-Tier (Meta): Vasto Lorde (Hollow), Kurosaki (Shinigami hybrid), Yhwach (Quincy).
  • A-Tier (Strong): Espada (Hollow), Stern Ritter (Quincy), Captain (Shinigami).
  • B-Tier (Decent): Adjuchas (Hollow), Lieutenant (Shinigami).

❌ Common Mistakes That Waste Time

  • Ignoring your clan's passive – Each clan gives a unique buff (e.g., Kurosaki: +15% attack speed). Read before picking.
  • Skipping dailies – Daily quests give 2k gold and a free reroll ticket.
  • Not using flash step – It's your only escape. Master its timing.
  • Fighting higher-level players – Level difference over 20 reduces your damage by 50%.

🎯 Final Advice for Becoming a Master

  • Reach level 100 to unlock "Resurrección" (Hollow) or "Voltstanding" (Quincy).
  • Save 100k gold for "Hogyoku" – evolves your race to the next tier.
  • Watch YouTube guides for advanced combos (e.g., flash step + slash + shikai).
  • Never fight a Vasto Lorde alone – they have a 30% damage reduction passive.
