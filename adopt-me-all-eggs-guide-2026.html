How Eggs Work

Each egg has a specific pool of pets. The rarity of the pet is determined by chance. Legendary pets have the lowest hatch rate. Some eggs are always available, others are limited-time event eggs.

Egg Hatching Strategy

  • Best for legendaries: Royal Egg (always available, 6% chance).
  • Best value: Pet Egg (cheap, decent chance).
  • Best limited-time: Summer 2026 Egg (10% chance).
  • Best guaranteed: Golden Egg (100% legendary).

Pro Tips

  • Don't buy Cracked Eggs – they have the worst legendary chance.
  • Save star rewards for Golden/Diamond Eggs – guaranteed legendaries.
  • During events, farm event currency and buy event eggs – higher legendary chances.
  • Hatch in bulk – some players believe it increases legendary chance (anecdotal).
  • Trade unwanted legendaries for eggs you want – trading is more reliable than hatching.
