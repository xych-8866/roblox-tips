Mad City – Ultimate Hero & Villain Guide: Best Superpowers, Money & Ranked Tips

🎮 Game Introduction

Mad City is a Roblox open-world superhero/villain game. Choose a side, unlock superpowers, rob banks or stop crimes, and climb the ranked ladder. This guide covers the best powers, fastest money methods, and PvP strategies.

💡 Basic Tips for Beginners

  • Start as Hero – Easier to learn; police cars are faster early.
  • Complete the tutorial – Gives 10k cash and a basic flight power.
  • Rob the jewelry store first – Takes 30 seconds, gives $5k. Low risk.
  • Use the minimap – Shows police patrol routes. Avoid them when robbing.

🔥 Advanced Strategies & Hidden Mechanics

  • Power synergy – Pair "Teleport" with "Explosion" for a hit-and-run combo.
  • Bank heist duo – One player distracts cops, the other drills the vault. Split $50k.
  • Secret hero base – Under the bridge at the docks, enter the pipe to access upgrade shop.
  • Villain disguise – Steal a police car and activate its siren; cops ignore you for 20 seconds.

📊 Best Superpowers Tier List (PvP & Robbery)

  • S-Tier (Meta): Teleport (escape any fight), Gravity (pull enemies), Laser (high DPS).
  • A-Tier (Strong): Flight (mobility), Explosion (AoE damage), Invisibility (stealth).
  • B-Tier (Situational): Super Strength (good for melee), Freeze (slow).

❌ Common Mistakes That Get You Arrested

  • Robbing without a mask – Cops identify you instantly. Buy a mask from black market ($1k).
  • Using the same escape route twice – Cops learn patterns. Alternate between sewers and rooftops.
  • Ignoring the wanted level – At 5 stars, helicopters spawn. Hide indoors until level drops.
  • Fighting heroes as a low-level villain – Grind money first, then unlock powers.

🎯 Final Advice for Ranked Mode

  • Reach level 50 to unlock "Storm" power – best for 1v1 PvP.
  • Save $500k for "Phoenix" vehicle – fastest escape car.
  • Join a villain gang for crew bonuses (+10% robbery cash).
  • Practice aiming with Laser power in shooting range.
