Mining Simulator – Ultimate Guide: Best Pickaxes, Fast Coins & Secret Ores

🎮 Game Introduction

Mining Simulator is a Roblox mining game. Dig for ores, sell them for coins, and upgrade your pickaxe. Unlock new worlds and rare ores. This guide covers the best pickaxes, coin farming routes, and hidden caves.

💡 Basic Tips for Beginners

  • Start with Iron Pickaxe – Cheap and efficient for early ores.
  • Sell ores in bulk – Holding ores longer doesn't increase value. Sell immediately to upgrade faster.
  • Use the auto-sell upgrade – Unlock at level 15 for passive income.
  • Complete daily mining challenges – Each gives 1k coins and a random booster.

🔥 Advanced Strategies & Hidden Mechanics

  • Ore multiplier stacking – Activate “Coin Boost” potion, then mine in the “Crystal Cave” for 4x ore value.
  • Secret diamond vein – In the “Lava Zone”, break the right wall with a gold pickaxe to reveal a diamond vein (100k coins).
  • Pet synergies – Certain pets (e.g., “Mole”) increase mining speed by 20%. Equip before mining.
  • Reset exploit – If you find a rare ore, reset your character to respawn the ore again (works up to 3 times).

📊 Best Pickaxes Tier List (Mining Speed)

  • S-Tier (Best): Titanium Pickaxe (fastest), Lava Pickaxe (auto-smelts ores), Crystal Pickaxe (2x ore value).
  • A-Tier (Strong): Gold Pickaxe (fast), Diamond Pickaxe (durable).
  • B-Tier (Decent): Iron Pickaxe (starter), Steel Pickaxe (mid).

❌ Common Mistakes That Waste Coins

  • Buying the most expensive pickaxe first – Upgrade gradually; each tier gives better value per coin.
  • Ignoring backpack upgrades – More inventory slots mean fewer trips to the sell station.
  • Not using mining bombs – Bombs clear large areas and reveal hidden ores. Use them in dense zones.
  • Skipping the “Mining Guild” – Joining costs 5k coins but gives +15% coin boost permanently.

🎯 Final Advice for Endgame

  • Save 1M coins for the “Mythical Pickaxe” – fastest mining speed and 3x ore value.
  • Join a mining crew for crew bonuses (+10% coins).
  • Farm the “Endless Mine” dungeon (unlocks at level 100) for unlimited rare ores.
  • Use the “Ore Magnet” item to attract nearby ores while mining.
