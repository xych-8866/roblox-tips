Blox Fruits Fighting Styles Guide 2026 – Best Styles & Combos

Fighting styles are your primary damage source when your fruit is on cooldown. This guide ranks every fighting style by performance in PvP, grinding, and boss fights, with recommended fruit combos.

How Fighting Styles Work

Each fighting style has unique moves (Z, X, C, and sometimes V). They are essential for combo extensions and provide consistent damage when your fruit abilities are on cooldown.

Best Fighting Style for Grinding

Electric Claw is the best for grinding. It has fast attacks, good AOE, and works perfectly with Buddha fruit. If you don't have access to Second Sea yet, use Water Kung Fu.

Best Fighting Style for PvP

Godhuman is the undisputed king of PvP. Its moves have incredible damage, stun-lock potential, and combo extension capabilities. Pair it with Kitsune or Dough fruit for the most powerful combos in the game.

Best Fighting Style for Boss Fights

Dragon Talon has the highest DPS against single targets. Use it with Magma or Buddha to melt bosses quickly.

Combos to Master

  • Godhuman + Kitsune: C (Kitsune) → Godhuman C → Z (Kitsune) → Godhuman X → Finisher.
  • Electric Claw + Buddha: Z (Buddha) → Electric Claw C → X (Buddha) → Electric Claw X → Finisher.
  • Death Step + Dragon: Death Step Z → Dragon X → Death Step C → Dragon C → Finisher.

How to Upgrade Fighting Styles

Most styles require defeating specific NPCs, completing quests, or reaching certain levels. Check the style teacher's location in each sea for upgrade requirements.
