🍕 Work at a Pizza Place – Fastest Money & Rank Guide (2026)

💰 Best Job for Quick Cash

  • Cashier: Simple, just take orders. ~$150–$300 per shift.
  • Pizza Baker: Medium difficulty, higher pay (~$250–$500).
  • Delivery Driver: Best for active players. Tips add up (~$400–$800).
  • Manager: Unlocks at rank 5. Passive income from others' work.

⏱️ Shift Efficiency Tips

  • Focus on ONE job per shift – switching lowers multiplier.
  • Use the "Turbo" gamepass (if possible) to move faster.
  • Cook multiple pizzas at once: start 3-4, then box them in sequence.
  • In delivery, plan route: drop off closest orders first.

📈 Rank Up Fast

  • Complete daily tasks (reset every 24h) – gives 2x XP.
  • Work during "Happy Hour" (double money & XP, announced in-game).
  • Buy the "Employee of the Month" gamepass for permanent XP boost.
