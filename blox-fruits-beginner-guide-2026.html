Blox Fruits Beginner Guide 2026 – Fastest Leveling 1-700

New to Blox Fruits? This guide covers the fastest leveling route, best fruits for grinding, and essential gear to get you to Second Sea in under 15 hours.

Choosing Your Starting Fruit

If you have Robux, buy Light or Ice – they are the best for grinding early game. No Robux? Use Flame or Smoke until you find a better one.

Leveling Route (1-700)

  • Level 1-50: Starter Island – kill bandits. Use any fruit.
  • Level 50-150: Pirate Village – bartender quests. Switch to Light or Ice if available.
  • Level 150-300: Desert – desert bandits. AOE fruits (Magma, Light) shine here.
  • Level 300-600: Frozen Village – yetis. Best XP spot in First Sea.
  • Level 600-700: Sky Islands (via Colosseum teleport) – grind until you can start the Second Sea quest.

Best Fighting Styles for Beginners

  • Water Kung Fu: Obtainable early, good combos.
  • Dark Step: High damage, requires Dark Fragments.
  • Electric: Great for mobility, easy to level up.

Essential Gear & Accessories

  • Dark Coat: Drops from Darkbeard (First Sea). Gives +10% melee damage.
  • Ghoul Mask: From the Ghoul questline. Best for grinding.
  • Pilot Helmet: Increases movement speed – great for questing.

Tips for New Players

  • Always keep a boat in your inventory – you'll need it for sea travel.
  • Save fragments for awakening Buddha or Magma – don't waste them on early fruits.
  • Join a crew – you get passive XP boosts and help with raids later.
  • Complete daily quests for bonus XP and Beli.

Common Mistakes

  • Eating a fruit without checking its value – some fruits are rare and better traded.
  • Spending Beli on cosmetics early – save for boat upgrades and stat resets.
  • Ignoring the sword slot – a good sword (e.g., Saber V2) can carry you to Second Sea.
