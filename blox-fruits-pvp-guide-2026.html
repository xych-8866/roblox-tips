Best PvP Fruits (S-Tier)

  • Kitsune – Insane mobility, high damage, best for 1v1.
  • Dragon (Reworked) – Massive AOE, tanky, great for team fights.
  • Leopard – Fast combos, bleed damage, good for chasing.
  • Dough (Awakened) – Incredible stun-lock, requires skill.

Best Fighting Styles for PvP

  • Godhuman – Highest damage, best for combo starters.
  • Electric Claw – Fast, good mobility, easy to learn.
  • Death Step – Great for area denial and escapes.

One-Shot Combo (Kitsune + Godhuman)

  1. C (Kitsune) → dash in
  2. Z (Kitsune) → stun
  3. Godhuman C → uppercut
  4. X (Kitsune) → follow-up
  5. Godhuman X → finisher

Practice in private server. Timing is key.

PvP Tips

  • Always keep Instinct (Observation) active.
  • Use potions (energy, speed) before ranked matches.
  • Learn to flash-step (double-tap jump) to dodge combos.
  • Avoid spamming moves – punish enemy cooldowns.
