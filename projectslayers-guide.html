⚡ Project Slayers – Best Breathing & Clan Guide (2026)

🌬️ Breathing Tier List

  • S-Tier: Sun Breathing (highest DPS), Moon Breathing (AoE king).
  • A-Tier: Water Breathing (balanced), Thunder Breathing (speed).
  • B-Tier: Wind Breathing, Stone Breathing – decent for early game.

🏠 Best Clans

  • Kamado: +15% damage vs demons – best for PvE.
  • Tokito: +10% speed + 10% agility – best for PvP.
  • Rengoku: +20% fire damage – great for Sun Breathing users.

⚔️ Fast Leveling (1-100)

  • Level 1-30: Forest monsters (near Wani village).
  • Level 30-60: Zombies at abandoned temple.
  • Level 60-100: Demon bosses with party (best XP/hour).

🗺️ Boss Drop Rates

  • Rui (Spider Demon): 5% chance for Legendary accessory.
  • Enmu (Train Demon): 2% for Mythical breathing scroll.
  • Daki & Gyutaro: 1% for Sun Breathing.
