Restaurant Tycoon 2 – Ultimate Profit Guide: Best Dishes, Layout & Automation

🎮 Game Introduction

Restaurant Tycoon 2 lets you build and manage your own restaurant. Choose dishes, hire chefs, decorate, and serve customers to earn cash. This guide covers the most profitable dishes, optimal kitchen layout, and how to automate everything.

💡 Basic Tips for Beginners

  • Start with a simple dish – Pizza or Burger. Low ingredient cost, fast cooking time.
  • Hire a dishwasher first – Dirty plates slow down service. Dishwasher costs $500.
  • Price your dishes just below average – Use the "Market Price" tab to see competitors. Undercut by $1-$2.
  • Upgrade your stove – Faster cooking = more customers served per hour.

🔥 Advanced Strategies & Hidden Mechanics

  • Combo meal trick – Offer a burger + fries + drink combo. Customers pay 30% more than separate items.
  • Layout efficiency – Place stove, prep table, and serving counter in a triangle. Minimize chef walking distance.
  • Employee specialization – Train one chef for each dish type (grill, fry, drink). Speed increases by 50%.
  • Event exploitation – During "Food Festival" (every Saturday), sell premium dishes for 2x price.

📊 Most Profitable Dishes (Profit per Minute)

  • Early Game: Cheeseburger ($15 profit, 45 sec cook) → $20/min.
  • Mid Game: Lobster Roll ($60 profit, 90 sec cook) → $40/min.
  • Late Game: Wagyu Steak ($150 profit, 120 sec cook) → $75/min.
  • Endgame: Omakase Sushi ($300 profit, 180 sec cook) → $100/min.

❌ Common Mistakes That Lose Money

  • Over-decorating early – Decorations don't bring customers. Spend money on kitchen upgrades first.
  • Hiring too many chefs – Two chefs are enough for 20 seats. Extra chefs just stand idle.
  • Ignoring customer patience – If wait time > 5 minutes, customers leave. Speed up cooking.
  • Selling only one dish – Customers get bored. Offer at least 3 dishes to keep them happy.

🎯 Final Advice for 5-Star Rating

  • Reach 5 stars by maintaining 95% customer satisfaction for 2 real-time hours.
  • Use the "Daily Special" board to boost a random dish's price by 50%.
  • Save $50k for the "Auto-Chef" upgrade – it automates cooking completely.
  • Join Restaurant Tycoon 2 Discord to share layouts and get rare recipes.
