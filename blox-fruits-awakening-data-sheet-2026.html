How Awakening Works

Each awakenable fruit has 4-5 moves. You must complete Fruit Raids (difficulty depends on your fruit). After each successful raid, you can spend fragments to unlock the next move. You cannot skip moves – you must awaken in sequence.

Fruit‑Specific Awakening Data

🐉 Buddha (Most Important to Awaken)

Total fragments: 7,500 | Recommended order: 3rd move (range) → Ultimate (size) → 2nd → 4th → 1st

  • Move 1 (Z): Small shockwave – low priority
  • Move 2 (X): Dash + knockback – useful for mobility
  • Move 3 (C): Large AOE stomp – massive range increase
  • Move 4 (V): Defense boost – good for survivability
  • Ultimate (F): Enlarged Buddha form – best for grinding

🔥 Magma (Best for Sea Events)

Total fragments: 7,500 | Recommended order: Ultimate (flying) → Move 2 → Move 3 → Move 1 → Move 4

  • Ultimate gives Magma flight – essential for sea beasts
  • Awakened Magma does 2x damage to sea events

🍩 Dough (PvP Beast)

Total fragments: 7,500 | Recommended order: Ultimate → Move 3 → Move 2 → Move 4 → Move 1

  • Dough requires high skill; full awakening transforms playstyle

🌊 Quake (Budget Awakening)

Total fragments: 5,000 (only 4 moves) | Recommended order: Move 2 → Move 3 → Move 1 → Move 4

  • Great for beginners – cheap and strong AOE

🌑 Dark (PvP & Utility)

Total fragments: 5,000 | Recommended order: Move 2 → Ultimate → Move 1 → Move 3

Fastest Fragment Farming Methods

  • Fruit Raids: 200-300 fragments per raid (10-15 min solo with Buddha)
  • Elite Pirates (Third Sea): 5-15 fragments each, respawn every 30 min
  • Rogue Sea Beast (night): 250 fragments per kill
  • Daily chests: 2-10 fragments per chest

Efficiency tip: Save 8,000 fragments before starting a fruit – complete all moves in one session.

Common Mistakes

  • Awakening a fruit you don't use – fragments are precious
  • Skipping Buddha awakening – it's the most impactful for grinding
  • Spending fragments on moves in wrong order (always prioritize AOE or mobility first)
