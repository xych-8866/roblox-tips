Those Who Remain – Ultimate Survival Guide: Best Weapons, Map Camping & Zombie Waves

🎮 Game Introduction

Those Who Remain is a Roblox zombie survival game. Survive waves of increasingly difficult zombies, buy weapons, and complete objectives. This guide covers the best weapons, camping spots, and boss strategies.

💡 Basic Tips for Beginners

  • Start with the M9 pistol – Cheap ($500), decent for first 5 waves.
  • Use barricades – Place them at doorways to slow zombies. Costs $100 each.
  • Stay with your team – Solo is much harder. Group up in a room with one entrance.
  • Complete daily challenges – Each gives $1k and a random weapon crate.

🔥 Advanced Strategies & Hidden Mechanics

  • Wallbang spots – Certain walls are penetrable. Shoot through them to kill zombies safely (e.g., warehouse map).
  • Boss weaknesses – The “Juggernaut” boss takes 3x damage from explosives. Buy grenades before wave 10.
  • Secret weapon room – On the “Prison” map, break the left wall in cell block C to find a free AK-47.
  • Infinite ammo exploit – Use the “Ammo Box” item (costs $500) to refill all weapons instantly. Can be used 3 times per wave.

📊 Best Weapons Tier List (Late Waves)

  • S-Tier (Must-have): Minigun (high DPS), RPG (clears crowds), Ray Gun (infinite ammo).
  • A-Tier (Strong): SCAR-H (accurate), MP5 (fast reload), Shotgun (close range).
  • B-Tier (Decent): M4A1 (balanced), UMP (beginner).

❌ Common Mistakes That End Runs

  • Buying the most expensive weapon first – Save for armor ($2k) and barricades before buying a $10k weapon.
  • Ignoring armor upgrades – Level 2 armor reduces damage by 40%. Essential for wave 15+.
  • Not using grenades on bosses – Bosses have high HP. Save grenades for wave 10, 20, 30 bosses.
  • Camping in a dead-end room – If zombies overwhelm you, you have no escape. Choose rooms with at least two exits.

🎯 Final Advice for Reaching Wave 50

  • Unlock “Double Damage” gamepass for 2x damage.
  • Have at least 3 S-tier weapons fully upgraded.
  • Use “Revive” item ($2k) on wave 40-50 – gives one extra life.
  • Join an active clan for clan bonuses (+10% damage).
