What Are Rainbow Pets?

Rainbow pets are pets with rainbow-colored variants. They deal 2x damage compared to normal pets and have a higher trading value. Some pets can only be obtained as rainbow through specific methods.

How to Get Rainbow Pets

1. Rainbow Potion (Direct Upgrade)

Use a Rainbow Potion on any pet to transform it into a rainbow variant.

How to get Rainbow Potions:

  • Fishing tournament (rare catch).
  • Event rewards (limited time).
  • Trading with other players.
  • Achievement rewards.

2. Rainbow Egg

Hatch a Rainbow Egg for a guaranteed rainbow pet.

How to get Rainbow Eggs:

  • Complete "Master Hatch" achievement.
  • Event rewards.
  • Rare spawn in Tech World.

3. Rainbow Fusion

Combine multiple normal pets of the same type to create a rainbow pet.

Requirements: 3-5 pets of the same type + diamonds.

4. Trading

Buy rainbow pets from other players in the Trading Plaza. Prices vary based on the pet's rarity.

5. Event Rewards

Some events give rainbow pets as completion rewards. Check the event board regularly.

Rainbow Pet Strategy

  1. Save Rainbow Potions – use them only on your best pets (legendaries or huge pets).
  2. Trade up – if you get a rainbow pet you don't need, trade it for a better rainbow pet or multiple normal legendaries.
  3. Farm Rainbow Eggs – use diamonds to buy Rainbow Eggs from the store (if available).
  4. Check events – some events offer free rainbow pets.

Pro Tips

  • Use Rainbow Potions only on legendaries or huge pets – don't waste them on commons.
  • Rainbow pets are much stronger than normal pets – prioritize getting them.
  • If you're F2P, focus on Rainbow Fusion – it's the most consistent method.
  • Check the Trading Plaza daily – sometimes players sell rainbow pets cheap.
