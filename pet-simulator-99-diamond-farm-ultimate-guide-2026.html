Diamond Farming Basics

Diamonds drop from breakables in all zones. Higher zones drop more diamonds per breakable. Your setup (enchants, pets, potions) determines how many diamonds you earn per hour.

Best Pet Loadout

  • Main Pet: Highest damage pet (preferably Huge). Damage affects breakable speed.
  • Secondary Pets: Equip pets with "Rainbow" or "Shiny" traits – they increase breakable speed.
  • Huge Pet Bonus: If you have a Huge pet, always equip it. Gives +100% breakable speed.

Daily Farming Routine

  1. Collect daily login rewards – free diamonds and hour chests.
  2. Equip full V enchants – Diamonds V, Treasure Hunter V, Fortune V, Crits V.
  3. Drink Diamond Potion + Speed Potion – stack them for max output.
  4. Farm Void World – stay near the central platform where breakables cluster.
  5. Repeat 2-3 times per day – each session lasts 30 minutes.

Pro Tips

  • Void World is the only zone worth farming – lower zones aren't efficient.
  • Save diamonds for event eggs – they give the rarest pets.
  • Use an auto-clicker – if allowed, it keeps you farming while AFK.
  • Farm during weekend events – 2x diamond events double your output.
  • Don't waste diamonds on basic eggs – use coins for those.
