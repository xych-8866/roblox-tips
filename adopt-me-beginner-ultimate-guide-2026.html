New to Adopt Me? This guide covers all the basics: getting your first pet, earning bucks, buying a house, trading, and navigating the event calendar.

Your First Steps

  1. Choose your character: Customize your look at the salon (free).
  2. Get your first pet: You start with a starter egg – hatch it immediately.
  3. Explore the map: Visit the nursery, school, hospital, and pizza place.
  4. Start earning bucks: Pizza delivery is the fastest way to earn money.

Getting Your First House

  • Starter House: Free (basic).
  • Cozy Cottage: $5,000 – your first upgrade.
  • Suburban Home: $15,000 – more rooms.
  • Best strategy: Save up $5,000 for the Cozy Cottage, then gradually upgrade.

Understanding Pets

  • Rarities: Common → Uncommon → Rare → Ultra-Rare → Legendary → Mythical.
  • Aging: Newborn → Junior → Pre-Teen → Teen → Post-Teen → Full Grown.
  • How to age: Complete tasks (eating, drinking, sleeping, playing).
  • Best pet for beginners: Any pet – they all work the same.

Trading Basics

  • How to trade: Click on a player → Trade → Select items → Accept.
  • What to trade: Duplicate pets, items you don't need.
  • What not to trade: Your best pet – keep it until you understand values.
  • Scam prevention: Always use the official trade window – never trade through chat.

Daily Routine for Beginners

  1. Login: Claim daily reward (free bucks/pets).
  2. Hidden cash stashes: Collect $500+ in under 5 minutes.
  3. Pizza delivery: Do 10-20 deliveries for extra bucks.
  4. Pet aging: Play with your pet and complete tasks.
  5. Check events: See if there's an active event with free items.

Pro Tips for New Players

  • Don't waste bucks on cosmetics early – save for houses and eggs.
  • Join a family – you get 2x task speed and more XP.
  • Collect hidden cash stashes daily – they add up.
  • Use a Hot Dog Stand overnight – it pays for itself in one night.
  • Check the event board regularly – many events give free pets.
