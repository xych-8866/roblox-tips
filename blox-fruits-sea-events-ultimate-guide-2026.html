What Are Sea Events?

Sea events are special encounters that happen while sailing between islands. They are the fastest way to farm fragments, Beli, and rare items.

1. Ship Raid (Most Common)

Spawn Location: Second and Third Sea – look for the ship icon on the minimap.

Spawn Time: Every 30 minutes.

Rewards: 50-150 fragments, 10-50 gems, random fruit (common to legendary).

Best Fruit: Magma, Buddha.

Strategy: Focus the captain first – he drops the best loot. Team up with crew members for faster completion.

2. Sea Beast (Random Spawn)

Spawn Location: Any sea – random spawn, more frequent at night.

Spawn Time: Random – requires a boat to engage.

Rewards: 30-80 fragments, 5-20 gems, Sea Beast drops (cores, scales).

Best Fruit: Magma (2x damage on sea events), Dragon.

Strategy: Stay at a distance and spam ranged attacks. Dodge its dive attack by moving sideways.

3. Rogue Sea Beast (Best Rewards)

Spawn Location: Third Sea – only at night.

Spawn Time: Rare spawn – guaranteed rewards.

Rewards: 250-300 fragments, 100-200 gems, guaranteed legendary fruit, Rogue Sea Beast Crown (accessory).

Best Fruit: Magma (awakened), Dragon.

Strategy: Requires a large crew (5+ players). Focus on its weak point – the glowing spot on its back.

4. Kraken (New – July 2026)

Spawn Location: Third Sea – random at night.

Spawn Time: Random – new sea event added in July 2026 update.

Rewards: 300-500 fragments, guaranteed legendary fruit, Kraken Crown (accessory).

Best Fruit: Magma, Dragon, Blizzard.

Strategy: Requires at least 3 players. Use ranged attacks – Kraken has tentacle attacks that can one-shot you.

5. Leviathan (Toughest)

Spawn Location: Frozen Dimension – sea event.

Spawn Time: Random at night – requires boat.

Rewards: 250 fragments, Leviathan Crown (accessory).

Best Fruit: Magma (2x sea damage).

Strategy: Dodge its tentacle attacks by moving sideways. Use long-range attacks to avoid its ice breath.

Best Fruits for Sea Events

  • Magma (Awakened): 2x damage on sea events – mandatory for efficient farming.
  • Dragon (Reworked): Massive AOE, good for groups.
  • Blizzard: Good crowd control for sea events.
  • Buddha: Tanky, good for survival.

Pro Tips

  • Always carry a boat in your inventory – sea events can spawn anytime.
  • Join a crew with active members – sea events are easier with a team.
  • Use the "Sea Event" channel in Discord to find active spawns.
  • Server hop after completing a Ship Raid to respawn it faster.
  • Rogue Sea Beast, Kraken, and Leviathan require a group – don't try them solo.
