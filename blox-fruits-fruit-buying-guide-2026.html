Fruits to Never Buy

  • Bomb, Smoke, Spike – Complete waste of Beli. You'll find better fruits naturally.
  • Spring – Useless in combat and grinding.
  • Kilo – Only for memes.

Best First Fruit to Buy

Buddha is the single best investment for any player. It makes grinding 2x faster, enables solo raids, and works well with any fighting style. Save 1.2M Beli and buy it as soon as you reach Level 700.

Best Fruit for Beginners (Budget)

Light at 650k Beli is the best budget fruit. Fast travel, decent damage, and good for both grinding and PvP. You can use it all the way to Second Sea.

Pro Tip

Don't waste Beli on fruits you can get from raids. Save your Beli for stat resets, race rerolls, and Buddha/Magma. If you find a rare fruit (Dough, Dragon, Kitsune), trade it for Beli rather than eating it unless you plan to use it long-term.
