Car Dealership Tycoon – Ultimate Money Guide: Best Upgrades & Fastest Cars

🎮 Game Introduction

Car Dealership Tycoon is a Roblox business sim where you build, customize, and sell cars. Expand your dealership, hire employees, and race to earn cash. This guide covers the best early investments, fastest money-making methods, and the best cars for each tier.

💡 Basic Tips for Beginners

  • Upgrade your showroom first – Level 2 showroom attracts 2x more customers.
  • Hire a mechanic early – Mechanics repair cars automatically, saving you time.
  • Sell the cheapest car first – Fast turnover builds initial capital.
  • Use the "Boost" button – Every 10 minutes, click the boost for 5 minutes of 2x sales.

🔥 Advanced Strategies & Hidden Mechanics

  • Car flipping trick – Buy used cars from the auction, repair them (cost $500), then sell for 3x price.
  • Employee synergy – One salesman + one mechanic + one detailer = 40% faster sales.
  • Racing exploit – In the race event, use nitrous only on straightaways. Drift on corners to maintain speed.
  • VIP lot – Unlock at level 10. Display only luxury cars; rich customers pay 50% more.

📊 Best Cars by Tier (Stats & Profit)

  • Starter (Free): Sedan – $5k profit. Good for first 10 sales.
  • Economy ($25k): Hatchback – $12k profit, fast turnover.
  • Mid ($100k): Sports Coupe – $45k profit, best for mid-game.
  • Luxury ($500k): Supercar – $250k profit, unlocks VIP lot.
  • Endgame ($2M): Hypercar – $1.2M profit, fastest car for racing.

❌ Common Mistakes That Slow Progress

  • Saving for expensive cars too early – Buy 3-4 mid-tier cars before saving for luxury.
  • Ignoring employee upgrades – Level 2 employees work 30% faster. Worth the $50k.
  • Not participating in races – Race rewards give $100k+ and rare parts.
  • Forgetting to collect daily bonus – Log in daily for $20k + a free car.

🎯 Final Advice for Becoming a Tycoon

  • Reinvest 80% of your profits into upgrades. Only take 20% for car purchases.
  • Save for the Hypercar after you have 5 luxury cars generating passive income.
  • Join a Car Dealership Tycoon Discord server for trade deals (rare cars).
  • Don't neglect the garage expansion – more display slots = more simultaneous sales.
