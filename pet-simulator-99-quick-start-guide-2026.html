Pet Simulator 99 Quick Start Guide 2026 – Get Rich in 7 Days

New to PS99? This 7-day plan takes you from zero to rich, with diamonds, pets, and progression milestones – all without spending real money.

Day 1: Get Started

  • Hatch your starter egg – you get 3 common pets.
  • Complete the tutorial – it gives coins and unlocks basic areas.
  • Start grinding in Grassy Plains – farm coins and breakables.
  • Buy the basic egg upgrades (Coin V enchant when you can).
  • Goal: Reach level 10 and unlock 3 pet slots.

Day 2: Build Your Farm

  • Unlock Desert Zone – better coin drops.
  • Start saving for Coin V enchant – it doubles coin farming.
  • Buy at least 2 new pets from the egg machine.
  • Complete daily quests for bonus rewards.
  • Goal: Reach level 25 and unlock 4 pet slots.

Day 3: First Diamond Farm

  • Unlock Ice Zone – first zone with diamond drops.
  • Equip Coin V enchant (if you have it) – speed up farming.
  • Start saving diamonds – don't spend them yet.
  • Complete achievements for extra diamonds.
  • Goal: Save 5,000 diamonds.

Day 4: Enchant Upgrades

  • Buy Diamonds V enchant – game-changer for diamond farming.
  • Unlock Tech World – better diamond and coin drops.
  • Start farming diamonds actively – at least 30 minutes/day.
  • Complete event quests if there's an active event.
  • Goal: Save 15,000 diamonds.

Day 5: Expand Your Farm

  • Unlock Void World – best diamond farming zone.
  • Buy Treasure Hunter V enchant – more breakables = more drops.
  • Use Diamond Potion (bought with diamonds) for 2x diamonds.
  • Farm Void World for 1 hour with all buffs active.
  • Goal: Reach 100,000 diamonds.

Day 6: First Huge Pet

  • Save 200,000+ diamonds.
  • Buy a low-tier huge pet from the Trading Plaza.
  • Equip it – huge pets give +100% breakable speed.
  • This is the biggest power spike in the game.
  • Goal: Own at least 1 huge pet.

Day 7: Start Trading

  • Use your huge pet to farm more diamonds.
  • Save 300,000+ diamonds.
  • Trade for better huge pets or exclusives.
  • You now have a steady diamond income and a huge pet – you're officially "rich."
  • Goal: Build a tradeable collection.

Pro Tips

  • Prioritize enchants – Coin V and Diamonds V are mandatory.
  • Farm during weekend events – double diamond and coin events speed everything up.
  • Don't waste diamonds on expensive eggs – use coins for eggs, save diamonds for enchants and huge pets.
  • Use an auto-clicker – if allowed, it keeps you farming while AFK.
