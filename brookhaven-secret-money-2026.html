Daily Secret Money Spots (Respawn Every 4 Hours)

  • Beach Cave: At the far end of the beach, inside the small cave – $200 cash.
  • Treehouse behind Salon: Left side of map, climb up – $150.
  • Rocket Ship near Playground: Inside the rocket, hidden switch gives $50.
  • Police Station Basement: Behind the desk – $100.
  • Hospital Roof: Use ladder behind building – $75.

Fast Money Jobs (Repeatable)

  • Pizza Delivery: $50 per delivery (fastest – 10 deliveries = $500 in 5 minutes).
  • Police Officer: Arrest criminals – $100 per arrest.
  • Firefighter: Put out fires – $75 per fire.
  • Doctor: Heal players – $40 per heal.

Passive Income

  • Own a house with a vending machine – earns $10 every 10 minutes.
  • Set up a lemonade stand in busy area – can earn $500–1000 per hour.

Pro Tips

  • Collect all secret spots first (takes 5 minutes, gives ~$600).
  • Then do Pizza Delivery repeatedly – it's the best time/money ratio.
  • Server hop after finishing jobs – fresh spawns reset some hidden stashes.
