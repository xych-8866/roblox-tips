Shooting Range Simulator – Ultimate Guide: Best Guns, Fast Coins & Accuracy Tips

🎮 Game Introduction

Shooting Range Simulator is a Roblox FPS training game. Shoot targets to earn coins, unlock new guns, and improve accuracy. Compete on leaderboards and complete daily challenges. This guide covers the best weapons, coin farming methods, and aiming tricks.

💡 Basic Tips for Beginners

  • Aim for headshots – Headshots give 2x coin bonus and more XP.
  • Use the starter pistol until level 5 – It has zero recoil, great for learning.
  • Complete daily targets – Each day gives 500 coins and a weapon crate.
  • Adjust your sensitivity – Lower sensitivity (3-4) improves accuracy.

🔥 Advanced Strategies & Hidden Mechanics

  • Scope glitch – Tap right-click quickly to reset aim sway. Keeps crosshair steady.
  • Target patterns – Moving targets follow the same path every 30 seconds. Memorize spawn points.
  • Coin multiplier stacking – Buy the "Coin Magnet" upgrade ($5k) and "2x Coin" gamepass for 3x earnings.
  • Perfect reload – Reload when the progress bar hits the green zone for 20% faster reload speed.

📊 Best Guns Tier List (Ranked)

  • S-Tier (Meta): AWM-S (1-shot kill), Vector (fast fire rate), SCAR-L (low recoil).
  • A-Tier (Strong): M4A1 (balanced), MP5 (accurate), Deagle (high damage).
  • B-Tier (Decent): UMP (beginner), AK-47 (unpredictable recoil).
  • C-Tier (Avoid): Pistol (outclassed), Shotgun (inconsistent at range).

❌ Common Mistakes That Waste Coins

  • Buying expensive guns too early – Stick with M4A1 until level 15; it's cost-effective.
  • Ignoring attachments – Red dot sight improves accuracy by 15% for only 1k coins.
  • Skipping target practice – The practice range gives free ammo and +50 coins per 10 targets.
  • Not using the shooting range leaderboard – Top 10 weekly earn 10k bonus coins.

🎯 Final Advice for Climbing Leaderboards

  • Master one S-tier weapon (AWM-S). Practice quickscoping for 10 minutes daily.
  • Focus on headshots only – ignore body shots for score.
  • Play during off-peak hours (early morning) for fewer server lags.
  • Join a shooting clan for clan bonuses (+5% coins).
