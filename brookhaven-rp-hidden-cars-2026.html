Brookhaven RP Hidden Cars 2026 – Secret Vehicle Locations

Beyond the dealership, there are hidden cars scattered around the map. Some are free, some require quests. This guide shows you where to find them.

1. Abandoned Muscle Car

Behind the gas station, there's a rusty red muscle car partially covered by bushes. Click it repeatedly until the engine starts – you'll unlock it for free.

2. Beach Buggy (Free)

Under the pier at the beach, there's a yellow buggy hidden in the shadows. No quest required, just claim it.

3. Police Interceptor

Inside the police station garage, you can find a police car. Unlock it by completing the "Speed Trap" quest (catch 5 speeders).

4. Vintage Convertible

Behind the museum, there's a classic convertible that spawns only at night. Click it between 10 PM and 5 AM in-game time.

5. Ice Cream Truck (Rare)

Spawns randomly near the playground. If you see it, get in quickly – it despawns after 2 minutes. Once you've driven it once, it's added to your garage.

How to Unlock All Hidden Cars

  • Explore every corner of the map – many cars are hidden behind buildings or in alleyways.
  • Check back daily – some cars only spawn on certain days of the week.
  • Join a server with fewer players – rare car spawns are more frequent in quiet servers.

Pro Tip

After unlocking a hidden car, drive it to the dealership to save it to your garage permanently. If you don't save it, you'll have to find it again.
