🎮 Roblox Beginner Guide – How to Get Started (2026)

Step 1: Create an Account

  • Go to Roblox.com and click "Sign Up".
  • Enter your birthday (must be 13+ for full features).
  • Choose a username and password – write them down!
  • Verify your email to secure your account.

Step 2: Customize Your Avatar

  • Click on the "Avatar" tab in the left menu.
  • Change skin tone, clothes, and accessories.
  • Use free items from Marketplace – search "free" and sort by price.

Step 3: Find Games to Play

  • Click "Discover" or search for popular games: Adopt Me, Brookhaven, Pet Simulator 99, Blox Fruits, etc.
  • Check "Active Players" count – higher means more fun.
  • Read game descriptions and ratings before joining.

Step 4: Safety Settings

  • Go to Settings → Privacy.
  • Set "Who can chat with me?" to "Friends" (recommended for kids).
  • Enable Account PIN to prevent unauthorized changes.
  • Never share your password with anyone.
