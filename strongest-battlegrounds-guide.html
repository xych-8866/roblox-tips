The Strongest Battlegrounds – Ultimate PvP Guide: Combos, Rankings & Tech

🎮 Game Introduction

The Strongest Battlegrounds is a Roblox fighting game inspired by One Punch Man. Choose a hero, battle in 1v1 or 2v2, and climb the leaderboard. This guide covers the best characters, true combos, and movement tech.

💡 Basic Tips for New Players

  • Learn your character's cooldowns – Every move has a timer. Don't waste your ultimate.
  • Block frequently – Hold F when enemy approaches. Blocks reduce damage by 50%.
  • Use dodge (double tap movement) – Invincibility frames for 0.3 seconds.
  • Save dash for escape – Dash (Q) is your only get-out-of-combo tool.

🔥 Advanced Combos & Hidden Mechanics

  • Saitama true combo: M1 x3 → Normal Punch → M1 x3 → Consecutive Normal Punches. Deals 80% HP.
  • Garou tech: After Whirlwind Iron Cutting Fist, cancel into dash → M1 → Hunter's Grasp. Unblockable.
  • Speed-o'-Sound Sonic: Use Shuriken to bait block, then Flash Kick (hits behind block).
  • Ultimate baiting: Pretend to run, then turn and use ultimate when enemy dashes at you.

📊 Character Tier List (1v1)

  • S-Tier (Best): Saitama, Garou (Human), Boros – high damage, versatile combos.
  • A-Tier (Strong): Speed-o'-Sound Sonic, Genos, Tanktop Master – good mix of range and pressure.
  • B-Tier (Situational): Mumen Rider, Metal Bat – requires perfect timing.
  • C-Tier (Weak): Child Emperor – low damage, predictable.

❌ Common Mistakes That Lose Matches

  • Spamming moves randomly – You'll get punished during recovery frames.
  • Blocking too long – Block breaks after 2 seconds of sustained attacks.
  • Using ultimate at full health – Save for when enemy is below 40% to secure kill.
  • Ignoring map hazards – Some maps have falling platforms or walls that can trap you.

🎯 Final Advice for Ranked Mode

  • Warm up in training mode for 5 minutes before ranked.
  • Learn at least two characters – in case your main gets countered.
  • Watch top players on YouTube to learn movement patterns.
  • Don't rage queue after a loss – take a break.
