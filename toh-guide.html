Tower of Hell – Speedrun Shortcuts & Proven Strategies (2026)

🏃 Basic Movement Tips

  • Shift Lock: Always enable – gives better camera control for precise jumps.
  • Edge Jumping: Jump slightly before reaching the edge to gain extra distance.
  • Strafe Jumps: Hold “W” + “A” or “D” while jumping to cover longer gaps.

🗺️ Best Shortcuts on Common Towers

  • Lava Tower: Skip the first spinning platform by wall-jumping on the left cliff.
  • Ice Tower: Use angled jumps on slippery blocks – aim for corners, not centers.
  • Sand Tower: Fall through false floors intentionally to reach lower, faster route.
  • Forest Tower: Climb vines by alternating “W” + space repeatedly for speed boost.

⚡ Advanced Techniques

  • Wrap Glitch: On certain towers, jumping into a wall at the right angle teleports you up 2-3 floors. Works on “Glass” and “Neon” towers.
  • Double Jump Exploit: After wall-jumping, tap “W” in mid-air to reset jump count. Practice in practice mode.
  • Reset Boost: Click “Reset” while falling – respawns you slightly higher than original position.

🎯 Tower-Specific Shortcuts

  • Radio Tower (floor 4-6): Jump from the antenna to the window ledge skipping 2 floors.
  • Prison Tower (floor 8-10): Use the broken fence to climb directly to the final platform.
  • Aquarium Tower: Swim through the left corner pipe – hidden teleport to floor 9.

⏱️ Speedrun Routine

  • Memorize 3-5 shortcuts per tower type.
  • Practice each shortcut in private server before racing.
  • Use “F3” to check FPS – higher FPS smooths jumps.
  • Aim for sub-2 minutes on easy towers, sub-5 on hard.

❌ Common Mistakes

  • Looking down while jumping – reduces air control.
  • Holding “Shift” too long – slows your speed on flat surfaces.
  • Ignoring reset jumps – using reset as a tool, not a penalty.
