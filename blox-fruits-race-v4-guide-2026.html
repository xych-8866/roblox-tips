Blox Fruits Race V4 Awakening Guide 2026 – Full Tutorial

Race V4 is the ultimate power-up in Blox Fruits. This guide covers step-by-step requirements for all races: Human, Rabbit, Shark, Angel, Ghoul. Updated for 2026.

Prerequisites (Before Starting)

  • Level 1500+ (recommended)
  • Race V3 fully awakened (all gears unlocked)
  • Access to Third Sea
  • Have a race reroll or be prepared to grind fragments

Step 1: Obtain Race V3 (if not already)

Complete each race's V2 → V3 trials. For Human: defeat 10 players in PvP. Rabbit: collect 100 flowers. Shark: survive waterfall. Angel: find altar. Ghoul: defeat 20 NPCs at night.

Step 2: Unlock the Temple of Time

In Third Sea, go to the Floating Turtle. Find the hidden door behind the waterfall. Use a God's Chalice + Fruit of Sorrow to open the Temple of Time.

Step 3: Race V4 Trials (All Races)

  • Human: Defeat 5 awakened bosses in the temple
  • Rabbit: Complete speed course under 1 minute
  • Shark: Survive 5 waves of water elementals
  • Angel: Heal 5000 HP of allies
  • Ghoul: Deal 10000 damage without dying

Step 4: Final Awakening

After completing your race trial, go to the central altar. Defeat the Ancient Guardian (level 2000). Once defeated, interact with the crystal to unlock Race V4 abilities.

Race V4 Abilities

  • Human: Full Body Haki – damage boost + stun immunity
  • Rabbit: Infinite Speed – dash cooldown removed
  • Shark: Water Body – 80% damage reduction in water
  • Angel: Wings of Light – flight + team healing
  • Ghoul: Bloodlust – life steal doubled
