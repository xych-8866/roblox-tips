How to Get Free Legendary Pets (No Robux)

The Golden Mermaid is the legendary pet. To get it for free:

  1. Collect 500 Summer Shells (event currency) by playing daily minigames.
  2. Complete the “Beach Cleanup” quest 5 times.
  3. Trade 3 ultra-rare pets to the event merchant.

Pro tip: focus on the fishing minigame – it gives 2x shells on weekends.

Limited Items & Furniture

Farming Strategy for F2P Players

Run the “Sandcastle building” activity repeatedly – 15 shells per 2 minutes. Combine with pet aging to maximize efficiency. Aim for 100 shells per hour.
