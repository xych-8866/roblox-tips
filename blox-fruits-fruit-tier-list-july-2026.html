How to Read This Tier List

Fruits are ranked based on three factors:

  • Grinding (PvE): How fast you can level up and farm Beli.
  • PvP: Combat effectiveness against other players.
  • Boss Fights: Damage output and survivability against bosses.

D-Tier (Avoid)

  • Kilo, Smoke, Spike – Complete waste of Beli. You'll find better fruits naturally.
  • Spin – Useless in combat and grinding.

Best Fruit for Each Playstyle

  • Grinding: Buddha (best), Magma, Light.
  • PvP: Kitsune, Dragon, Leopard, Dough (Awakened).
  • Boss Fights: Dragon, Magma, Buddha.
  • Sea Events: Magma (2x damage), Dragon.

Pro Tips

  • Focus on Buddha first – it's the best grinding fruit and lets you farm fragments for awakening.
  • If you find a rare fruit (Dough, Dragon, Kitsune), don't eat it unless you're sure – trade it for Beli or for fruits that fit your playstyle.
  • Check value lists before eating a fruit – some are worth millions of Beli.
  • Awakening makes a fruit much stronger – prioritize awakening Buddha, Magma, or Dough.
