Adopt Me Neon Pet Guide 2026 – How to Make Neon Pets Fast

Making a neon pet requires aging four fully-grown pets and combining them. This guide shows the fastest methods, from newborn to mega neon.

What is a Neon Pet?

A neon pet has a glowing body and special effects. It requires 4 fully-grown pets of the same species. Mega neon = 16 pets (4 neons).

Step-by-Step: From Newborn to Neon

  1. Aging Phase: Raise 4 identical pets from Newborn → Junior → Pre-teen → Teen → Post-teen → Full Grown.
  2. Combine in Neon Cave: Go to the secret cave behind the playground (under the waterfall).
  3. Place all 4 fully-grown pets on the glowing platforms inside.
  4. Wait 30 seconds – the pets merge into one neon pet.

Fastest Ways to Age Pets

  • Use a Baby or Family member – tasks appear twice as fast.
  • Equip Speed Potion from the hospital (cost 20 bucks).
  • Complete School tasks repeatedly – they give the most XP.
  • Use Premium Pet Food if you have Robux (not necessary).
  • AFK method: Enter a family with an alt account, feed pet while baby sleeps.

Neon Cave Location

Behind the main playground, follow the waterfall until you see a cave entrance. Enter and walk to the neon fusion room.

Mega Neon (16 pets)

Make 4 neon pets of the same species → fuse them in the same cave → mega neon (rainbow glow).

Best Pets to Neon for Trading Value

  • Shadow Dragon / Bat Dragon (highest value)
  • Frost Dragon
  • Unicorn (common but always wanted)
  • Legendary event pets (limited time)
