Pet Simulator 99 Pet Acquisition Guide 2026 – How to Get Every Pet

There are many ways to get pets in PS99. This guide covers every acquisition method, from hatching to trading to secret pets.

1. Hatching (Most Common Method)

Hatch eggs to get pets. Different eggs give different rarities:

  • Cracked Egg – Common to rare. Low legendary chance.
  • Pet Egg – Uncommon to legendary. Better chance.
  • Royal Egg – Best legendary chance among store eggs.
  • Event Eggs – Limited time, contains event pets.

2. Trading (Fastest Way to Get Specific Pets)

Trade with other players in the Trading Plaza. You can offer:

  • Diamonds – The most common trading currency.
  • Other pets – Trade duplicates for pets you need.
  • Enchants – Diamonds V and Treasure Hunter V are highly valued.
  • Potions – Luck and diamond potions are popular trade items.

3. Event Rewards (Limited Time Pets)

During special events (Easter, Halloween, Summer, Christmas), you can earn event-exclusive pets by:

  • Completing event quests.
  • Opening event eggs with event currency.
  • Reaching event milestones.

4. Secret Pets (Hidden Locations)

  • Tech Egg – Behind the giant computer in Tech World.
  • Jungle Egg – Inside the waterfall in Jungle zone.
  • Crystal Egg – In Fantasy World, break the dark purple crystal.
  • Void Egg – In Void World, on the floating island.

5. Codes (Free Pets)

Redeem working codes for free pets:

  • Check our PS99 Secret Codes guide for updated codes.
  • Codes often give event pets or exclusive items.

6. Challenges & Achievements

  • Complete the "Master Hatch" challenge – reward: Lucky Egg.
  • Complete the "Collector" achievement – reward: 1000 diamonds.
  • Reach level 100 with a pet – unlock rainbow evolution.

Pro Tips

  • Save diamonds for event eggs – they give the rarest pets.
  • Use Lucky Potions before hatching to increase legendary chances.
  • Trade duplicates for pets you don't have.
  • Check value lists before trading – avoid bad deals.
