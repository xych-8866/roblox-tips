Jailbreak – Ultimate Prison Break Guide: Best Escape Routes & Money Farming

🎮 Game Introduction

Jailbreak is a Roblox cops-and-robbers game. Play as a prisoner and escape, then rob banks, stores, and casinos. Or play as a cop and arrest criminals. This guide covers the fastest escape routes, best vehicles, and money-making strategies.

💡 Basic Tips for Prisoners

  • Collect tools first – Find a shovel, flashlight, and keycard in the prison yard or cells.
  • Escape via the sewer – Dig under the fence near the left corner of the yard. Fastest solo escape.
  • Use the supply drop – Every 10 minutes, a crate lands near the prison. Contains weapons.
  • Stay low when cops are near – Crouching reduces noise and visibility.

🔥 Advanced Strategies & Hidden Mechanics

  • Cop disguise trick – If you steal a cop car, you can drive past police without suspicion for 30 seconds.
  • Bank heist duo – One player distracts cops, the other drills the vault. Split the cash.
  • Black market location – Behind the gas station, buy C4 and grappling hooks for $10k each.
  • Escape via helicopter – Steal the police helicopter from the roof of the police station. Requires pilot's license ($5k).

📊 Best Vehicles for Robbing

  • S-Tier: Bugatti (fastest for getaways), Blackhawk (helicopter, avoids traffic).
  • A-Tier: Tesla (silent, good for sneaking), SUV (durable).
  • B-Tier: Motorcycle (nimble), Muscle Car (fast but loud).

❌ Common Mistakes That Get You Arrested

  • Robbing the bank without a disguise – Always wear a mask from the black market.
  • Driving past police with stolen car – Switch cars frequently or use helicopter.
  • Ignoring the security cameras – Shoot them out before entering a store.
  • Teaming with untrustworthy players – They might betray you for the bounty.

🎯 Final Advice for Becoming a Millionaire

  • Save $1M for the "Private Island" – gives passive income of $50k per hour.
  • Rob the casino vault between midnight and 4 AM – less police patrol.
  • Use the "Double Money" gamepass (if you have Robux) to double all earnings.
  • Practice the cargo plane heist – it gives $200k each time.
