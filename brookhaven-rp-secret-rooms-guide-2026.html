Brookhaven RP Secret Rooms Guide 2026 – Hidden Locations & What's Inside

Brookhaven RP has many hidden rooms and secret locations that most players never find. This guide reveals all of them and what treasures they contain.

1. Police Station Secret Basement

Location: Police station, behind the evidence locker.

How to access: Click the brick on the back wall – it opens a hidden door.

Rewards: $100 cash, police uniform (rare), and a key to the armory.

2. Hospital Rooftop Room

Location: Hospital, elevator – go to the top floor then climb the ladder.

How to access: Take the elevator to the top floor, then climb the ladder to the rooftop.

Rewards: $75 cash, medical kit (heals 50 HP).

3. Museum Hidden Vault

Location: Museum, behind the dinosaur skeleton.

How to access: Click the bones in the right order (leg, tail, head, rib).

Rewards: $500 cash + golden artifact (sell for 200 bucks).

4. Beach Cave (Secret Room)

Location: Beach, behind the waterfall (climb through the water).

How to access: Walk through the waterfall – there's a cave behind it.

Rewards: $150 cash, beach ball prop, surfboard (limited).

5. Grocery Store Stockroom

Location: Grocery store, behind the freezer section.

How to access: Click the freezer door 5 times – it opens to the stockroom.

Rewards: $50 cash, free food items, and a coupon for 10% off.

6. School Hidden Library

Location: School, behind the bookshelf in the main hallway.

How to access: Pull the red book – the bookshelf swings open.

Rewards: $200 cash, rare book (sell for 100 bucks), and a student ID.

7. Gas Station Underground Room

Location: Gas station, behind the counter.

How to access: Click the floor tile that's slightly raised – it opens a hatch.

Rewards: $300 cash, car parts (upgrade your vehicle), and a fuel can.
