Island Order (Fastest XP per Hour)

  • Level 1-50: Starter Island – kill bandits. Use Light or Ice fruit.
  • Level 50-150: Pirate Village – quests from bartender. Switch to Buddha at level 100 if possible.
  • Level 150-300: Desert – desert bandits. AOE fruits (Buddha, Magma) shine here.
  • Level 300-600: Frozen Village – yeti quests. Stay in the cave.
  • Level 600-900: Sky Islands (use teleport from Colosseum) – best spot: upper cloud.
  • Level 900-1200: Green Zone – elite pirates. Group them for AOE.
  • Level 1200-1500: Second Sea: Kingdom of Rose – long bridge.
  • Level 1500-1900: Third Sea: Great Tree – acolytes.
  • Level 1900-2550: Floating Turtle & Castle on Sea – final grind.

Best Fruits for Leveling (Ranked)

  • #1 Buddha (Awakened): Best for grinding – huge AOE, tanky, low cooldown.
  • #2 Light: Fast travel, good AOE, cheap to obtain.
  • #3 Magma: High damage AOE, perfect for sea events and bosses.
  • #4 Ice: Great crowd control, easy to spam.

Fighting Styles & Accessories

Accessories: Ghoul Mask (life steal) + Hunter Cape (XP boost from raids).
