Blox Fruits Fragment Farming 2026 – Fastest Ways to Get Fragments

Fragments are essential for awakening fruits, buying race rerolls, and crafting. This guide ranks the best fragment farming methods.

1. Raids (Best Overall)

Complete Fruit Raids or Regular Raids. Each successful raid gives 100-300 fragments. Use Buddha fruit for solo raids. Join a crew for faster runs.

2. Elite Pirates (Third Sea)

Kill elite pirates around the Floating Turtle. Each gives 5-15 fragments. Respawn every 30 minutes. Good for passive farming between raids.

3. Sea Events

Participate in Ship Raids, Sea Beasts, and Rogue Sea Beast. The Rogue Sea Beast drops 250 fragments per kill. Spawns at night.

4. Bosses

Darkbeard (30 frags), Awakened Ice Admiral (50 frags), Diamond (20 frags). Server hop to respawn bosses quickly.

5. Daily Chests

Collect chests in the Sea of Treats (Third Sea). Each chest gives 2-10 fragments. Route: start at Floating Turtle, circle clockwise.
