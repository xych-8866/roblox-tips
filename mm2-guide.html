Murder Mystery 2: Rare Weapons Value & Trading Guide 2026

🔪 Godly Tier (Highest Value)

  • Chroma Laser (Godly): ~12,000 value – one of the rarest chromas.
  • Corrupt (Godly): ~8,500 value – sought after for its dark effect.
  • Icebreaker (Godly): ~6,000 value – stable, good for starting trades.

⚔️ Ancient Tier (Very Rare)

  • Harvester (Ancient): ~7,500 value – limited event item, rising.
  • Icewing (Ancient): ~1,200 value – lower but still demanded.

📈 Best Trading Strategies

  • Trade Chromas for Sets: Complete sets (e.g., Virtual, Nebula) increase total value.
  • Avoid Overpaying for New Items: New weapons drop in value after 1-2 weeks.
  • Use Mid-Tier Weapons as Bait: Trade a godly + small add for a higher tier.

🤝 Where to Trade Safely

  • Official MM2 Discord (use verified middlemen).
  • Supreme MM2 Values – check live value list before accepting trades.
  • Avoid random servers – scammers use “duping” tricks.

❌ Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Trading without checking Supreme/MM2V values.
  • Accepting “item sets” that are incomplete.
  • Trusting “trust trades” (always use middleman).

💎 Pro Tips

  • Collect seasonal weapons (Halloween, Christmas) – they spike in value off-season.
  • Stack multiple low-tier legendaries to trade up to a godly.
  • Never trade your entire inventory – keep 2-3 stable items for quick trades.
