Breaking Point – Ultimate Guide: Best Maps, Tips & Tricks for Survivors and Murderers

🎮 Game Introduction

Breaking Point is a Roblox social deduction game. One player is the murderer, others are survivors. Survivors must complete tasks and identify the killer, while the murderer tries to eliminate everyone. This guide covers best strategies for both roles.

💡 Basic Tips for Beginners

  • Always check the map – Know where tasks are located to avoid being cornered.
  • Use the report button – If you find a body, report immediately to call an emergency meeting.
  • Stick with a group – Lone survivors are easy targets.
  • Listen for footsteps – The murderer’s footsteps are louder than survivors’.

🔥 Advanced Strategies & Hidden Mechanics

  • Faking tasks – As murderer, stand near task locations to look busy. Don’t actually do tasks (they give away your role).
  • Voting manipulation – In meetings, accuse someone randomly to create confusion. Survivors often vote without evidence.
  • Sabotage doors – On maps with doors (e.g., Mansion), lock doors to trap survivors.
  • Survivor escape trick – If you see the murderer, run toward a crowded area. They won’t risk being seen.

📊 Best Maps for Each Role

  • For Survivors: Laboratory (many hiding spots), City (lots of witnesses).
  • For Murderer: Mansion (twisty corridors), Ship (isolated rooms).
  • Balanced: School – good visibility for both sides.

❌ Common Mistakes That Get You Killed

  • Wandering alone – You’re asking to be killed.
  • Accusing without proof – You’ll be voted out as “suspicious”.
  • Not using the map – Knowing escape routes saves lives.
  • Standing still too long – Murderers look for motionless targets.

🎯 Final Advice for Win Streaks

  • As survivor, complete tasks quickly and stay near others.
  • As murderer, kill in isolated areas and destroy cameras if possible.
  • Watch your “suspicion meter” – if it fills up, survivors will vote you out.
  • Practice on private servers to learn map layouts.
