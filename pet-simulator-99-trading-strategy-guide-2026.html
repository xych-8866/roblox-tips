The Golden Rules of PS99 Trading

  • Rule 1: Always check value lists before accepting any trade.
  • Rule 2: Never rush – the best deals take time.
  • Rule 3: Buy low, sell high – the oldest rule in trading.
  • Rule 4: Don't get attached – pets are assets, not friends.

Strategy 1: Flipping Event Pets

Event pets are the most profitable items to flip. The strategy:

  • Buy during the event: Prices drop as players sell their duplicates.
  • Hold for 2-3 months: Value increases as supply decreases.
  • Sell before the next event: Prices peak just before new events.

Example: Golden Mermaid (Summer 2026) – bought for 50B, sold for 150B after 2 months.

Strategy 2: Arbitrage (Cross-Server Trading)

Different servers have different prices. Here's how to profit:

  1. Find a pet that is undervalued in one server.
  2. Buy it there, then switch to another server and sell it for a higher price.
  3. Repeat with multiple items – the profit adds up.

Best items for arbitrage: Exclusives, Huge pets, and rare enchants.

Strategy 3: The "Trade-Up" Method

Start with low-value pets and gradually trade up:

  1. Trade 4 common pets for 1 uncommon.
  2. Trade 4 uncommon for 1 rare.
  3. Trade 4 rare for 1 ultra-rare.
  4. Trade 4 ultra-rare for 1 legendary.
  5. Trade 4 legendaries for 1 huge pet.

This method takes time but is the most consistent way to get huge pets without spending diamonds.

Strategy 5: Flipping Enchants

Enchants are always in demand. Best enchants to flip:

  • Diamonds V: Buy for 300k, sell for 400k.
  • Treasure Hunter V: Buy for 250k, sell for 350k.
  • Fortune V: Buy for 200k, sell for 300k.

How to Spot Undervalued Pets

  • Check value lists daily: Look for pets that are dropping in value – they might be a good buy if demand is rising.
  • Watch for updates: Pets that are getting buffs will rise in value.
  • Buy during events: When players are selling to get event currency, prices drop.
  • Check Discord servers: Players often post "selling cheap" – buy from them and resell at market price.

Pro Tips

  • Use multiple accounts to farm event pets – more pets = more profit.
  • Never trade huge pets for multiple exclusives – huge pets are always more valuable.
  • Join a trading group – you'll get better deals and insider info.
  • Don't be afraid to negotiate – most traders expect some back-and-forth.
