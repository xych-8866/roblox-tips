Brookhaven RP Easter Eggs 2026 – Hidden Secrets & References

Fun easter eggs and developer secrets hidden around the map. Most players never find these.

1. Developer Grave

Behind the church, there's a hidden gravestone with the name "Wolfpaq" – the original creator. Click it to get a Ghost Wolf pet.

2. UFO Sighting

At 2 AM in-game time, look up at the sky near the gas station. A UFO flies over. Click it to get a UFO Hat.

3. Secret Arcade

Inside the bowling alley, break the back wall with a pickaxe. Leads to a retro arcade with free tickets (redeem for limited items).

4. Talking Toilet

In the public restroom, use the toilet 5 times. A face appears. Click it – says "I'm watching you." Gives no reward, just funny.

5. Code on the Billboard

On the highway billboard, there's a hidden code: 0420. Enter this code at the police station's keypad. Opens a room with 5000 cash.
