Camping – Ultimate Survival Guide: Best Camping Spots, Fishing & Crafting

🎮 Game Introduction

Camping is a Roblox survival game where you set up a campsite, fish, hunt, and survive wildlife. Earn points to unlock better gear. This guide covers the best camping locations, fishing hotspots, and crafting recipes.

💡 Basic Tips for Beginners

  • Set up near water – You need water for drinking and fishing.
  • Build a fire first – Keeps you warm and scares away bears.
  • Collect wood and stones daily – Respawn every 10 minutes. Use them for tools.
  • Complete ranger missions – Each gives 200 points and a random supply crate.

🔥 Advanced Strategies & Hidden Mechanics

  • Bear baiting – Place raw fish on a rock to lure bears away from your camp.
  • Secret fishing spot – Behind the waterfall on the west side of the lake; catch rare “Golden Trout” (worth 500 points).
  • Weather exploit – During rain, fish bite 2x faster. Farm rain for quick points.
  • Camp upgrade priority – Tent → Storage → Fire → Fence. Upgraded tent gives 2x rest bonus.

📊 Best Camping Spots (Resource Density)

  • S-Tier (Best): Lakeside Meadow (water + fish + wood).
  • A-Tier (Good): Pine Forest (abundant wood, less water).
  • B-Tier (Decent): Mountain Ridge (scenic, but far from resources).

❌ Common Mistakes That End Runs

  • Leaving food out – Attracts bears and wolves. Store food in the cooler.
  • Not checking the weather – Storms damage your tent. Repair before rain.
  • Ignoring the temperature – Low temperature drains energy faster. Stay near fire.
  • Fishing without bait – Bait increases catch rate by 50%. Dig for worms in dirt patches.

🎯 Final Advice for Master Camper

  • Save 5k points for “Portable Stove” – allows cooking anywhere.
  • Join a camping club for club bonuses (+10% points).
  • Complete the “Survivalist” achievement (survive 30 days) to unlock a golden tent.
  • Play with friends – group camps have 2x safety against bears.
