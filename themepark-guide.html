🎢 Theme Park Tycoon 2 – Best Money & Design Guide (2026)

💰 Fastest Money-Making Rides

  • Roller Coaster (2x2 mini): Best early-game profit. Build short loops to keep throughput high.
  • Drop Tower: Medium price, high popularity. Place near park entrance.
  • Log Flume: Generates steady income. Add scenery to boost ticket price.
  • Ferris Wheel: Cheap to build, good for starting cash flow.

📈 Upgrade Priority

  • Upgrade Ride Capacity first (more guests per ride).
  • Then Queue Line to reduce waiting time.
  • Add Scenery Rating around rides to increase ticket price by up to 50%.
  • Finally upgrade Shop Restock Speed for food/drink stalls.

🏰 Best Park Layout Tips

  • Place food stalls near exit of thrill rides – guests are hungry after riding.
  • Keep restrooms within 20 tiles of any ride – avoids low happiness.
  • Use wide paths (2-3 tiles) for main walkways to prevent congestion.
  • Add benches near ride exits – tired guests will stay longer and spend more.

🎟️ Guest Happiness Hacks

  • Keep trash bins every 15 tiles – litter reduces happiness fast.
  • Hire enough mechanics (1 per 4 rides) to avoid breakdowns.
  • Set prices to "Normal" – higher prices reduce customers.
  • Build "Premium Pass" entrance gate – doubles ticket income after 200 guests.
