👁️ Jujutsu Kaiden – Best Cursed Techniques & Domain Expansion (2026)

Cursed Technique Tier List

  • S-Tier: Limitless + Six Eyes, Malevolent Shrine, Idle Death Gamble – best for PvP.
  • A-Tier: Cursed Speech, Blood Manipulation, Tool Manipulation – great combos.
  • B-Tier: Ratio Technique, Straw Doll – decent for beginners.

🌀 Best Innate Abilities

  • Six Eyes: Reduced cursed energy cost – best for Limitless users.
  • Black Flash: 20% damage boost after critical hits.
  • Reverse Cursed Technique: Auto-heal outside combat.

⚔️ Domain Expansion Guide

  • Reach level 50 and complete "Cursed Energy" quest.
  • Defeat 100 cursed spirits to unlock domain shard.
  • Best domain: Unlimited Void (stun + damage) – farm bosses in Shinjuku.

💰 Fast Leveling (1-60)

  • Level 1-20: Defeat low-level curses in Abandoned School.
  • Level 20-40: Farm special grades in Shibuya (party recommended).
  • Level 40-60: Boss rush mode – 2x XP on weekends.
