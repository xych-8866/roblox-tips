Grave/Digger – Complete Survival Guide: Best Tools, Secrets & Bosses

🎮 Game Introduction

Grave/Digger is a Roblox horror survival game where you dig graves, avoid monsters, and collect loot. Each night brings new threats. This guide covers the best tools, hidden items, and how to survive all bosses.

💡 Basic Tips for Beginners

  • Upgrade your shovel first – Faster digging means less time exposed to monsters.
  • Always carry a lantern – Darkness attracts enemies. Keep it fueled.
  • Listen for footsteps – Each monster has unique sound cues. Learn them.
  • Use the cemetery map – Memorize grave locations to dig efficiently.

🔥 Advanced Strategies & Hidden Mechanics

  • Bait and hide – Drop a piece of meat to lure monsters away from a grave.
  • Grave combinations – Digging three specific graves in order spawns a secret chest (coordinates: 5, 12, 24).
  • Night skip trick – If you collect 10 bones before midnight, the night ends early.
  • Multiplayer synergy – One player distracts the boss while others dig.

📊 Best Tools Tier List

  • S-Tier: Golden Shovel (2x dig speed), Lantern of Souls (infinite light).
  • A-Tier: Silver Shovel (fast), Iron Lantern (durable).
  • B-Tier: Wooden Shovel (starter), Candle (low light).

❌ Common Mistakes That Get You Killed

  • Digging without checking surroundings – Always do a 360° look before starting.
  • Ignoring the sanity meter – Low sanity makes monsters see you from farther. Use a lantern to restore it.
  • Hoarding items – Use bones to upgrade your tools immediately.
  • Running in open areas – Sprinting makes noise. Crouch when near monsters.

🎯 Final Advice for Surviving Night 10+

  • Unlock the "Gravekeeper" perk (50 bones) – reduces monster aggro range by 30%.
  • Team up with 2-3 friends; solo is much harder after night 7.
  • Save your lantern fuel for the final boss (night 10).
  • Learn each boss's pattern: Warden (slow but strong), Reaper (teleports), Hound (fast).
