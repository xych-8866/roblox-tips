Blade League – Ultimate Sword Fighting Guide: Best Blades, Combos & Ranked Tips

🎮 Game Introduction

Blade League is a Roblox sword-fighting game with fast-paced duels. Choose from dozens of blades, each with unique stats (damage, speed, range). Compete in ranked matches, earn trophies, and unlock legendary swords. This guide covers the best swords, true combos, and movement tech.

💡 Basic Tips for Beginners

  • Master the basic M1 combo – Three light attacks then a heavy. Practice the timing.
  • Use block (F) to parry – Block right before an enemy swing to stagger them for 1 second.
  • Dash is your best defense – Double tap any direction. Use it to dodge ultimates.
  • Don't spam attacks – Every swing leaves you vulnerable for 0.5 seconds.

🔥 Advanced Strategies & Hidden Mechanics

  • Jump reset – After a light attack, jump to cancel recovery and continue the combo.
  • Wall splat combo – Knock opponent into a wall, then use heavy attack for double damage.
  • Feinting – Start a heavy attack, then dash cancel. Bait enemy parry.
  • Ultimate timing – Use your ultimate when enemy is out of stamina (yellow bar empty).

📊 Best Swords Tier List (Ranked)

  • S-Tier (Meta): Shadow Blade (invisibility on dash), Frostfang (slows enemies), Dragon Slayer (high damage).
  • A-Tier (Strong): Stormbreaker (chain lightning), Rose Thorn (life steal), Void Edge (teleport on heavy).
  • B-Tier (Situational): Steel Cleaver (good stats but boring), Wind Blade (fast but low damage).
  • C-Tier (Weak): Wooden Sword (starter only), Rusty Blade (worse stats).

❌ Common Mistakes That Lose Fights

  • Overcommitting to combos – If the enemy blocks, stop attacking. You'll get parried.
  • Ignoring stamina bar – When stamina is red, you can't dodge or block. Run away.
  • Using ultimate at low health – Enemies will block or dodge. Use at 50% HP.
  • Not switching swords – Each sword has a different playstyle. Pick one that fits your rank meta.

🎯 Final Advice for Climbing Ranks

  • Stick to one S-tier sword for 50 matches to master its combos.
  • Learn to parry – it's the difference between bronze and diamond.
  • Watch top players' replays to learn positioning.
  • Don't rage queue – after two losses, practice in training mode.
