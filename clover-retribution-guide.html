Clover Retribution – Ultimate Magic Guide: Best Grimoires, PvP Combos & Leveling

🎮 Game Introduction

Clover Retribution is a Roblox RPG inspired by Black Clover. Choose a grimoire, learn spells, and battle other mages. This guide covers the best magic types, fastest leveling routes, and PvP tactics.

💡 Basic Tips for Beginners

  • Start with Fire or Wind magic – Balanced damage and mana cost. Good for learning.
  • Complete the first 5 quests – Unlocks your second spell slot.
  • Farm mobs in the Forest – Best XP for levels 1-20.
  • Join a squad – Squad bonuses include +10% XP and +5% mana regen.

🔥 Advanced Strategies & Hidden Mechanics

  • Spell chaining – Use a knockback spell, then a fast projectile during their recovery.
  • Hidden dungeon – Behind the waterfall in the cave; contains a rare grimoire drop (5%).
  • Mana shield exploit – Activate mana shield just before taking damage; it absorbs 80% instead of 50% for 0.5 seconds.
  • Elemental advantage – Fire > Grass > Water > Fire. Use it in PvP for +25% damage.

📊 Best Grimoires Tier List (PvP)

  • S-Tier (Meta): Dark Magic (life steal), Light Magic (fast projectiles), Demon Magic (high burst).
  • A-Tier (Strong): Fire (burn DoT), Ice (slow), Lightning (stun chance).
  • B-Tier (Decent): Wind (fast but weak), Earth (tanky but slow).

❌ Common Mistakes That Waste Time

  • Ignoring mana regen gear – Mana potions are expensive. Wear the “Mage Robe” for +50% regen.
  • Skipping daily arena – Daily PvP matches give 500 XP and a random spell scroll.
  • Fighting higher-level players – Level difference over 20 reduces your damage by 40%.
  • Not using dodges – Double tap to dodge. Essential against Dark Magic users.

🎯 Final Advice for Becoming Archmage

  • Reach level 100 to unlock “Grimoire Awakening” (evolves your spells).
  • Save 100k gold for “Mana Core” – increases mana pool by 50%.
  • Watch top players' replays to learn advanced combos (e.g., Dark + Light).
  • Never fight a Demon Magic user in close range – they have a 30% damage boost.
