🐷 Piggy – Complete Survival Guide & Secret Endings (2026)

🗺️ Chapter 1 – Escape Room Basics

  • Search everything: Drawers, beds, and desks often contain keys or hints.
  • Avoid Piggy footsteps: Listen for her grunt – hide in closets or under beds immediately.
  • Team play: In multiplayer, split tasks: one does puzzles, others distract Piggy.

🔑 Chapter 2 – Key Item Locations

  • Red key: Usually in the kitchen drawer (Chapter 1).
  • Blue key: Behind the painting in the hallway (Chapter 2).
  • Green key: Inside the plant pot near the staircase (Chapter 3).
  • Hammer: In the garage toolbox – use to break boards.

🤖 Chapter 3 – Robot & Traps

  • Robot decoy: Activate robot in lab to lure Piggy away for 30 seconds.
  • Bear traps: Place near doors – freezes Piggy for 5 seconds.
  • Hidden passage: In Chapter 4, crawl through the air vent behind the bookshelf.

🏆 Chapter 4 – Secret Endings

  • Good ending: Give Piggy the flower (found in garden) before escaping – extra cutscene.
  • True ending (all chapters): Collect 10 hidden piggy coins (one per chapter). After final door, insert coins into statue.
  • Speedrun ending: Finish any chapter under 4 minutes – unlocks golden hammer skin.

⏱️ Speedrun Tricks

  • Memorize item spawns – they are fixed per chapter.
  • Ignore side rooms unless required.
  • Use the “reset” button to respawn near Piggy – risky but sometimes saves backtracking.
