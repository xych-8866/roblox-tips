Brookhaven RP Beginner Tips 2026 – 10 Things Every New Player Should Know

New to Brookhaven RP? This guide covers the 10 most important tips to get started quickly, earn cash, and enjoy the game.

1. Start with Pizza Delivery

Pizza delivery is the best beginner job. It pays $50 per delivery and takes 45 seconds. In one hour, you can make $4,000+. This is the fastest way to get your first car and house.

2. Collect Hidden Cash Stashes Daily

Every day, collect these free cash stashes:

  • Camping Tent: $200
  • Beach Cave: $150 (respawns every 4 hours)
  • Police Station Basement: $100
  • Hospital Roof: $75

Total: $500+ in under 5 minutes.

3. Buy a Starter House

Your first house costs $5,000. Save up from pizza deliveries and buy it as soon as possible. It gives you a permanent spawn point and storage for your items.

4. Get a Car Early

Buy the cheapest car ($2,000) after you get your house. It makes traveling around the map much faster, which increases your job efficiency.

5. Explore the Map

Brookhaven has many hidden areas. Explore the map to find secret jobs, hidden cash, and exclusive items.

6. Use the Bank

You can store cash in the bank (near the town center). This protects your money from being lost if you die.

7. Join Jobs for Extra Income

After pizza delivery, try these jobs for variety:

  • Police Officer: Arrest criminals for $100 per arrest.
  • Firefighter: Put out fires for $75 per fire.
  • Cashier: $40 per checkout (easy AFK).

8. Buy a Hot Dog Stand (AFK Money)

Buy the hot dog stand for $200. Place it in a busy area (near nursery). It gives $15 per sale automatically. AFK overnight for 5,000-10,000 cash.

9. Customize Your Character

Visit the salon to change your character's appearance. Some clothes and accessories are free – start there before spending cash.

10. Have Fun and Make Friends

Brookhaven is a social game. Join groups, play minigames, and make friends. Many players trade items and help each other.

Quick Start Checklist

  • Collect hidden cash stashes ✅
  • Buy a cheap car ✅
  • Buy a starter house ✅
  • Start pizza delivery ✅
  • Buy a hot dog stand ✅
