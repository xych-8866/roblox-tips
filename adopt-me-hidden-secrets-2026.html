Adopt Me Hidden Secrets 2026 – Easter Eggs & Secret Locations

Hidden rooms, secret NPCs, and rare items that most players miss.

1. Hidden Party Room

Behind the grocery store, click the trash can 5 times. A secret door opens to a party room with free balloons and cake (50 bucks).

2. The Ghost of Adopt Me

At 3 AM in-game time, go to the cemetery. A ghost NPC appears. Give him a candy and he gives you a Ghost Plushie.

3. Underground Tunnel

In the school basement, break the back wall with a pickaxe. Leads to a tunnel with chests containing 100-500 bucks.

4. Talking Tree

Behind the playground, whisper "I love pets" to the big tree. It drops 3 rare fruit (sell for 50 bucks each).

5. Secret Pet Rock

Click the rock near the bridge 50 times. It turns into a Pet Rock (common pet, but rare collector item).
