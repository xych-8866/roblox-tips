Pet Simulator 99 Secret Eggs Locations 2026

There are hidden eggs scattered across PS99 worlds. These eggs contain rare and huge pets. This guide shows exactly where to find them.

Secret Egg #1: Tech Egg

Location: Tech World – behind the giant computer screen in the main hub. Jump onto the console and slide down the back.

Contains: Cyber pets, chance of Huge Tech Cat.

Secret Egg #2: Jungle Egg

Location: Jungle zone – hidden inside the waterfall on the left side. Use a flying pet to reach the ledge.

Contains: Exotic jungle pets, leaf dragons.

Secret Egg #3: Crystal Egg

Location: Fantasy World – inside the crystal cavern. Break the dark purple crystal with 10 hits.

Contains: Gem pets, shiny variants.

Secret Egg #4: Void Egg

Location: Void zone – after the final area, go to the floating island. Use a magnet to pull the egg from the void rift.

Contains: Dark matter pets, Huge Void Bat.

How to Open Secret Eggs

Most secret eggs require 1,000 coins or 250 diamonds per hatch. Some have a 1-hour cooldown. Use Lucky Potions before hatching.

Pro Tips

  • Collect all 4 secret eggs daily – they reset every 24 hours.
  • Equip Egg Luck enchant for better huge chances.
  • Hatch during server events (2x luck on weekends).
