🐝 Bee Swarm Simulator – Best Hive Setup & Honey Farming Guide (2026)

🍯 Best Ways to Farm Honey Fast

  • Pumpkin Patch – Use gifted bees with high pollen collection. ~500k honey/hour.
  • Coconut Field – Requires level 8+ bees. Use Honey Mask for extra conversion.
  • Strawberry Field – Best for early game. Use Baby Love buff for 2x pollen.
  • King Beetle Amulet – Increases pollen from all fields by 25%.

🐝 Best Hive Composition (25 Bees)

  • 2x Carpenter Bee – Essential for pollen collection.
  • 2x Music Bee – Grants melody buff for extra conversion.
  • 2x Shy Bee – Boosts pollen from ability tokens.
  • 1x Tabby Bee – Scales with tabby love, must-have.
  • 1x Photon Bee – Instant conversion at hive.
  • 1x Vicious Bee – Good for attack and grinding mobs.
  • Fill the rest with legendary and event bees.

🎒 Gear Upgrade Priority

  • Save honey for Honey Mask (25M honey) – best early investment.
  • Next: Porcelain Dipper (50M honey) – improves collection.
  • Finally: Crimson Guard & Cobalt Guard – balance attack and collection.

✨ Field Boosters & Items

  • Use Field Dice to reroll field bonuses.
  • Activate Wealth Clock every 4 hours for free honey.
  • Use Enzymes and Glitter during boost sessions.
