Twisted – Ultimate Survival Guide: Best Characters, Abilities & Map Strategies

🎮 Game Introduction

Twisted is a Roblox survival game where you must outlast a killer in a procedurally generated map. Repair generators, avoid traps, and escape. This guide covers the best characters, generator locations, and killer counterplay.

💡 Basic Tips for Beginners

  • Start with the Scout – Fast movement speed, good for learning map layouts.
  • Always repair generators as a team – Solo repairs take 30 seconds; team repairs take 15.
  • Use your flashlight sparingly – Light reveals your location to the killer. Turn it off when hiding.
  • Complete the tutorial – Gives 500 coins to buy your first character upgrade.

🔥 Advanced Strategies & Hidden Mechanics

  • Killer tracking – The killer’s heartbeat gets louder as they approach. Hide in lockers when heartbeat is max.
  • Perk synergy – Combine “Quick Fix” (faster repairs) with “Lightweight” (quieter footsteps) for stealth repairs.
  • Secret escape route – On the “Abandoned Factory” map, break the left wall in the boiler room to find a hidden door.
  • Generator order exploit – Completing generators in a specific order (1 → 3 → 2) makes the killer’s patrol path predictable.

📊 Best Characters Tier List (Survival)

  • S-Tier (Best): Medic (self-heal), Engineer (fast repairs), Ninja (silent footsteps).
  • A-Tier (Strong): Scout (speed), Tank (extra health), Hacker (reveal killer on map).
  • B-Tier (Decent): Default (balanced), Clown (distract killer with balloons).

❌ Common Mistakes That Get You Killed

  • Repairing without checking surroundings – Always look for the killer’s red glow before starting a generator.
  • Using the same hiding spot twice – The killer learns your patterns. Move between lockers.
  • Ignoring the exit gate – Once 4 generators are done, the exit gate appears. Leave immediately; don't wait.
  • Not using items – Medkits and flashbangs are cheap (100 coins). Buy 3 before each match.

🎯 Final Advice for Win Streaks

  • Save 10k coins for “Master Survivor” perk – grants 20% faster generator repair permanently.
  • Play with friends – coordinated teams win 80% more matches.
  • Learn each killer’s ability (e.g., “Wraith” can turn invisible for 5 seconds).
  • Watch YouTube guides for map-specific hiding spots.
