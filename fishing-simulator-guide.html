Fishing Simulator – Ultimate Guide: Best Rods, Rare Fish Locations & Fast Coins

🎮 Game Introduction

Fishing Simulator is a Roblox relaxing fishing game. Catch fish, sell them for coins, upgrade your rod, and unlock new islands. This guide covers the best rods, rare fish spawns, and coin farming strategies.

💡 Basic Tips for Beginners

  • Start with the Wooden Rod – Cheap and effective for early fish.
  • Fish near bubbles – Bubble spots give 2x bite rate.
  • Sell fish immediately – Holding them doesn't increase value. Upgrade faster.
  • Complete daily challenges – Each gives 500 coins and bait.

🔥 Advanced Strategies & Hidden Mechanics

  • Rare fish respawn timer – Legendary fish (e.g., “Golden Bass”) spawn every 2 hours in specific locations.
  • Bait stacking – Use “Super Bait” + “Lucky Charm” for 4x rare fish chance.
  • Secret fishing cave – Behind the waterfall on Island 3; contains a 1% chance for “Mythic Angler” rod.
  • Auto-fish exploit – Upgrade to “Auto Fisher” (cost 50k coins) to AFK farm overnight.

📊 Best Rods Tier List (Catch Speed & Rare Chance)

  • S-Tier (Best): Mythic Rod (2x rare fish), Golden Rod (3x coin multiplier), Void Rod (auto-reel).
  • A-Tier (Strong): Diamond Rod (durable), Carbon Rod (lightweight).
  • B-Tier (Decent): Iron Rod (starter), Wooden Rod (slow).

❌ Common Mistakes That Waste Coins

  • Buying the most expensive rod first – Upgrade gradually; each tier gives better value per coin.
  • Ignoring bait upgrades – Better bait increases catch speed. Use “Worm” over “Bread”.
  • Not using the aquarium – Display rare fish in aquarium for passive income (5% of value per hour).
  • Skipping the “Fishing Guild” – Joining costs 2k coins but gives +10% coin boost permanently.

🎯 Final Advice for Endgame

  • Save 500k coins for the “Mythic Rod” – fastest catch speed and 2x rare chance.
  • Join a fishing crew for crew bonuses (+10% coins).
  • Farm the “Endless Ocean” dungeon (unlocks at level 50) for unlimited rare fish.
  • Use the “Fish Radar” item to find legendary spawns instantly.
