Blox Fruits Sea Events Guide 2026 – Ship Raid, Sea Beast & Rogue

Sea events are the best way to get fragments, gems, and rare items. This guide covers spawn times, strategies, and rewards for every sea event.

1. Ship Raid (Most Common)

Spawn: Every 30 minutes in Second and Third Sea. Look for the ship icon on the map.

Rewards: 50-150 fragments, 10-50 gems, random fruit (common to legendary).

Strategy: Use Magma or Buddha fruit. Focus on the captain first – he drops the best loot. Team up with crew members for faster completion.

2. Sea Beast (Random Spawn)

Spawn: Random, but more frequent at night. Requires a boat to engage.

Rewards: 30-80 fragments, 5-20 gems, Sea Beast drops (cores, scales).

Strategy: Use Magma fruit (2x damage on sea events). Stay at a distance and spam ranged attacks. Watch for its dive attack – dodge by moving sideways.

3. Rogue Sea Beast (Best Rewards)

Spawn: Only at night in Third Sea. Rare spawn, but guaranteed rewards.

Rewards: 250-300 fragments, 100-200 gems, guaranteed legendary fruit, plus Rogue Sea Beast Crown (accessory).

Strategy: Requires a large crew (5+ players). Use awakened Magma or Dragon fruit. Focus on its weak point (the glowing spot on its back).

Rewards Comparison

  • Ship Raid: Best for fragments/hour.
  • Sea Beast: Good for solo farming.
  • Rogue Sea Beast: Best for rare drops and legendary fruits.

Pro Tips for Sea Events

  • Always carry a boat in your inventory – you never know when a Sea Beast will spawn.
  • Join a crew with active members – sea events are easier with a team.
  • Use the "Sea Event" channel in Discord to find active spawns.
  • Server hop after completing a Ship Raid to respawn it faster.
