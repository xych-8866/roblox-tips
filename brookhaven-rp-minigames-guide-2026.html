Brookhaven RP Minigames Guide 2026 – Best Games & Rewards

Brookhaven RP isn't just about jobs and roleplay – it has hidden minigames that give cash, exclusive items, and fun experiences. This guide covers all minigames, rewards, and strategies.

1. Bowling Alley – The Classic

Location: Bowling alley (near the town center).

How to play: Pick up a ball, aim, and roll it down the lane.

Rewards: 10-50 cash per game, plus a chance for a Bowling Trophy (limited item).

Tips: Aim for the center pin – perfect strikes give bonus rewards.

2. Arcade Machines – Hidden Games

Location: Inside the bowling alley (back corner).

How to play: Click on any arcade machine – each has a different game.

Rewards: Arcade tickets (redeem for limited items) and cash.

Best game: "Pixel Racer" – easiest to score high points.

3. Park Basketball Court

Location: The park (near the playground).

How to play: Click the ball, aim, and shoot.

Rewards: 5 cash per basket, plus Basketball Jersey (rare item) after 100 baskets.

Tips: Stand at the free throw line – it's the most consistent angle.

4. Beach Volleyball

Location: The beach (near the lifeguard tower).

How to play: Requires 2+ players. Hit the ball back and forth – last team to miss loses.

Rewards: 20 cash per win, plus Beach Volleyball Trophy (seasonal item).

Tips: Play during peak hours – more players = more matches.

5. Skate Park – Halfpipe Trick

Location: Skate park (near the gas station).

How to play: Click the ramp, then press Space when you reach the top to perform a trick.

Rewards: 10-50 cash per trick, plus Skateboard (rare item) after 100 tricks.

Tips: Time your Space press at the very peak of the ramp for the best score.

6. Museum Quiz – Trivia Minigame

Location: The museum (inside the main hall).

How to play: Click the quiz board near the entrance. Answer 10 questions about Brookhaven history.

Rewards: 100 cash for a perfect score, plus "Historian" title.

Tips: Answers are displayed on plaques around the museum – explore first.
