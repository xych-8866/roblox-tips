How Swords Work in Blox Fruits

Swords are a secondary weapon class that complements your fruit and fighting style. Each sword has unique moves (Z, X, and sometimes C). They are essential for PvP combos and provide a damage alternative when your fruit is on cooldown.

Best Swords by Sea

  • First Sea: Saber V2 – best free option, easy to obtain.
  • Second Sea: Shark Saw – best for grinding, Rengoku – best for PvP.
  • Third Sea: Dark Dagger (PvP), Triple Katana (DPS), Dragon Trident (grinding).

Sword Combos (PvP)

  • Dark Dagger + Godhuman: C (DD) → Z (DD) → Godhuman C → X (DD) → Finisher.
  • Koko + Electric Claw: Z (Koko) → Electric Claw C → X (Koko) → Finisher.
  • Triple Katana + Dragon Fruit: Use sword to extend combos while fruit is on cooldown.

How to Get Swords Fast

  • Focus on Saber V2 first – it's the easiest A‑Tier sword.
  • Join a crew – raid bosses spawn faster with more players.
  • Save 100 Bones for Dark Dagger – it's worth the grind.
