Adopt Me Ultimate Guide: From Beginner to Pro

How to Hatch Eggs Faster

Getting eggs in Adopt Me is just the beginning. The real challenge is hatching them efficiently. Here are proven tips to speed up your hatching process:

  • Use a Baby or Family: Taking care of a baby or family member gives you 2x task speed. This directly affects how quickly your eggs hatch.
  • Stack Your Tasks: Always keep your egg task active while doing other chores. Never let your pet idle.
  • Best Spots: Stay near the school, hospital, and playground. These areas have the fastest task rotations.

Best Pets for Trading in 2026

Not all pets are worth the same. Here's what you should aim for:

  • High-Tier Legendary: Shadow Dragon, Giraffe, Bat Dragon. These hold value and are great for big trades.
  • Mid-Tier Legendary: Frost Dragon, Owl, Parrot. These are stable and easy to flip.
  • Rising Stars: Space Whale, Pumpkin Friend. These are new and gaining demand quickly.

Pro Tip: Never trade your pet right after hatching. Wait a few days for values to settle. Use online value lists before confirming any trade.

Secret Locations for Money (Buried Treasure, etc.)

Money (bucks) is essential for buying eggs and accessories. Here's where to find hidden stashes:

  • Camping Tent: Near the playground, behind the large tree. Tap the glowing spot daily for free bucks.
  • Beach Cave: At the far end of the beach, inside the small cave. A treasure chest spawns every 4 hours.
  • Treehouse Behind Salon: On the left side of the map. Climb up and check the corner.
  • Rocket Ship: Inside the rocket near the playground. The hidden switch gives you 20 bucks each time.

Also, always collect your daily login rewards and complete the school tasks — they give you 10–30 bucks per task.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

Many beginners lose time, pets, or bucks because of these errors:

  • Overpaying in Trades: Always check current values. Don't trust random players saying "big win."
  • Hatching Without Tasks Active: You waste time if your task queue is empty. Always keep a task running.
  • Ignoring Family: Solo play is slower. Adding a baby or family member doubles your task speed.
  • Skipping Daily Login: You lose free eggs and bucks. Log in every day, even for one minute.
  • Buying Random Eggs: Focus on one egg type at a time. Don't spread your bucks too thin.

Final Tips for Beginners

  • Join Discord trading servers to learn real-time values.
  • Watch YouTube traders before making your first big trade.
  • Be patient. A pet that seems low-value today might skyrocket next month.
  • Don't trade while tired or rushed. Most bad trades happen in a hurry.
