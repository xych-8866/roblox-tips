How Fusion Works

Fusion allows you to combine multiple pets of the same rarity into a single pet of a higher rarity. The process costs gold and time. Higher rarity fusions take longer but yield better results.

Fusion Requirements

  • Same Rarity: You need 3-5 pets of the same rarity to fuse.
  • Gold Cost: Fusion costs gold – the higher the rarity, the more it costs.
  • Time: Fusion takes time (from 1 minute for common to 24 hours for mythical).
  • Luck: Some fusions have a chance to produce a "shiny" or "rainbow" variant.

Fusion Tier List (Best Outputs)

  • S-Tier: Huge Fusion (5 exclusives) – rare but game-changing.
  • A-Tier: Legendary → Mythical fusion – gives access to top-tier pets.
  • B-Tier: Ultra-Rare → Legendary – good for farming legendaries.
  • C-Tier: Rare → Ultra-Rare – not worth the gold unless you have excess.

Tips to Maximize Fusion

  • Save your exclusives – don't fuse them unless you have 5 of the same type.
  • Use Luck Potions before starting a high-level fusion to increase the chance of a shiny or rainbow outcome.
  • Fuse during events – some events offer bonus fusion speed or reduced costs.
  • Check the value – before fusing, check if the output pet is worth more than the input pets combined.
  • Don't fuse commons – it's not worth the gold. Save them for trading or selling.

Common Fusion Mistakes

  • Fusing too early – wait until you have a good collection of pets before starting high-level fusions.
  • Ignoring gold costs – high-level fusions can cost millions of gold. Plan accordingly.
  • Fusing exclusives – only do this if you have duplicates. Exclusives are valuable for trading.

Fusion Calendar (Best Times to Fuse)

  • Weekends: 2x fusion speed events often occur on weekends.
  • Holidays: Special fusion events with reduced costs.
  • Server Events: Sometimes fusion costs are halved during server-wide events.
