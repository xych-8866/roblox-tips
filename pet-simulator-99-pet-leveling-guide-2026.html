Pet Simulator 99 Pet Leveling Guide 2026 – Fast XP & Rainbow

Leveling pets increases their damage and unlocks zones. This guide shows the fastest way to get pets to level 100.

Best Farming Zones by Level

  • Level 1-20: Grassy Plains (fast breakables).
  • Level 20-40: Desert Zone – use auto-clicker.
  • Level 40-60: Ice Zone – best XP/hour.
  • Level 60-80: Tech World – spawns elite breakables.
  • Level 80-100: Void World – highest XP, but requires strong pets.

XP Boost Stacking

  • Use XP Potion (2x) + Pet XP Boost (1.5x) = 3x total.
  • Equip "Team Work" enchant on one pet.
  • Party up with friends – nearby players give XP bonus.
  • Farm during weekend events (2x XP).

Rainbow Evolution

At level 100, pets can be evolved to Rainbow. Requires 1000 diamonds + 10 rainbows. Rainbow pets deal 2x damage.

AFK Setup

Use an auto-clicker in Void World, stand near breakable spawn points. Check every hour to claim coins.
