PS99 has many secret locations with free rewards. This guide reveals hidden chests, secret eggs, and rare spawn points that most players miss.

Secret Chest Locations

1. Tech World – Diamond Chest

Location: Behind the giant computer (far right side of Tech World).

Rewards: 250 diamonds + 5,000 coins.

Respawn: Every 4 hours.

Tip: Click the computer first to activate a light that shows the chest.

2. Fantasy World – Crystal Chest

Location: Behind the crystal castle (hidden by a large purple crystal).

Rewards: 250 diamonds + random enchanted item.

Respawn: Every 6 hours.

Tip: Break the crystal to reveal the chest.

3. Void World – Floating Island Chest

Location: On the floating island above Void World (jump from the tallest platform).

Rewards: 500 diamonds + 10,000 coins.

Respawn: Every 6 hours.

Tip: Use a fast pet to reach the island – it's a long jump.

4. Jungle Zone – Waterfall Chest

Location: Inside the waterfall in Jungle Zone.

Rewards: 100 diamonds + 2,000 coins.

Respawn: Every 3 hours.

5. Ice Zone – Frozen Chest

Location: Behind the ice cave (hidden by a frozen wall).

Rewards: 200 diamonds + 5,000 coins.

Respawn: Every 4 hours.

Tip: Break the ice wall with a fire enchanted pet.

Secret Eggs

6. Tech Egg

Location: Inside the giant computer (click the screen 10 times).

Rewards: Tech-exclusive pet (rare).

Tip: Can only be hatched once per account.

7. Jungle Egg

Location: Inside the temple, behind the stone statue.

Rewards: Jungle-exclusive pet.

Tip: Complete the temple puzzle to unlock it.

8. Crystal Egg

Location: Fantasy World – break the dark purple crystal.

Rewards: Crystal-exclusive pet.

Tip: Use a strong pet to break the crystal quickly.

Rare Spawn Locations

9. Diamond V Enchant (Spawn)

Location: Tech World – behind the computer (rare spawn).

Rewards: Diamonds V enchant book.

Tip: Check every 30 minutes – it's a rare spawn.

10. Rare Fish Spawn (Fishing)

Location: Fishing area during weekend events.

Rewards: Rare fish (sell for 50-200 diamonds).

Tip: Use Golden Fishing Rod for better rates.
