Deadline – Ultimate FPS Guide: Best Weapons, Maps & Ranked Strategies

🎮 Game Introduction

Deadline is a Roblox first-person shooter with realistic mechanics. Play team deathmatch, capture the flag, and ranked modes. This guide covers the best weapons, map callouts, and aim training.

💡 Basic Tips for Beginners

  • Start with the M4A1 – Low recoil, good for learning recoil control.
  • Use cover – Peek from corners, don’t stand in the open.
  • Check your minimap – Red dots indicate gunfire. Move toward them to find enemies.
  • Complete daily challenges – Earn 500 XP and a weapon crate.

🔥 Advanced Strategies & Hidden Mechanics

  • Headshot multiplier – Headshots deal 2x damage. Practice aiming at head level.
  • Slide canceling – Slide, then jump to cancel the slide animation and move faster.
  • Wallbang spots – Certain walls (e.g., on “Warehouse” map) are penetrable. Shoot through them.
  • Gun leveling – Each weapon has levels. Unlock attachments (scopes, grips) by getting 50 kills.

📊 Best Weapons Tier List (Ranked)

  • S-Tier (Meta): AK-47 (high damage), MP5 (fast fire rate), AWP (one-shot kill).
  • A-Tier (Strong): SCAR-H (accurate), UMP (stable), Deagle (secondary).
  • B-Tier (Decent): M4A1 (beginner), Shotgun (close range).

❌ Common Mistakes That Get You Killed

  • Reloading in the open – Always find cover before reloading.
  • Ignoring the bomb timer – In demolition mode, the bomb takes 45 seconds to explode. Don’t camp too far.
  • Using the same angle twice – Enemies will pre-aim your position. Move after each kill.
  • Not buying armor – Armor reduces damage by 30%. Always buy it in the first round.

🎯 Final Advice for Reaching Rank 50

  • Master one S-tier weapon and one A-tier backup.
  • Play with a team – communication wins 80% of matches.
  • Watch your killcam to learn enemy positions.
  • Take breaks after two losses to avoid tilting.
