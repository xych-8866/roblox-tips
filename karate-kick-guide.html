🥋 Karate Kick – Best Belts & Fighting Guide (2026)

🥋 Belt Progression

  • White Belt: Starter – learn basic punches.
  • Yellow Belt: Unlock roundhouse kick – 500 XP.
  • Orange Belt: Unlock spin kick – 1,500 XP.
  • Green Belt: Unlock combo punch – 3,000 XP.
  • Blue Belt: Unlock flying kick – 5,000 XP.
  • Brown Belt: Unlock backfist – 8,000 XP.
  • Black Belt: Unlock all moves – 12,000 XP.

💪 Best Fighting Combos

  • Basic Combo: Jab → Cross → Roundhouse Kick.
  • Advanced Combo: Spin Kick → Flying Kick → Backfist.
  • Defensive: Parry → Counter Punch → Leg Sweep.

🎯 Fast XP Farming

  • Complete daily sparring challenges – 500 XP each.
