🛏️ BedWars – Best Kits & Rush Strategy (2026)

🏆 Best Kits for Beginners

  • Pyro: Fire damage over time – great for rushing beds.
  • Bounty Hunter: Earn extra iron from kills – snowball early.
  • Builder: Instant wood blocks – best for defense.

⚡ Rush Strategy (Solo/Duo)

  • Buy wool + pickaxe first – ignore armor.
  • Bridge to neighbor before they build defense (first 45 seconds).
  • Break bed → wipe team → take their generator.

🛡️ Base Defense Tips

  • Layer bed with wood (3 layers) + stone (1 layer) by 2-minute mark.
  • Place blast-proof glass around bed – resists fireballs.
  • Leave one observer on base at all times in 4v4.

💰 Shop Priority

  • Iron: Pickaxe > Wooden Sword > Armor.
  • Emerald: Obsidian (bed defense) > Diamond Armor.
  • Diamond: Damage upgrade first → Speed upgrade.
