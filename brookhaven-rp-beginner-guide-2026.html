Getting Started

Choose a character. You start with $500. Use it to buy a starter house ($5k – save up) or rent an apartment ($500/week).

Best Jobs for Beginners

  • Pizza Delivery – $50 per delivery (best money/hour).
  • Police Officer – $100 per arrest (requires training).
  • Firefighter – $75 per fire.
  • Cashier – $40 per checkout (easy AFK).

How to Buy a House

Save $5k from pizza deliveries (100 deliveries = 2 hours). Go to the Real Estate building. Starter house is $5k. Upgrade to Beach House ($45k) later.

Daily Money Spots

  • Camping tent: $200 daily
  • Beach cave: $150 every 4 hours
  • Police station basement: $100 daily
  • Hospital roof: $75 daily
