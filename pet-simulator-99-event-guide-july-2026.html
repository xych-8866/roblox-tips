How to Earn Summer Sparks

  • Minigame 1: Firework Show – Click fireworks for 5-10 sparks each (best AFK).
  • Minigame 2: Summer Race – Race against other players for 20-50 sparks per win.
  • Minigame 3: Beach Cleanup – Collect trash for 10 sparks per round.
  • Daily Quests: 50-100 sparks per day (complete all 3).
  • Weekend Bonus: Double sparks from all activities on Saturdays and Sundays.

Best Strategy for Sparks

  1. AFK: Run Firework Show overnight – it gives the most sparks per hour (100-150/hour).
  2. Active: Do Summer Race during peak hours – more players = more competition = higher rewards.
  3. Daily: Complete all 3 daily quests first (5 minutes, 100 sparks).
  4. Weekend: Use weekends to farm twice as fast – save your playtime for Saturday/Sunday.

Pro Tips

  • Save sparks for Golden Phoenix first – it's the most valuable pet from the event.
  • Don't spend sparks on cosmetics until you have the Golden Phoenix.
  • Use Lucky Potions when opening event eggs – it increases legendary chance.
  • If you have multiple accounts, farm sparks on all of them – trade pets to your main account.
