Brookhaven RP: Secret Money Locations & Daily Cash Guide

💰 Daily Money Tree

Behind the large tree near the playground — click the glowing money bag every 24 hours to receive 500–2,000 free bucks.

🏦 Bank Vault Code

Enter the bank in the city centre. Use code 0824 on the keypad inside the vault room. Gives 2,500 bucks once per day.

🚤 Dock Hidden Treasure

At the west end of the docks, dive into the water near the red buoy. A treasure chest spawns every 2 hours containing 200–800 bucks.

🏥 Hospital Trash Can

Inside the hospital lobby, check the trash can. Spawns 100–300 bucks every 30 minutes.

🎭 Theater Backstage Drawer

Go backstage in the theater. The locked drawer code is 1127. One-time reward: 1,500 bucks.

🏠 Your House Mailbox

Check your house mailbox daily for 50–200 bucks. Also interact with furniture (lights, TV) — random small cash rewards.

🚓 Police Station Evidence Room

Complete 5 police tasks to get the keycard. Enter the evidence room, open the briefcase for 3,000 bucks weekly.

🕹️ Arcade Crash Game

Play the "Crash" game in the arcade. Bet 100 bucks → chance to win 1,000–5,000 bucks (~15% win rate).

🎣 Hidden Fishing Spot

Behind the beach shack, find the fishing rod. Catch rare fish (golden bass) and sell for 500–1,500 bucks. Best during in-game evening.
