Anime Adventures – Ultimate Tower Defense Guide: Best Units, Evolution & Infinite Mode

🎮 Game Introduction

Anime Adventures is a Roblox tower defense game featuring characters from popular anime. Summon units, upgrade them, and survive waves of enemies. This guide covers the best units, evolution priority, and infinite mode strategies.

💡 Basic Tips for Beginners

  • Summon from the basic banner first – Costs 50 gems, gives good starter units (Naruto, Luffy).
  • Place units at chokepoints – Corners and intersections maximize coverage.
  • Upgrade your summoning luck – Spend first 1k gems on luck upgrade (unlock at level 10).
  • Complete daily missions – Each gives 200 gems and XP boost.

🔥 Advanced Strategies & Hidden Mechanics

  • Unit synergy – Pair "Goku" (AoE) with "Vegeta" (single target) to cover all enemy types.
  • Evolution secret – Some units (e.g., "Ichigo") evolve at level 30 after collecting 5 evolution shards from bosses.
  • Infinite mode trick – Sell and replace units every 10 waves to reset their cooldowns.
  • Hidden gold farming – In Story Mode chapter 3, break all barrels for 500 gems each run.

📊 Best Units Tier List (Endgame)

  • S-Tier (Must-have): Goku (Ultra Instinct), Luffy (Gear 5), Ichigo (True Bankai).
  • A-Tier (Strong): Naruto (Six Paths), Gojo (Domain Expansion), Zoro (Ashura).
  • B-Tier (Decent): Deku (100%), Tanjiro (Sun Breathing).

❌ Common Mistakes That Waste Gems

  • Summoning on the wrong banner – Legendary banner costs 500 gems but has low drop rates. Stick with rare banner until you have 10 A-tier units.
  • Ignoring tower placement – Clustering units in one spot leaves other lanes open.
  • Not using buff items – Damage potions (+20% for 3 waves) cost only 50 gems each. Use them on boss waves.
  • Selling evolved units – Evolved units give only half the gems. Keep them.

🎯 Final Advice for Infinite Mode Wave 100+

  • Unlock "Double Damage" gamepass for 2x damage.
  • Have at least 3 S-tier units at max evolution.
  • Use "Time Stop" item on wave 90, 95, 99 bosses.
  • Join the official Discord for trade offers (rare units).
