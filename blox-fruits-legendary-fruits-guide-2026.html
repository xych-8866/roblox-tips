What Makes a Legendary Fruit?

Legendary fruits are the highest rarity tier. They have powerful abilities, high damage, and are extremely valuable for trading. Some legendary fruits are rarer than others.

How to Get Legendary Fruits

  • Hatching – Random chance from any fruit (very low).
  • Boss Drops – Dough King, Rip_Indra, and Rogue Sea Beast have higher chances.
  • Trading – The most reliable method. Trade multiple rare fruits for one legendary.
  • Chests – Rarely found in Third Sea chests.
  • Events – Limited-time events sometimes guarantee legendary drops.

Which Legendary to Keep vs Trade

  • Keep: Kitsune, Dragon, Dough (if you PvP). Buddha (not legendary but essential).
  • Trade: Venom, Shadow, Spirit – good for trading up to better fruits.
  • Hold: Control – wait for the rework, value will spike.
  • Avoid: Don't eat a fruit just because it's legendary – check if it fits your playstyle.

Pro Tips

  • Save fragments for awakening Dough or Dragon – they become much stronger.
  • Never trade a legendary fruit without checking current values first.
  • Check Discord value lists before accepting trades.
  • Some legendary fruits (like Control) are undervalued – buy low before reworks.
