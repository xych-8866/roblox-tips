Vehicle Legends – Ultimate Guide: Best Cars, Fast Money & Racing Tips

🎮 Game Introduction

Vehicle Legends is a Roblox racing game where you collect, upgrade, and race cars. Earn money by winning races, then buy legendary vehicles. This guide covers the fastest cars, best upgrades, and money-making strategies.

💡 Basic Tips for Beginners

  • Start with the Starter Car – Upgrade its engine and tires first. It's cheap and effective.
  • Use nitrous only on straightaways – Wasting nitrous in corners slows you down.
  • Learn to drift – Drifting around corners maintains speed. Practice in solo mode.
  • Complete daily challenges – Each challenge gives $5k-$15k cash.

🔥 Advanced Strategies & Hidden Mechanics

  • Slipstreaming – Drive directly behind another car to gain +10% speed. Use it to pass.
  • Corner cutting – Some tracks allow you to cut grass without penalty. Learn the shortcuts.
  • Upgrade priority – Engine > Tires > Nitrous > Brakes. Speed matters most.
  • Race lobby trick – Join races with fewer players (2-4) to guarantee top 3 placement.

📊 Best Cars Tier List (Ranked)

  • S-Tier (Meta): Void (best acceleration), Cyber Phantom (highest top speed).
  • A-Tier (Strong): Dragonfly (balanced), Thunderbird (good handling).
  • B-Tier (Decent): Roadster, Specter – good for mid-game.
  • C-Tier (Beginner): Starter, Cruiser – upgrade quickly.

❌ Common Mistakes That Lose Races

  • Using nitrous at the start – Save it for the final straight.
  • Braking too much – Lift off the gas instead of braking in corners.
  • Ignoring daily logins – Login streak gives free cash and car parts.
  • Selling cars too early – Keep cars until fully upgraded; resale value is lower.

🎯 Final Advice for Becoming a Legend

  • Save $500k for the Void – it dominates ranked races.
  • Join a racing crew for crew bonuses (+5% cash).
  • Watch replay of your losses to see where you lost time.
  • Practice the same track 10 times to memorize corners.
