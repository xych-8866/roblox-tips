Best Enchant Loadout (Diamond Farming)

EnchantPriorityWhy
Diamonds V#1 Mandatory2x diamond drops – the single most important enchant.
Treasure Hunter V#2More breakables = more diamonds per minute.
Fortune V#3Additional diamond chance on each break.
Crits V#4Faster breakable destruction = faster respawn.
Team Work VOptionalBoosts all pets in party – good for group farming.

Best Zone for Farming

Void World (Last Area) is still the best. It has the highest base diamond drops and the fastest breakable respawn rate. Stay near the central platform where breakables cluster together.

Best Pet Lineup

  • Main Pet: Highest damage pet (preferably Huge). Damage affects breakable speed.
  • Secondary Pets: Equip pets with "Rainbow" or "Shiny" traits – they increase breakable speed.
  • If you have a Huge pet: Always equip it. Huge pets give +100% breakable speed.

Potion Stacking

For maximum output, stack these potions:

  • Diamond Potion – 3x diamonds (30 minutes)
  • Speed Potion V – Faster breakable respawn
  • Lucky Potion – More rare drops (sell for extra diamonds)

Daily Farming Routine

  1. Collect daily login rewards and hour chests (free diamonds).
  2. Equip Diamonds V + Treasure Hunter V enchants.
  3. Drink Diamond Potion + Speed Potion.
  4. Farm Void World for 30 minutes.
  5. Repeat 2-3 times per day.

Expected Output

With full V enchants + Diamond Potion + Huge pet, you can expect 80k-120k diamonds per hour. Without Huge pet, around 40k-60k per hour.
