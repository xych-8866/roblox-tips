Blox Fruits All Bosses Guide 2026 – Locations, Drops & Strategies

Bosses are the best source of fragments, Beli, and rare items in Blox Fruits. This guide covers every boss in all three seas, including spawn times, drop tables, and strategies to defeat them efficiently.

How Bosses Work

Bosses spawn at specific locations and times. They drop fragments, Beli, and exclusive accessories. Some bosses require specific conditions to spawn (e.g., night time, specific quests).

First Sea Bosses

1. Gorilla King

Location: Jungle (inside the temple).

Spawn Time: Every 15 minutes.

Rewards: 5k Beli + common fruit.

Strategy: Easy kill. Use any fruit or fighting style. Dodge his ground slam by jumping.

2. Saber Expert (Saber V2)

Location: Jungle (behind the waterfall).

Spawn Time: Requires puzzle completion.

Rewards: Saber V2 upgrade.

Strategy: Complete the puzzle by finding all the clues. The boss is medium difficulty – use a ranged fruit.

3. Darkbeard

Location: Frozen Village (night only).

Spawn Time: Night (10 PM – 5 AM in-game).

Rewards: 10k Beli + 30 fragments + Dark Coat (accessory).

Strategy: Spawns at night. Use Magma or Buddha. Dodge his AOE attacks by staying behind him.

Second Sea Bosses

4. Awakened Ice Admiral

Location: Ice Castle (inside the castle).

Spawn Time: Every 20 minutes.

Rewards: 50 fragments + Winter Cape (accessory).

Strategy: Use ranged attacks. He has a freezing AOE – dodge by moving sideways.

5. Diamond

Location: Kingdom of Rose (near the fountain).

Spawn Time: Every 25 minutes.

Rewards: Diamond accessories (ring, necklace).

Strategy: High defense. Use a fruit with AOE damage (Magma, Buddha).

6. Venom

Location: Green Zone (after killing 30 enemies).

Spawn Time: After 30 kills in Green Zone.

Rewards: Venom fruit (rare drop).

Strategy: Fast and aggressive. Keep moving – use dodges to avoid his poison attacks.

7. Dough King

Location: Sea of Treats (Third Sea – requires Dough raid).

Spawn Time: After completing Dough raid.

Rewards: 50k Beli + 250 fragments + Dough fruit.

Strategy: Requires a group. Use ranged attacks. He has a teleport ability – watch for the tell.

Third Sea Bosses

8. Rip_Indra

Location: Mansion (requires God's Chalice).

Spawn Time: After using God's Chalice.

Rewards: 100k Beli + 300 fragments + Pale Scarf (accessory).

Strategy: Very difficult. Use Buddha + Ghoul Mask for survivability. Dodge his lightning attacks by moving constantly.

9. Abyss Guardian (New)

Location: Third Sea (underwater cave).

Spawn Time: Every 30 minutes.

Rewards: 400 fragments + Abyss Crown (accessory) + random legendary fruit.

Strategy: Use Magma for sea event damage. Stay in the center of the arena – avoid the edges.

10. Leviathan

Location: Frozen Dimension (sea event).

Spawn Time: Random at night.

Rewards: 250 fragments + Leviathan Crown (accessory).

Strategy: Requires a boat. Use Magma (2x sea damage). Dodge its tentacle attacks by moving sideways.

Boss Farming Route (Optimal)

  1. First Sea: Darkbeard (night) → Gorilla King → Saber Expert.
  2. Second Sea: Awakened Ice Admiral → Diamond → Venom.
  3. Third Sea: Abyss Guardian → Rip_Indra → Dough King → Leviathan.

Pro Tips

  • Server hop – after defeating a boss, switch servers to respawn it instantly.
  • Use Ghoul Mask – life steal keeps you alive during long boss fights.
  • Bring potions – energy and speed potions help in tough fights.
  • Group up – some bosses (Dough King, Rip_Indra, Abyss Guardian) are much easier with a crew.
  • Focus on fragments – prioritize Darkbeard, Awakened Ice Admiral, Rip_Indra, and Abyss Guardian for fragments.
