Fastest Leveling Setup

Before you start grinding, make sure you have these:

  • Fruit: Buddha (best) or Light/Magma (second best)

XP Optimization Tips

  • Use Buddha fruit – it has the largest AOE and lets you kill groups of NPCs at once.
  • Use Ghoul Mask – life steal keeps you alive and reduces downtime.
  • Server hop – if an area is too crowded, switch servers to find empty spots.
  • Use XP potions – 2x XP for 30 minutes. Use during peak grinding sessions.
  • Complete daily quests – they give bonus XP and Beli.

Pro Tips

  • Don't waste time on bosses while grinding – they slow down XP/hour.
  • Focus on NPCs that spawn in groups – you kill more per minute.
  • If you have 2x Mastery game pass, use it – it doubles all XP gained.
  • If you're struggling to survive, use Ghoul Mask + Dark Coat for survivability.
