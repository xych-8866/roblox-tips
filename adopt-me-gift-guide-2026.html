Adopt Me Gift Guide 2026 – Best Gifts for Friends & Trading

Not sure what to buy with your bucks or what to give to friends? This guide covers the best gifts in Adopt Me, from affordable to high-end.

Budget Gifts (Under 500 Bucks)

  • Cracked Egg – 350 bucks. Fun to hatch with friends.
  • Pizza – 100 bucks. Quick and easy gift.
  • Lemonade – 50 bucks. Cheap but appreciated.
  • Gifts (the item) – Available at the Gift Shop, prices vary.

Mid-Tier Gifts (500-2,000 Bucks)

  • Pet Egg – 600 bucks. Better chance of rare pets.
  • Royal Egg – 1,450 bucks. Highest legendary chance in store.
  • Fashion Accessories – 500-1,500 bucks. Great for players who love customization.
  • Vehicles (Basic) – 1,000-2,000 bucks. Fun for exploring together.

High-Tier Gifts (2,000+ Bucks)

  • House Upgrades – 5,000-20,000 bucks. Perfect for players who enjoy decorating.
  • Legendary Pets (from trading) – Can be gifted as a surprise.
  • Fly/Ride Potions – 1,000-2,000 bucks (or trade value). Great for gameplay.
  • Event Limited Items – Varies in price. Hold value well.

Best Gifts by Player Type

  • New Players: Pet Egg, basic vehicle, fashion accessories.
  • Collectors: Limited event items, rare strollers.
  • Traders: Fly/Ride potions, rare toys.
  • Decorators: House upgrades, furniture items.

Gifting Tips

  • Check the player's inventory before gifting – avoid giving duplicates.
  • Event items are always a safe choice – they hold value and are appreciated.
  • Use the trade window to gift – never drop items on the floor.
  • Gift with a note – it makes it more personal.

What Not to Gift

  • Common pets – most players have them already.
  • Food items – temporary and low value.
  • Items you know they dislike – ask first.
