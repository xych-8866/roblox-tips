Blox Fruits Race V3 Guide 2026 – How to Unlock All Races

Race V3 gives powerful passive abilities. This guide covers requirements and trials for each race.

Prerequisites

  • Level 1000+ (Second Sea)
  • Race V2 completed (basic awakening)
  • Access to the King's Castle in Second Sea

Human V3 Trial

Defeat 10 players in PvP without dying. Reward: Full Body Haki (damage boost).

Rabbit V3 Trial

Collect 100 flowers around the map within 30 minutes. Reward: Infinite Stamina (sprinting no longer drains).

Shark V3 Trial

Survive the waterfall in Frozen Village for 2 minutes. Reward: Water Body (damage reduction in water).

Angel V3 Trial

Heal 5000 HP to allies using Angel abilities. Reward: Wings of Light (flight + team healing).

Ghoul V3 Trial

Defeat 20 NPCs at night in any sea. Reward: Bloodlust (life steal doubled).

Tips

  • Use private server for Human PvP trial.
  • Flowers for Rabbit spawn randomly – use a fast fruit like Light.
  • Ghoul trial is easiest in First Sea at Jungle (bandits).
