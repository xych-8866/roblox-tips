Adopt Me All Eggs & How to Get Them 2026 Guide

Eggs are the only way to get new pets. This guide lists every egg in the game, where to buy or find it, and the best pets inside.

Starter Eggs (Free)

  • Cracked Egg – 350 bucks. Commons/uncommons, low legendary chance.
  • Pet Egg – 600 bucks. Slightly better legendary rate.
  • Royal Egg – 1,450 bucks. Highest legendary chance among store eggs.

Limited Eggs (Rotating)

  • Farm Egg – retired. Contains Cow, Pig, Chicken (high trade value).
  • Jungle Egg – retired. Elephant, Parrot, Crocodile.
  • Christmas Egg – available in December. Arctic Reindeer, Snow Owl.
  • Summer 2026 Event Egg – limited-time event (June-July). Contains Golden Mermaid.

Special Eggs

  • Golden Egg – from star rewards (500 stars). Guaranteed legendary (Griffin, Dragon, Unicorn).
  • Diamond Egg – 1,000 stars. Higher chance of top-tier legendary.
  • Easter Egg 2026 – from bunny hunt event. Contains event pets.

Fastest Way to Farm Bucks for Eggs

Take care of a baby or family member – tasks give 2x faster cash. Also collect daily login and play the hot dog stand minigame (200 bucks per round).
