🌪️ Natural Disaster Survival – How to Survive Every Disaster (2026)

🌊 Tsunami

  • Climb the tallest building – Lighthouse or hotel roof.
  • Avoid low ground – Beach and parking lot are death traps.
  • Stay away from glass windows – They break and cause damage.

🔥 Wildfire

  • Head to water – Swimming pool, river, or ocean.
  • Move constantly – Fire spreads quickly, don't stay in one spot.
  • Avoid wooden structures – They burn fastest.

🌪️ Tornado

  • Hide in basement or underground parking – Safest spots.
  • Stay away from windows – Debris will shatter them.
  • If outside, lie flat in a ditch – Low ground reduces wind impact.

⛈️ Flash Flood

  • Get to high ground immediately – Hills, tall buildings, or trees.
  • Avoid jumping into water – Current is stronger than it looks.
  • Swim to floating objects – Cars, logs, or debris can save you.

🗻 Earthquake

  • Stay in open area – Avoid buildings and power lines.
  • Drop, cover, hold on – If inside, hide under a table.
  • After shaking stops, watch for aftershocks – Keep moving.

💎 Tips for Survival

  • Wear the "Life Jacket" accessory – gives extra time in water.
  • Use "Jetpack" gamepass for faster movement.
  • Join servers with fewer players – less competition for high ground.
  • Learn map layouts – knowing escape routes saves seconds.
