The Mimic – Complete Survival Guide: Monsters, Secrets & Endings

🎮 Game Introduction

The Mimic is a Roblox horror game based on Japanese folklore. You must survive four chapters, each with unique monsters and puzzles. This guide covers monster behaviors, hidden items, and how to unlock the true ending.

💡 Basic Tips for Beginners

  • Listen for sound cues – Each monster has a unique noise before appearing. Learn them.
  • Hide in lockers or closets – But don't stay too long; the monster will check after 15 seconds.
  • Use your flashlight sparingly – Light attracts some monsters (e.g., Kintoru).
  • Complete the tutorial – It explains basic mechanics and gives a free lantern.

🔥 Advanced Strategies & Hidden Mechanics

  • Bait and loop – Some monsters (like Jealous) are slow. Run around tables to waste their time.
  • Secret shrine items – In Chapter 2, collect 5 hidden talismans to unlock a shortcut.
  • True ending condition – Find all 4 scrolls (one per chapter) and place them on the altar after the final boss.
  • Monster weakness – The “Mimic” monster (Chapter 4) stops moving if you shine a light on it for 3 seconds.

📊 Monster Difficulty Tier List

  • S-Tier (Hardest): Kintoru (fast, unpredictable), Follower (spawns in groups).
  • A-Tier (Challenging): Jealous (slow but one-shot), Kusonoki (poison).
  • B-Tier (Easy): Hiachi (predictable patrol), Yurei (slow).

❌ Common Mistakes That Get You Killed

  • Running in open areas – Sprinting makes noise. Crouch walk when near monsters.
  • Ignoring environmental clues – Blood trails or broken objects indicate a monster nearby.
  • Hiding in the same spot twice – Monsters remember your last hiding place.
  • Not using the map – Memorize safe rooms (marked by red lanterns).

🎯 Final Advice for Beating Chapter 4

  • Save 3 flashbangs for the final chase sequence – use them when the monster is 2 steps behind.
  • Follow the blood stains on the floor – they lead to the correct exit.
  • Play with friends – one person distracts the monster while others solve puzzles.
  • Watch speedruns to learn optimal routes for each chapter.
