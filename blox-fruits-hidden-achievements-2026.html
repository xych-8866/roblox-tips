Blox Fruits Hidden Achievements 2026 – How to Unlock

There are secret achievements that give rare titles and rewards. This guide reveals all hidden achievements and how to get them.

1. "The Unkillable"

Requirement: Survive 10 player attacks without dying or logging off.
Reward: Title "Unkillable" + 10 Fragments.
Tip: Use Buddha + Ghoul mask in a private server.

2. "Treasure Hunter"

Requirement: Find 50 chests in the sea within one hour.
Reward: Title "Treasure Hunter" + 20 Fragments.
Best route: Third sea – around Floating Turtle.

3. "Arena Master"

Requirement: Win 10 consecutive matches in the PvP Arena.
Reward: Title "Arena Master" + 50 Fragments.
Strategy: Use Kitsune or Dragon fruit.

4. "Ghost"

Requirement: Escape from a player without taking damage 5 times.
Reward: Title "Ghost" + 5 Fragments.
Tip: Use Portal fruit to teleport away.
