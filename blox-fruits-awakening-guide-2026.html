Blox Fruits Awakening Guide 2026 – How to Awaken Any Fruit

Awakening makes your fruit 2x stronger. This guide covers fragment costs, raid requirements, and the best order to awaken.

Requirements

  • Level 1100+ (Second Sea)
  • Have the fruit you want to awaken (must be awakenable)
  • Fragments: 1000-3000 per fruit
  • Complete raids (join via Laboratory in Second Sea)

Best Fruits to Awaken First

  • Buddha – #1 priority (massive grind boost)
  • Magma – Best for sea events and bosses
  • Dough – PvP beast after awakening
  • Quake – Cheap and strong for beginners

Awakening Order (Example: Buddha)

  1. First move: 1000 fragments
  2. Second move: 1000 fragments
  3. Third move: 1500 fragments
  4. Fourth move: 2000 fragments
  5. Ultimate: 2500 fragments

Total: ~8000 fragments per fruit. Farm fragments via raids (200-300 per raid).

Tips

  • Use Buddha fruit to solo raids and grind fragments faster.
  • Join a crew – crew members can help you complete raids.
  • Awaken moves that increase damage first, then utility moves.
