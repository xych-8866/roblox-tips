Ninja Legends – Ultimate Guide: Best Ninjas, Fast Chakra & Prestige Strategies

🎮 Game Introduction

Ninja Legends is a Roblox training game where you punch training dummies, earn chakra, and unlock powerful ninjas. Prestige to reset and gain permanent boosts. This guide covers the best ninjas, fastest chakra farming, and prestige timing.

💡 Basic Tips for Beginners

  • Start with Fire Ninja – Balanced damage and speed. Good for early levels.
  • Use the Auto Clicker upgrade – Unlock at level 10 for AFK farming.
  • Complete daily training quests – Each gives 500 chakra and a boost potion.
  • Join a clan – Clan bonuses include +10% chakra gain.

🔥 Advanced Strategies & Hidden Mechanics

  • Chakra multiplier stacking – Activate “Chakra Boost” potion, then train in the “Sacred Dojo” for 3x chakra.
  • Prestige exploit – Prestige when you reach exactly level 100; you keep all ninjas but gain double prestige points.
  • Hidden ninja scroll – Behind the waterfall in the training area; click to unlock “Shadow Ninja” (rare).
  • Training dummy trick – Hit the same dummy repeatedly without stopping; combo meter increases chakra per hit up to 5x.

📊 Best Ninjas Tier List (PvP & Farming)

  • S-Tier (Meta): Shadow Ninja (invisible), Lightning Ninja (fast attacks), Dragon Ninja (high AoE).
  • A-Tier (Strong): Ice Ninja (slow), Fire Ninja (burn), Earth Ninja (tanky).
  • B-Tier (Decent): Water Ninja (heal), Wind Ninja (speed).

❌ Common Mistakes That Waste Chakra

  • Prestige too early – Wait until level 100; earlier prestiges give fewer permanent bonuses.
  • Ignoring clan wars – Clan wars give 5k chakra per win. Participate daily.
  • Buying expensive ninjas first – Start with cheap ninjas (Fire, Water) to build a team.
  • Not using training scrolls – Scrolls double chakra for 10 minutes. Use them during boost events.

🎯 Final Advice for Prestige 10+

  • Save 100k chakra for “Eternal Ninja” – grants permanent 2x chakra.
  • Focus on one S-tier ninja and max its level to 100 before prestiging.
  • Join the official Discord for trading rare ninja skins.
  • Use “Clan Boost” during double chakra weekends (announced in-game).
