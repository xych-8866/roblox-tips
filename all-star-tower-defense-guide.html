All Star Tower Defense – Ultimate Guide: Best Units, Evolution & Infinite Mode Strategies

🎮 Game Introduction

All Star Tower Defense (ASTD) is a Roblox tower defense game with a massive roster of anime-inspired units. Summon, evolve, and place towers to defend your base. This guide covers the top units, fastest star farming, and infinite mode meta.

💡 Basic Tips for Beginners

  • Start with the free summon ticket – Use code "ASTD2026" for a free legendary summon.
  • Focus on one or two damage dealers – Spreading resources weakens your defense.
  • Place units on high ground – Elevated positions (e.g., hills in desert map) give +10% range.
  • Complete story mode first – Rewards 5k stars and a guaranteed 5-star unit.

🔥 Advanced Strategies & Hidden Mechanics

  • Elemental advantage – Fire > Grass > Water > Fire. Use it to deal double damage on some maps.
  • Unit stacking – Placing two same units next to each other triggers a "synergy aura" (+15% attack speed).
  • Secret evolution – Certain units (e.g., "Gogeta") evolve only when you have both "Goku" and "Vegeta" in your inventory.
  • Infinity mode farm – Stop at wave 40, then reset. Wave 40 gives the best star/time ratio (500 stars per run).

📊 Best Units Tier List (Infinite Mode)

  • S-Tier (Essential): Gogeta (SSGSS), Shadow Monarch, Golden Frieza.
  • A-Tier (Strong): Hollow Ichigo, Madara (Six Paths), Esdeath.
  • B-Tier (Decent): Tanjiro (Demon Slayer), Deku (Full Cowling).

❌ Common Mistakes That Waste Stars

  • Summoning on the wrong banner – Limited-time banners have lower 6-star rates. Save for "Super Rate Up" events.
  • Evolving too early – Wait until your unit is level 60; evolution resets level to 1.
  • Ignoring supports – Buffers like "Bulma" (damage boost) are as important as damage dealers.
  • Selling duplicate 5-stars – Keep them for "limit breaking" (fuse 5 copies for +10% stats).

🎯 Final Advice for Reaching Wave 200+

  • Unlock "Double Damage" and "Auto Upgrade" gamepasses.
  • Have at least 3 S-tier units at max limit break (level 120).
  • Use "Time Stop" and "Double Cash" items on boss waves (50, 100, 150).
  • Join an active clan for "Clan Buffs" (+20% damage).
