Blox Fruits All Bosses Locations & Drops Guide 2026

Bosses are the best way to get fragments, rare fruits, and exclusive items. This guide covers every boss in First, Second, and Third Sea – updated for 2026.

First Sea Bosses

  • Gorilla King (Jungle) – spawns every 15 min. Drops: 5k cash, random common fruit, Gorilla Crown (accessory).
  • Saber Expert (Jungle) – requires torch puzzle. Drops: Saber V2 upgrade.
  • Darkbeard (Frozen Village) – spawns at night. Drops: Dark Coat, fragments.

Second Sea Bosses

  • Awakened Ice Admiral (Ice Castle) – drops 50 fragments, winter cape.
  • Diamond (Kingdom of Rose) – high defense. Drops diamond accessories.
  • Venom (Green Zone) – spawns after 30 kills. Drops Venom fruit (rare).

Third Sea Bosses (Endgame)

  • Rip_Indra (Mansion) – needs God's Chalice. Drops Dark Fragment, Pale Scarf, race reroll.
  • Dough King (Cake Land) – spawns after completing Dough raid. Drops Dough fruit, gear pieces.
  • Leviathan (Frozen Dimension) – sea event, requires boat. Drops Leviathan Crown, 250 frags.

Boss Farming Tips

Use Buddha fruit + Ghoul mask for fastest boss kills. Server hop after each kill to respawn boss immediately. Save fragments for race awakenings.
