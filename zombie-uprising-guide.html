Zombie Uprising – Complete Survival Guide: Best Weapons, Perks & Bosses

🎮 Game Introduction

Zombie Uprising is a Roblox co-op survival game. Fight waves of zombies, earn cash, buy weapons and perks. Survive 30 waves to escape. This guide covers the best loadouts, perk priorities, and boss strategies.

💡 Basic Tips for Beginners

  • Stick with your team – Solo is much harder. Group up to share ammo.
  • Buy the M16 first – Costs $2k, good for waves 1-10.
  • Use barricades – Place them at choke points to slow zombies.
  • Save 10% of cash for perks – Don't spend everything on weapons.

🔥 Advanced Strategies & Hidden Mechanics

  • Train zombies – Run in circles to group zombies, then use grenades for multi-kills.
  • Perk stacking – Buy "Double Tap" (faster fire rate) + "Speed Cola" (reload speed) for max DPS.
  • Secret weapon room – On wave 15, break the wall behind the armory (3 grenades) to unlock Ray Gun.
  • Boss weakness – Wave 20 boss (Abomination) takes 3x damage from fire weapons (Molotov, Flamethrower).

📊 Best Weapons Tier List (Endless Mode)

  • S-Tier: Ray Gun (infinite ammo), Flamethrower (crowd control), Minigun (high DPS).
  • A-Tier: RPG (clears groups), M249 (large magazine), MP5 (accurate).
  • B-Tier: Shotgun (good early game), M16 (cheap but weak later).

❌ Common Mistakes That End Runs

  • Buying the Ray Gun too early – It's expensive ($15k) and weak until upgraded. Wait for wave 20.
  • Ignoring armor upgrades – Level 2 armor reduces damage by 40%. Critical for waves 15+.
  • Not reviving teammates – A player down means less firepower. Always revive within 10 seconds.
  • Camping in corners – Zombies will surround you. Keep moving.

🎯 Final Advice for Completing Wave 30

  • Unlock "Juggernog" perk ($5k) – doubles health. Mandatory for wave 20+.
  • Save your ultimate ability (nuke) for wave 29's final horde.
  • Have one player dedicated to repairing barricades.
  • Learn to kite the Wave 30 boss (Megaton) – shoot its legs to slow it down.
