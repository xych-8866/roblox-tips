Pressure – Complete Survival Guide: Monsters, Items & Endless Mode

🎮 Game Introduction

Pressure is a Roblox horror survival game where you explore an underwater facility filled with monsters, traps, and secrets. Your goal is to survive waves of enemies, collect data, and escape. This guide covers every monster, best items, and how to reach the end.

💡 Basic Survival Tips

  • Listen for audio cues – Each monster has a unique sound before attacking. Learn them.
  • Use lockers wisely – Hiding in lockers drains your oxygen. Stay inside max 10 seconds.
  • Flashlight is not always safe – Some monsters (like Angler) are attracted to light. Turn it off.
  • Always carry a medkit – You can find them in green cabinets. Use after taking damage.

🔥 Advanced Strategies & Hidden Mechanics

  • Angler dodge timing – When you hear a loud roar, hide in a locker for 5 seconds exactly. Count in your head.
  • Chain lightning exploit – Stay behind walls when you see flickering lights. A monster is about to pass.
  • The Ridge path – The final area has no lockers. Memorize the safe spots (broken pipes, corners).
  • Multiplayer revive trick – If a friend dies, use a Medkit near their body within 15 seconds. They revive.

📊 Monster Tier List (Danger Level)

  • S-Tier (Instant kill if careless): Angler, Chainsmoker, Pinkie – spawn frequently, hard to avoid.
  • A-Tier (High threat): Froger, Blitz, Miner – predictable but deadly in narrow halls.
  • B-Tier (Annoying but avoidable): Squiddle, Vacula, Wall Dweller – learn their patterns.
  • C-Tier (Low risk): Good People, Deep Sea Bunnies – mostly distractions.

❌ Common Mistakes That Kill You

  • Holding flashlight in open areas – Attracts Angler from far away.
  • Running out of lockers too early – Stay hidden until the monster sound fades completely.
  • Ignoring battery management – Flashlight dies fast. Save batteries for dark rooms only.
  • Not checking corners for Wall Dwellers – They blend in. Use camera lean (Q/E) to peek.

🎯 Final Advice for Endless Mode

  • Survive 50+ doors by rotating lockers and using the "safe room" strategy (wait in rooms with multiple exits).
  • Best items to hoard: Flashlights, Batteries, Medkits, and the Gummylight (infinite battery but dim).
  • Learn door sounds – a creaking door means a monster is on the other side. Don't open immediately.
  • Play with friends – revived teammates save runs.
