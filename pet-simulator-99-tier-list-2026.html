D-Tier (Avoid)

These pets are not worth keeping. Use them for fusion or sell them for coins.

  • Common Cats, Dogs, and Birds – Low stats, low value.
  • Basic Event Pets (2024 and older) – Outclassed by newer pets.
  • Non-Huge Mythical Pets – Unless you're a collector, they're not worth the grind.

How to Get Top-Tier Pets

  • Hatch Event Eggs – Save diamonds for limited-time events (1/1000 chance for huge).
  • Trade Up – Start with low-tier legendaries and gradually trade up to better pets.
  • Farm Diamonds – Use the best farming setup (Diamonds V + Treasure Hunter V + Void World) to save up for huge pets.
  • Complete Achievements – Some achievements reward exclusive pets.

Pro Tips

  • Focus on S-Tier pets first – they give the biggest return on investment.
  • Don't waste diamonds on D-Tier pets – you'll regret it.
  • Check value lists before trading – avoid bad deals.
  • Save event eggs – they often rise in value after the event ends.
