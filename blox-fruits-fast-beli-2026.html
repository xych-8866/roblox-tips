Blox Fruits Fast Beli 2026 – Best Ways to Get Rich

Beli is the main currency in Blox Fruits. This guide ranks the fastest methods to earn Beli, from grinding NPCs to sea events and trading.

1. Sea Events (Best Beli/Hour)

  • Ship Raid – 50k-150k Beli per raid, spawns every 30 minutes.
  • Sea Beast – 30k-80k Beli, random spawn.
  • Rogue Sea Beast – 200k-500k Beli, spawns only at night. Best Beli source.

2. Grinding NPCs (Consistent Income)

  • First Sea: Jungle bandits (5k-10k per kill).
  • Second Sea: Elite pirates in Kingdom of Rose (20k-30k per kill).
  • Third Sea: Great Tree acolytes (50k-80k per kill).

3. Boss Farming (Fragments + Beli)

  • Darkbeard – 10k Beli + 30 fragments.
  • Dough King – 50k Beli + 250 fragments.
  • Rip_Indra – 100k Beli + 300 fragments.

4. Trading Fruits (High Risk, High Reward)

Buy low (trade legendaries for cheap fruits) and sell high. Example: Trade a Dough fruit for 10M Beli, buy a Venom for 7M, repeat. Requires knowledge of market values.

5. Daily Quests & Activities

  • Daily quests: 50k-100k Beli per day.
  • Weekly quests: 500k-1M Beli per week.
  • Collect chests in Third Sea – 1k-5k Beli each.

Pro Tips

  • Use Magma fruit for sea events – it deals 2x damage.
  • Server hop after killing a boss to respawn it instantly.
  • Join a crew – active crew members increase spawn rates for sea events.
  • Save Beli for stat resets and race rerolls – don't waste on cosmetics early.
