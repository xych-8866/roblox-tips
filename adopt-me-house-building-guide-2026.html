Best House Layouts

  • Open Concept: Remove interior walls (if possible) to create a large living space. Place furniture to define zones (living, dining, kitchen).
  • Split Level: Use stairs to create separate areas for sleeping and socializing. Good for medium houses.
  • Minimalist: Clean lines, light colors, and minimal clutter. Great for modern houses.
  • Cozy Family: Warm colors, lots of seating, and pet-friendly furniture. Best for larger houses.

Decoration Tips

  • Use rugs to define spaces in open-concept homes.
  • Layer furniture – don't place everything against the wall.
  • Add plants for a fresh, natural feel.
  • Use lighting – chandeliers and lamps add warmth.
  • Create a pet corner with beds, toys, and food bowls.
  • Hang art on empty walls – it transforms a room.

Budget Building Tips

  • Start with a cheap house and upgrade later – you can sell it for 80% of the original price.
  • Collect free items from events and daily rewards.
  • Focus on one room at a time – don't spread your budget too thin.
  • Look for budget furniture in the store (there are always cheap options).

How to Save for a House

  • Pizza Delivery – $50 per delivery, 4,000+/hour.
  • Hot Dog Stand – AFK income, 500-1,000/hour.
  • Hidden cash stashes – $500+ daily.
  • Pet sitting – 900/hour (if you find clients).

Pro Tips

  • Check the store daily – limited-time furniture appears regularly.
  • Visit other players' houses for inspiration.
  • Use the "grid" option for perfect furniture alignment.
  • Take before/after photos – it helps you see improvements.
