How Accessories Work

Accessories give permanent stat boosts when equipped. You can only wear one accessory at a time. Choose based on your playstyle and the content you're doing.

Best Accessories for Each Playstyle

  • Grinding: Ghoul Mask (life steal) + Dark Coat (damage).
  • PvP: Pale Scarf (damage) + Pilot Helmet (speed).
  • Boss Fights: Ghoul Mask (life steal) + Leviathan Crown (defense).
  • Farming (Beli/Fragments): Pilot Helmet (speed) + Dark Coat (damage).

Where to Farm Accessories

  • Darkbeard: Spawns at night in First Sea – farm for Dark Coat.
  • Rip_Indra: Requires God's Chalice – drops Pale Scarf.
  • Leviathan: Sea event in Third Sea – drops Leviathan Crown.
  • Ghoul Mask: Complete the Ghoul questline in Second Sea.

Pro Tips

  • Focus on Ghoul Mask first – it's the most versatile accessory.
  • Don't waste time on B-Tier accessories unless you're early game.
  • Save fragments for awakening, not for accessory farming.
  • Trade duplicate accessories for fragments or Beli.
