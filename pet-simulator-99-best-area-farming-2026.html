Pet Simulator 99 Best Area Farming 2026 – Coins & Diamonds

Maximize your farming efficiency by choosing the right zone for your level and pets.

Coins Farming (Early Game)

  • Grassy Plains – Best for beginners (up to 5k coins/hour).
  • Desert Zone – Good for level 10-20 (10k coins/hour).
  • Ice Zone – Level 20-30 (15k coins/hour).

Coins Farming (Late Game)

  • Tech World – 50k coins/hour, requires level 50+ pets.
  • Void World – 100k coins/hour, best for level 80+ pets with good enchants.

Diamond Farming

  • Fantasy World – 100-200 diamonds/hour (low).
  • Void World (center) – 500-800 diamonds/hour with Diamonds V enchant.
  • Event Zones – Limited time, often 2x diamonds.

Best Enchants for Farming

  • Diamonds V – Mandatory for diamond farming.
  • Coin V – For coin farming.
  • Treasure Hunter V – More breakables = more loot.
