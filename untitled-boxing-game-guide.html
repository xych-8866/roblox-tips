Untitled Boxing Game – Complete Guide: Best Styles, Combos & Ranked Tips

🎮 Game Introduction

Untitled Boxing Game is a Roblox boxing simulation with deep mechanics. Choose a style (Smash, Hitman, Counter, etc.), train stats, and fight in ranked matches. This guide covers the best styles, true combos, and stamina management.

💡 Basic Tips for Beginners

  • Manage stamina – Every punch costs stamina. Stop attacking when bar turns red.
  • Use jabs (M1) to measure range – Jabs are fast and safe. Use them to find your distance.
  • Block high first – Most players aim for the head. Hold F to block head punches.
  • Dash after missing – If you whiff a heavy punch, dash backward immediately (double tap S).

🔥 Advanced Strategies & Hidden Mechanics

  • Feinting – Start a heavy punch then cancel with block. Bait opponent's parry.
  • Corner trap – Push enemy to the corner then use body hooks (M2) to drain their stamina faster.
  • Counter timing – When opponent throws a jab, step back and immediately throw your own cross. Guaranteed counter damage.
  • Ultimate usage – Use only when opponent is below 30% stamina or dazed.

📊 Style Tier List (Ranked)

  • S-Tier (Meta): Hitman (long reach, great combos), Counter (parry grants free punch).
  • A-Tier (Strong): Smash (high damage, slow start), Ghost (fast but low damage).
  • B-Tier (Niche): Slugger (good hooks, bad against outboxers), Outboxer (safe but low kill pressure).
  • C-Tier (Avoid): Infighter – too short range, gets outranged by everything.

❌ Common Mistakes That Lose Fights

  • Spamming heavies – Easy to sidestep and counter.
  • Forgetting to block body – Some players target body to drain stamina.
  • Using ultimate too early – It's predictable and can be dodged.
  • Not training stats – Strength, Speed, and Vitality matter. Upgrade them between fights.

🎯 Final Advice for Climbing Ranks

  • Stick to one style until you reach Rank A. Master its combos.
  • Watch your replays to see why you got hit.
  • Learn to read opponent patterns – most players have 2-3 favorite combos.
  • Stay calm when low on health – panicking leads to mistakes.
