Blox Fruits Race V3 Awakening Guide 2026 – Unlock All Races

Race V3 gives powerful passive abilities. This guide covers each race's trial requirements and rewards. Updated for 2026.

Prerequisites

Before you can start Race V3, you must meet these requirements:

  • Level 1000+ (Second Sea)
  • Race V2 must be fully unlocked (completed the V2 trial for your current race).
  • Access to the King's Castle in Second Sea.

Human V3 Trial

Requirement: Defeat 10 players in PvP without dying.

Reward: Full Body Haki – damage boost + stun immunity.

Tip: Use a private server or ask friends to help you farm kills.

Rabbit V3 Trial

Requirement: Collect 100 flowers scattered across the map within 30 minutes.

Reward: Infinite Stamina – sprinting no longer drains stamina.

Tip: Use a fast fruit like Light or Portal to cover ground quickly.

Shark V3 Trial

Requirement: Survive the waterfall in Frozen Village for 2 minutes without dying.

Reward: Water Body – 80% damage reduction while swimming.

Tip: Use a fruit with healing (like Buddha) or bring potions.

Angel V3 Trial

Requirement: Heal 5,000 HP to allies using Angel abilities.

Reward: Wings of Light – flight + team healing.

Tip: Find a group of players and spam healing abilities in a safe zone.

Ghoul V3 Trial

Requirement: Defeat 20 NPCs at night in any sea.

Reward: Bloodlust – life steal doubled.

Tip: Go to Jungle in First Sea at night – bandits respawn quickly.

Pro Tips

  • You can switch races using a Race Reroll (costs 100 fragments).
  • Complete all races to unlock Race V4 (see our separate guide).
  • Human and Ghoul are the best for PvP; Rabbit and Angel are great for farming.
  • Don't forget to equip your Race V3 ability in the menu after unlocking it.
