Current top pets (May 2026): Huge Crystal Dog, Titanic Fire Fox, Rainbow Unicorn. These pets have the highest damage and coin multiplier. Focus on hatching exclusive eggs during events.

Area 12 (Volcano) – destroy lava rocks; each gives 500-1500 coins.
Secret Rainbow Zone – reachable via portal in Area 9 after collecting 50 rainbow shards.

🗺️ Secret Teleport Locations

Behind the big tree in Area 3, click the hidden acorn → teleports to **Golden Apple Field** (10k coins per apple).
In Area 6, jump into the fountain three times → opens **Treasure Vault** (random huge pet).

📈 Trading Value Guide

High demand pets: Huge Crystal Dog (~3,000 gems), Titanic Fire Fox (~8,000 gems). Avoid trading for ordinary rainbow pets – their value drops fast after updates.
