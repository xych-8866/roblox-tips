Blox Fruits Race Guide 2026 – All Races & Best Abilities

Your race determines your passive abilities in Blox Fruits. This guide covers all 6 races, their abilities, how to unlock V2, V3, and V4, and which race is best for your playstyle.

How Races Work

You are assigned a random race when you start the game. You can change your race using a Race Reroll (costs 100 fragments). Each race has unique passive abilities that get stronger as you unlock higher versions (V1, V2, V3, V4).

All Races & Abilities

1. Human

Best For: PvP

V1: None

V2: +10% damage below 50% HP

V3 (Full Body Haki): Damage boost + stun immunity (unlocked by defeating 10 players in PvP without dying)

V4: Superhuman – +25% damage + speed boost

2. Rabbit

Best For: Speed, mobility

V1: +10% jump height

V2: +20% movement speed

V3 (Infinite Stamina): Sprinting no longer drains stamina (unlocked by collecting 100 flowers in 30 minutes)

V4: Flash – teleport dash ability

3. Shark

Best For: Sea events, water combat

V1: +15% swim speed

V2: +25% damage in water

V3 (Water Body): 80% damage reduction while swimming (unlocked by surviving the Frozen Village waterfall for 2 minutes)

V4: King of the Sea – water abilities and damage boost

4. Angel

Best For: Support, team healing

V1: +5% health regeneration

V2: +15% healing to allies

V3 (Wings of Light): Flight + team healing (unlocked by healing 5,000 HP to allies)

V4: Archangel – resurrection ability

5. Ghoul

Best For: PvP, life steal

V1: +5% life steal

V2: +15% life steal

V3 (Bloodlust): Life steal doubled (unlocked by defeating 20 NPCs at night)

V4: Vampire – 50% life steal

6. Mink (Cyborg)

Best For: PvP, speed (unlocked in Third Sea)

V1: +10% movement speed

V2: +20% movement speed + electric AOE

V3: Electric dash – stun enemies (unlocked by completing Mink quest)

V4: Cyborg mode – speed + damage

How to Unlock V2

Reach level 500 and talk to the Awakening NPC in Second Sea. Complete the trial for your race.

How to Unlock V3

Complete the specific trial for your race (see above). All V3 trials require level 1000+.

How to Unlock V4

Complete all V3 trials for all races (you need to switch races and unlock V3 on each one). Then talk to the NPC in Third Sea. V4 is the ultimate ability and is very powerful.

Pro Tips

  • Human and Ghoul are the best for PvP – focus on these if you fight often.
  • Rabbit is best for grinding – infinite stamina saves a lot of time.
  • Shark is mandatory for sea events – if you farm sea events, use Shark.
  • Angel is niche – only use if you play in a group.
  • Use Race Rerolls – if you get a race you don't want, reroll until you get Human, Ghoul, or Rabbit.
